Katie,
I have found 5# to 6# sheet in all thicknesses at Michaels craft stores and
at art supply stores such as Dick Blick and Binders. I take my pocket size
gram scale with me. You should see the inquisitive looks from the clerks.
Whenever I visit a different city, I visit the craft stores as well as hobby
shops. From time to time, I find some real treasures in the balsa wood
displays. My best so far: a 4# sheet of 1/4"x3"x36". Amazing!
