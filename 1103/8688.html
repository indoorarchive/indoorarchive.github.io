On 5.3.2011 23:18, Don DeLoach wrote:

> F1M is intriguing except for how absurdly long they are. They seem
> difficult to make turn in a small site plus the packing/travel

??? My fuselages have similar length of motor stick and tailboom, and
split in the half, pack neatly under the wing. I do a lot of practice in
a small gym, and have found no difficulties to make the model turn in
the gym by adding a little tail tilt to the "large hall" trim setting. I
can barely make my F1D turn in that gym (but needs constant steering),
so a F1M turns tighter than a F1D. At least for me.
