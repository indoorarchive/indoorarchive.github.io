I'll try to get a picture uploaded, but I am one who uses the model
box idea... I have a piece of 1/4" plywood, about 2' long, with
velcro strips down the middle, and a bracket to hold my winder on the
end... on the other end, I have a hole drilled, which a screw goes
through and into the top of one of my boxes (I got some aluminum tool
cases at Home Depot for about $15 each... lightweight and very
sturdy...) in the lid of the case, I put a block of wood Goop'ed to
the top, with a screw insert to screw the holder onto... when I use
my folding table, I have the same insert in the top of that... on the
bottom of my TMeter, I have velcro, so I can move it around to the
right length...
