> Don's answer is correct. It's a modification to the
> same glider I flew at Kent last year. The wing has
> been shortened for shipping purposes and winglets
> added. It has a new fuse similar to the old one
> except 2" longer nose and a removable wing mounted
> on foam. Also the carbon tow wing reinforcing was

> a lot of velocity now.

> was a Slow Poker scaled up to 100 sq. in. and built

> glider and has been flown hundreds of times over
> about a 3 year period. It just keeps getting better.
