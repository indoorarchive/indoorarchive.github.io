Hi Bill, you neglected to say what type of vehicle you drive, whether it is
indoors etc. I use a unit I built to fit my Ford Ranger with a Trailer hitch
which has a removable ball ( "Hidden Hitch")
mine fits in the Ball mount. It is made from 2" sq. X 1/4" tubing, over
kill I will admit but does nor require anchors. My next build will be
from lighter Material and use the bed anchors to anchor the stooge. I have
seen some that were mounted to cars with hitches and anchored to the Hinge
Mounts.
