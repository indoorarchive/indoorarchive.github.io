I've seen a few folks who have stooges and torque meter attached to
their model boxes. I'll take a few photos of my setup and pass them
along, but I just use a C-clamp to attach my setup to the available
table-top. Lincoln Ross has a wooden box which he attaches to a card
table. He attaches the torque meter to that. This gets the winding up
high, no bending to wind. It looks more comfortable.

I've also seen a stooge attached to a folding tripod. Looked sturdy
and the height adjustment with the tripod looked great.
