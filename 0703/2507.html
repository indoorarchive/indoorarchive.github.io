Some awesome flying on display today!

 

F1D Kagan had several flights above 21:00 and one of 22+. Crushed the site
record (by more than 6 minutes). Sova and Leppard were both doing
18:00-19:00.

 

F1L site record was also broken several times throughout the day. Bill Gowen
is currently leading with a best single flight of 11:18.

 

Gliders, PP/LPP/F1M, scale, and more lightweights tomorrow. No one has ever
broken :40 officially in our site, in CLG or HLG. Should be interesting as
there are lots of good glider flyers in attendance.
