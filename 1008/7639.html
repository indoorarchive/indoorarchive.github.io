Ben will probably post a more detailed update. John Kagan and Brett Sanborn (listed as Sunborn in the results :) finished third and fourth respectively in this years Dorcol cup, the prelude event to the 2010 World Indoor Championships. John had the longest single time on his sixth and final flight but did not have a long enough second time to win (places are determined by the sum of the two longest times out of six flights). The winner was Ivan Treger, the current (2008) world champion.

Bravo to the USA team.
