Hello Indoor List,

To those who are not here in Serbia, I would like to let you know how the
first day of flying for the Dorcol Cup was. The end of the day saw the high
time of 34:19 by Mr. Ivan Tregor. Second high time of 34:12 by the French
flier Mr. Dieber. My spelling may be wrong, so forgive me.

The air has been turbulent as per usual in the mornings and early
afternoons, with the air calming down in the evenings. There were many
fliers in F1D but only 1 in 35 CM. The competition is very close.

The weather has been hot, but not as bad as the past.

I will post photos of the results sheet on my facebook page for people to
see. If you are not my friend there, you can request to friend me and I will
add you. Or go to the Float Kickstarter page where I will also post the
photos with results.

Tomorrow is the second and final day of flying for the Dorcol Cup and then
on the the WC!
