The Banks Lmt. Pennyplane and a few others is also downloadable from
Files section of Indoor_Construction. Look for "Indoor Plans" file.

The best $23 you can spend on Indoor Anything would be Lew Gitlow's
book _Indoor Flying Models_ (google for Indoor Model Supply.) It
contains a graded progression of model plans, along with most basic
building techniques you need. And post for advice here when you get
stuck or confused about anything.
