Hi,

The Nijmeegse Luchtvaart Club would like to invite you for a new
event on the international free flight
calendar; the First International Indoor Fly-In! This event has been
run for several years on a national
level and 2008 will be the premiere for attracting flyers from abroad
as well. Our intention is to make this
a recurring event, this will however depend on interest.

The preliminary schedule for the event:
Saturday 15 March. 8.00-19.00
Sunday 16 March 8.00-18.00
A detailed schedule will be provided at the end of February to the
flyers that have entered.

Location:
The hall we will use is the Jan Massinkhal, located in the outskirts
of Nijmegen. The characteristics of
the hall are a 2400 square meters floor, height is nearly 8 meters
(Cat. 1 hall). The ceiling is fairly
smooth and unobstructed.

Contest:
We will be flying contests in several classes. The classes are: F1D,
F1M,F1M small (Juniors), Sainte,
Pistachio, F4F (Peanut) F4D (Open scale rubber) and F4E (Open scale
electric/CO2).

More info:
http://fly.to/iifi2008
rlucas_at_dds.nl

Yours,

Kevin Lamers
