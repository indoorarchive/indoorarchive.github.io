Hello Everyone

I am sad to announce the passing of Ed Stoll after a short hospitalization
at the age of 80. He was a long time member of the Cloudbuster and Detroit
Balsa Bug model clubs. Many of you may not remember or have met Ed but he
was an exceptional freeflight flyer and I believe a F1D team member in the
past. Ed gave me plenty of good advice on F1D when I started flying old
rules models back in 1996.

Fred Tellier
