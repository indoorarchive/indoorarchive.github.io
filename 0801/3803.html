At the last USIC, three of us flew AMA Event 221, Free Flight Electric: Ray
Harlan, Rob Romash, and myself.

 

(1) Will this event be flown at the 2008 Indoor Nats/USIC? I did not see it
listed on my forms.

 

(2) Ray Harlan proposed a rule change IND-09-2 that changed the battery from
no more than two 50 mAh Nickel Cadmium cells to one 10 mAh LiPo cell and
reduced the maximum weight from one ounce to 10 g. Ray's proposal, which I
support, received an 8-1 Pass vote from the Indoor Contest Board. Does this
vote complete the rules change or, are there additional votes required?
Maybe this event will be flown at some other contest if not at the USIC.
