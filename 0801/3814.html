I just sent this email to everyone I could think of at AMA headquarters:

Dear Technical Director Hahn
Attempting a World Record involved a tremendous amount of work, expense, travel and coordination before, during and after the attempt. I had dozens of emails and telephone calls to AMA. Never in that entire process was there a mention of a $250.00 recording fee for my record attempt - which was successful and has been ratified by FAI.

I refuse to pay this fee.

I also doubt that anyone will attempt an Indoor Free Flight world record in the future if it becomes known that this fee is in place.
Bill Gowen
