Are there any files showing film patching techniques ? I read somewhere
that you save patches between 2 wet sheets of newsprint, then cut the
piece to size, remove one side of newsprint and then place the patch dry
over the hole, then wick glue around the edges. I did this on my
pennyplane and it worked relatively well (in a clumsy sort of way) but
the patch, rather than laying smooth, went down like a piece of alligator
skin. Are there other techniques out there ?
