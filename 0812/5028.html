The thread on USIC history jogged my memory.

My Dad, Chuck Furutani, was one of the co-founders of the Wilmington(Calif. ) Indoor Model Aiplane Club (WIMAC). There were two major contributions/accomplishments by WIMAC during his leadership back in the 50's-60's. One was the establishment of the site categories (I - IV), the other was the developement of the EZB class.

I have some of the documentation, newsletters and correspondence, and was wondering if there was any interest or repository for this info?
