Fred Tellier patched my LPP using a bit of microfilm. It was
increadibly quick and easy, a reason to pour some microfilm.

The film was placed between newspaper, and cut to size. The paper was
removed from one side and a light spray of water on the patch and then
placing the patch in place, removing the newspaper.

There is a question of whether this is legal for many of the indoor
classes, but for pennyplane, F1M and F1D, it should be fine.
