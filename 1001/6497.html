The 1949 Plymouth Internats had a paper covered Class A ROG event for Novice (13yr max).

Like virtually all events at the time it had flowery language stating that stab area in excess of 50% of wing area was to be considered as wing area. Seems fairer than outlawing tandems, and would be consistant with other events. There were no wt rules so no one even approached 50%, much less go for a 22.5sqin tandem.
