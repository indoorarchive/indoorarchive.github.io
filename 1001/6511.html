I don't use a hot box. I do wrinkle the paper, water shrink it several times & iron it after it has air dried. I generally do this in the winter when the humidity is low. However, I have done it in the summer, too. All of my planes are several years old & do not have any warping issues or saggy covering. The covering does get slightly looser in higher humidity conditions, but is not a problem.
Gary Hodson
