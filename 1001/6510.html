I use a steam iron to iron condenser paper. I put the paper on a flat towel and then iron it flat with the steam turned on. Then I turn the steam off and iron it until it's dry. I don't have problems with it changing much after that. This technique was developed during several years of helping SO kids before the rules were mercifully changed to allow film.

I don't have to do this with film - but I guess everyone knows that.
