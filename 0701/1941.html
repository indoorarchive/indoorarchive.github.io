I don't have a drawing. If you look at a reverse S hook from the rear it looks like a backwards S. Big surprise huh? The o-ring from the motor needs to sit in the crooks of the S when the rubber is driving the prop so the size depends on the model type, rubber size and o-ring size. The S on my LPP prop is about 3/16" wide.
