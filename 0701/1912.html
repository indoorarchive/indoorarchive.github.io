Come fly in Colorado Springs with the MMM Club!

 

The MMM "Indoor Town" Spring Cat II indoor bash on March 24-25, 2007 will
feature flying both days from 9am-5pm. I was able to secure the 24th for a
reduced rate to accommodate the expected lower number of F1D flyers. Entry
fee will remain at $10 per flyer per day.

 

3/24 Sat. 9 am - 5 pm F1D, Intermediate Stick, AMA Easy B
only. Nothing heavier or faster than these-we need to protect these
superlight models. F1D regional team qualifier.

 

3/25 Sun. 9 am - 11am HLG*, Std CLG*, UCLG* only. no rubber
flying during this time.

                        11 am -2 pm LPP*, PP/F1M combined, FAC No-Cal,
FAC Peanut, Bostonian, P-24, WWII No-cal Mass Launch. Lighter models at your
own risk!

                        2 pm - 5 pm EZB, F1L*, Ministick*, A-6 only.
Nothing heavier.

 

* = NFFS Indoor National Cup sanctioned

 

Colorado Springs City Auditorium has a peak height of 37' and a smooth tiled
ceiling with a few volleyball holes in it. Still, hangups are rare. We have
10M poles available for steering.

 

More details to follow. CD is Rob Romash.

 

--Don DeLoach
