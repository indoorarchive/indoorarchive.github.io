Guys,

 

Don't forget to make your plans to attend the MMM "Pikes Peak Ceiling Climb"
2-day indoor meet on March 24-25. This is a decent Cat 2 site with a mostly
smooth ceiling that peaks at about 37'. This is a great tune up meet for the
Kent contest the next weekend.

 

--F1D regional qualifier on March 24th-9-5 all day!

--NFFS National Cup sanctioned: F1L, LPP, Ministick, HLG, SCLG, UCLG

--only $10 per flyer per day

--MMM members can pick you up from the airport(!); flying site is only 20
min away.

--reasonably priced hotels within walking distance

--Colorado Springs is only minutes away from great tourist destinations-make
it a mini-vacation!

 

More to come in the next week or so when I get a flyer produced.
