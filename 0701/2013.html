We hosted Chris Goins from Atlanta at our club meet today. Chris wowed the
crowd with one of the first indoor discus-launch gliders seen on the US.
Impressive. He was trimming it for Kent in April. His times of around 30 sec
were from a transition height of around 25-30 ft. Chris also managed to hang
up his DLG in a ceiling tile, then hang up his F1M on his hung-up glider!
I've got a pic of this monumental achievement.

 

This meet was not AMA sanctioned; therefore no National Cup points were
awarded. March 24-25th is the big one in Colorado Springs. AMA and NC
sanctioned, and F1D regional qualifier. Email ddeloach_at_comcast.ent for a
flyer!

 

--Don DeLoach
