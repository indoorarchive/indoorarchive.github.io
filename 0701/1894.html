I use a couple nails in a vise. Cut the heads off and file the ends round
so you won't cut yourself on the sharp ends. Put them side by side in the
vise and file flat faces on opposite sides of part of the length to clamp in the
vise. Put the flat sections in the vise with your wire between them and
clamp the vise down. Bend the wire around the nails.
