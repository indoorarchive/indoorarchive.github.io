 In the Chicago area, the Woodland Aeromodelers host an indoor flying night on Thursdays most weeks through April. They use the gym at Trinity Lutheran School (Category I) in Burr Ridge, IL. AMA membership is required to fly and a $5 donation is requested. The session starts at 7:00 PM CST and ends at 10:00. The members generally fly rubber powered own-design planes. I tune up FAC planes for Racine there.
