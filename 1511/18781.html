 I would like to encourage the indoor community to join us in the Slanic Salt Mine for the 2016 Indoor World Championship.
 

 I believe it will be the most competitive world championship in history. I count SEVEN (7) fliers who are neck-to-neck for the top spot. I consider Zoltan Sukosd of Hungary to be the favorite, but the other SIX are very very close. If you are familiar with the F1D community, you should know who the six top contenders are..

 

 Come and support your friends, team, and the indoor community. Come as a helper or to give moral support. Come as a spectator to witness the historic showdown. It's difficult to predict how many more indoor world championships will be held in the future.
 

 You can also come a couple of days before the contest and fly your F1D in the Salt Mine.
 

 It will be a great time. The Romanians are great hosts. Flying ends each day by 5 pm, and there will be plenty of time for socializing. In-country costs are low, and you can eat and drink like a king (or queen)!
 

 The official dates are April 12 to April 17, 2016. I hope to see you there.
 

 -Kang
