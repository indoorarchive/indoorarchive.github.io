The reason that the P-100 is thicker is because its filaments are thicker
than those in M60J (10um vs 5um).
Looking at the grams/meter the P-100 fibre seems about 3 times as thick
(0.32 vs 0.103), of course neglecting the difference in density.
Separating in smaller bundles will do the trick of course.
