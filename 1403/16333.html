Slight tweaks below. What does everyone think of the various tasks?

 Mike, all tasks would be required for each level.
 

 Chris, ceiling height would certainly make a difference - as would model selection for the various tasks. All variables that would be up to the individual.
 

 LIFA (League of Indoor FreeFlight Achievement)
  
 Level 1:
 - 5 minute Indoor rubber flight
  
 Level 2:
 - 10 minute Indoor rubber flight
 - 30 second Indoor glider flight
  
 Level 3:
 - 20 minute Indoor rubber flight
 - 5 minutes with a cabin model
 - Attend 2 contests
  
 Level 4:
 - 30 minute Indoor rubber flight
 - 1 minute Indoor glider flight
 - Attend 3 out-of-state contests
 - Obtain a CD license
  
 Level 5:
 - 40 minute Indoor rubber flight
 - 10 minutes with a cabin model
 - Win 3 events at sanctioned local or out-of-state contests
 - Write an Indoor FF article for publication
 - CD an Indoor FF contest
