They are .006" These are the ones I use specifically:
http://www.ebay.com/itm/Vintage-Box-Star-Razor-Blades-5-Per-Box-NOS-/162587723513?hash=item25dafbc6f9:g:m6kAAOSwEUVZZUe-

Sometime a big box of 20 will come up for about $20.

The Treet Classic carbon blades I have measure about .004". I found the
thinner blades would sometimes dig into the balsa when planing. So
stayed with the Star blades.
