I bought one of these folding tailgating tables last year. Was going to
mention it in the indoor column in the AMA magazine and buy a second one
but then they disappeared from the stores so they definitely are a
seasonal item as they have been out of stock online for about 9 months.
They are now available online again.

https://www.walmart.com/ip/Mainstays-6-Fold-In-Half-Tailgate-Table-Multiple-Colors/51686669?variantFieldId=actual_color

Last year in the stores they had several different colors, red, white,
and black so I bought a red one. I was drawn to these tables as they are
lighter and thinner compared to other plastic folding tables that are
6ft long. The legs lock down and it also locks in the open position and
has a cloth handle as well. The stores around me only now only sell
these in black year round, they do not stock the colors anymore. The
colors you have to order online but if you add in a small item you can
get free shipping with a $35 order. I bought a small toy for my dog on
clearance to get the $35 threshold. I thought I would mention these
tables if a person is looking for a low cost folding table to take to an
indoor contest. My dad has been using the red one I bought last summer
so if you have seen a red table at the contests we have been at lately
these are the same tables.
