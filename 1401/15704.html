No problems at this end. I log out when I'm done and automatically when I close my browser - if I forget to do so manually.

If you DON'T have "clear cookies/cache/chronic at close" checked in your browser settings (that will auto log-out everything at browser close) You can leave yourself open to hacking. Okay, it makes "life" easier to just go to the site and type away, but the few seconds it takes to log in (along with a decent password) does make life easier in the long run.
