I've been thinking about making a winder for the left-handed 12% among us (only 10% for girls). Turns out that there's little change to the winder itself, in fact the brake is easier to apply in a left hended version. The big change is in the counter module which has to be made in mirror image to the regular one. I have to make up a prototype to be sure the reed switch picks up the rotor signal. I'll make it an option, which can also be used to upgrade old ones with the big 7111 counter. Art.
