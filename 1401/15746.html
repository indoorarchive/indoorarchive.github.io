Finally I have uploaded the whole movie (70 minutes). It is a treat for
those who are going to this year WC!

It will give you a great impression of flying a championship in the
saltmine. This was not the last 65 cm WC, I believe we had one more WC in
Slanic before we changed the rules. All models you see where covered in
microfilm.

Unfortunately this was done before the days of HD, so it is standard
resolution.

Enjoy and leave some comments.
