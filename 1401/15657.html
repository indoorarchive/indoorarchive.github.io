Hi LeRoy.

No video, but here is what I do for LPP or A6 blades.

Apply glue to spar when it is in the pitch gauge. Put blade in place. Using bits of blue tape, adhere the blade to the pitch gauge. If needed, use bits of tape to hold the spar to the blade. Let dry.
Adjust using acetone if necessary.

Regards.
Mike Kirda
