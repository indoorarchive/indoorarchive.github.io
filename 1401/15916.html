He described the construction such that it is rowing spread to as thin
and even layer as possible, thus solid carbon and epoxy, no balsa. I
tried to copy that construction, but found out that it is really hard t
spread the carbon, without leaving too large holes. I do not recall how
much the tubes weight, but I recall it was comparable to balsa tube with
4 borons. Not 180 mg as Kang requested, but rather 250 to 280 mg.
