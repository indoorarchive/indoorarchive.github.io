Gary,

I believe Gatorfoam Board is intended for outdoor use and denser foam with a
wood fiber veneer laminate impregnated with resin for water resistance.
Foamboard is polystyrene foam core coated with a paper stock.

 

I purchase foamboard in bulk from Blick Studio. Up to 40" x 60" inch sheets
in white. 48" x 96" in black. They also offer Gatorfoam. They usally have
offers for free shipping on smaller sizes and also look for the discount
coupons.
