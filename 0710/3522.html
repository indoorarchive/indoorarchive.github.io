Hello Indoor fans,

Are you making plans to join us in Colorado Springs this indoor season? Our
first one-day meet is fast approaching--November 18th (Sunday) 9-5. This is
an AMA sanctioned, NFFS National Cup event, as is our big 2-day "Pikes Peak
Ceiling Climb" January 5-6, 2008.

Our site is the Colorado Springs City Auditorium, a Cat II (37') site with a
mostly smooth ceiling.

Send me an email for contest fliers, travel, lodging info, etc. The COS
airport is only 20 minutes from the site and MMM club members can pick you
up!

Regards,
