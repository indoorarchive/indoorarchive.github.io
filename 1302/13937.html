Dear (European) Indoor Friends,

I noticed that in the "Victor of Belgrade" -contest (open international
preceding the EuroChamps) there will be only F1D, no other classes (F1M,
F1L, F1R). I mailed the contest director asking if it was possible to add
those (as it is the only possibility to fly those classes in the Fair), but
got a reply that as the classes are unpopular and getting prizes for them
would be costly, they will not be included. If you feel that those should
be also contested (I think that a printed diploma would well suffice for a
prize, most of all I would like the possibility to attempt for a national
record) it would be good if you e-mailed him and requested for those extra
classes!



-Tapio-
