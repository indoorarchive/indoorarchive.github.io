This is a fun contest in a great site. More hours this year.

One of the NFFS Indoor FF Committee's goals is to support a network of national caliber sites to cover the US. No one site will be easily accessible by everyone.

This one is admittedly somewhat remote, but it does cover an otherwise under-served area in the southwest. (And the scenery on the drive in from Albuquerque is spectacular!)

If you are in the region, or are willing to travel, please put this on your calendar. Participation will keep it going.
