Point taken on who the "NFFS" is!
I have never flown P-24, but I did check with Tem Johnson who does. After some discussion, one suggestion would be to allow those who have the Mace props to continue using them if they want to because the event has a minimum weight which negates any weight advantage of a lighter prop. People with existing models would not have to modify them to adapt the new prop & still meet the minimum weight. Tem was not sure what other props are currently available so could not comment on their weight & efficiency. If the decision is to only allow currently available props, he will not have any issue with that either, as long as the decision is made & announced soon.
