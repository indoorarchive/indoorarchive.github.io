I have ordered wood a couple of times from them over the past 18 months. The "C" grain I ordered (.006 for 1/2 A and .012 for F1L) for prop blades was very good; however, most of the "A" grain has been very wavy. I suppose it would be okay for curved outlines, but I am not sure I can straighten them enough for straight planforms. I did not complain to them but have considered just buying the remaining IMS wood instead of TWIB. In their (A2Z's) defense, I should say that in the past I had ordered some 6" wide blade plastic props that were badly warped. I called and after I returned the defective props they replaced them with good ones, but I still had to pay the postage to fix the problem. I hope your issue is resolved soon.
James Alderson
