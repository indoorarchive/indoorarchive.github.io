This would be really interesting to determine a base line for new comers.
However, its difficult to say what will happen model to model. The more
efficient the model is the wider the torque range can be. Because with
respect to energy usage, everything happens more slowly as the model
becomes more efficient.

I am still not sure I fully understand how changing the high pitch effects
range of torques the hub can change over. It would seem the higher the high
pitch, the longer the hub would need to take to go from open to closed.
