Got to try a couple flights in the gym, pretty wild !!!

First flight on about 500 turns looked decent, about 40 sec. Then the
2nd, put in 900 turns, nice climb - steep at first - then settled down
to easy climb, second circle bounced of a roof girder, nice recovery but
then got under the heater fans (mini tornado), one half outside loop,
recovered, next a neat snap roll, recovered, another something aerobatic
then only a few bumps to landing at just over 60 secs.

Class was impressed as was the teacher, wants me to build a lighter one !!!

wombat - but it did survive !!
