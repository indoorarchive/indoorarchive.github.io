This is to remind you that there will be indoor model airplane building
and flying in the Moreland Community Center Gym on Sunday, June 7, from 6
to 8 PM. The address is 1850 Fallbrook Avenue in San Jose, 95130.
Fallbrook is off Campbell Avenue, between Saratoga Avenue and San Tomas
Expressway. Flyers 19 or over pay $6 and flyers 18 or under pay $3.
Building instruction for kids is available at $10 per 2-hour session.
Separate materials fees are payable to the instructor. There is no
charge for observers.

Outdoor Flying Field

On Wednesday we met again with County Parks staff to begin the permit
process. OCD will be granted an exclusive permit to fly free flight
rubber and hand launch models at Harvey Bear Ranch. This will be a one
year pilot project. Parks staff will prepare a draft permit as we gather
our insurance documents. We will meet again in the near future to
complete the process and sign the papers. It looks like we may be able
to begin flying there within a month.

It would be nice to open the field with a kids AMA Cub contest. What do
you say, kids?

Hope to see you Sunday.
