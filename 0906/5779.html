Pete; This is a pic of how I make my strippers. The easiest way is to
glue a piece of balsa the thickness you want on the side of a bit of
hardwood an 1'8" or so up from the bottom, then glue a piece of razor
blade on the balsa. You can make a set of various sizes that way on the
cheap.
