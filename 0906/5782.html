> The PPP film from Roy White is perfect for a LPP. In fact, two of the "P"'s in PPP stand for Penny Plane. It is .7 micron film.
>
> For the other roll: colorless equals heavy. Which would also be perfect for a LPP (although probably not the lighter classes). In the past there have been films sold in the Indoor FF market with thicknesses of .3-4 microns, .5 microns, .7 microns, .9 microns, on up to 1.5 microns. Yours is probably in the .9 and up upper end.
