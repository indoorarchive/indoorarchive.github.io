There was some discussion of this subject earlier this year when I asked a similar question. Maybe you can look back through the messages and dig it up. I settled on glass. I bought a 12 x 24 piece of plate glass with finished edges from a glass shop in Decatur. I think it cost about $13. It is an excellent surface for sanding glider parts as well as building frames for duration models. I use 3/4" steel nuts and a steel straight edge to locate parts. If I need to use a plan I slip it under the glass. It works great. When I get glue on it I scrape it with a single edge razor and it's like new again.
