I make a template out of 1/32" thick plastic. I also make a spacer out of a piece of scrap balsa & place it & the template on top of the piece of balsa I will be cutting ribs out of. I cut one rib, then slice a strip the thickness of one rib off the spacer move the template down & cut another rib. Repeat.
