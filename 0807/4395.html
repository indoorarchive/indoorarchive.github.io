I just received the official sanction from the AMA for the Lakehurst
Mid-Summer Open, July 26th and 27th.

- All AMA Indoor Classes
- Sanctioned Record Trials both Days
- Certificates to Winners in all flown events

License, Insurance and Registration (-must- be current... Cars have

card... You can contact Rob Romash if you need an ECIM card...
