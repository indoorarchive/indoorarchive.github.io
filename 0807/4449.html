About the F1d Team "qualifier" Regional--it's supposed to be separate
from the "Kibbie Dome Annual F1d". The Regional Qualifier's purpose
is to earn the right to compete at Team Finals along with picking
up "bonus points" to be applied to future Team Finals scores.
The "Kibbie Dome Annual" is supposed to be considered an AMA event
along with the other KD events, and in the past, has been awarded the
usual merchandise prize. So, some F1d flyers (at least I did) opt to
put best flight times on "Kibbie Dome Annual" sheet in hope for a
merchandise award, and others are more interested in bonus points
(the "other" sheet.) As it turned out, this concept is apparently
too confusing, and neither contest got any regular awards at
ceremony, unlike past years. Anyhow, sorry I didn't copy Regional
times, but I think the order was 1. Steve Brown (pair of 32s) 2.Mark
Bennett 3. Cezar Banks 4. Larry Norval.
