  Dear Indoor Community,

  I'm again with you, wishing to tell you about good news from Belgrade...

  On Saturday, July 5. 2008., in Belgrade fair hale 1, was held Serbian
  National
  Championship in indoor models, with big success: so Serbia is reached with 2
  new
  national records: in "35 cm Open" with 26 minutes, and 27 sec, and in F1D
  with
  34 minutes and 08 sec (both are mine). Results of competition are in the
  file
  section under title NCh Indoor Belgrade 2008.xls.

  Organizer of competition was new Air Club "Free flyer" from Belgrade, owners
  brooders Bosko&Lazar Lacimic, who have regard as capable to organize very
  good
  Indoor competition.

  Due to big efforts of Mr. Ljubomir Radosavljevic, Director of WCh F1D 2008.,
  hale 1 in Belgrade is now absolutely ready for WCh, as one of the best
  places for
  indoor flying in the World. There is no more anything hanging from roof, and
  no
  obstacles are there more. Hale 1 is wonderful for flying, with no or very
  small air
  movement, so the model lands on the same place where you started it.
  In the central roof zone is a concrete circle of about 20-25 m, near as flat
  plate,
  height about 28-29 m (to our measurements with balloon and rope). So the
  hale
  is according to FAI in the Categories 3. That's make the hale 1 as on ideal
  place
  to make new World Records. And we expect that's will happens in August at
  WCh.
  Temperature in the hale was 28-33 grade Celsius.

  Wishing you all the best,
  Slobodan Midic
