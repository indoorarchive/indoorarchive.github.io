Today I received my annual mailing announcing the upcoming publication
of the National Free Flight Society Symposium. One orders by becoming a
donor.

are outdoor, some are indoor, and many apply to both. This is one of the
major FF publications of the world. I just ordered (aka became a patron)
via PayPal. You can also mail in a check.

Thanks to Indoor_Construction members who contributed to this effort.
