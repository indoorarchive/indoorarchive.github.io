The message below is being distributed the Free Flight community at large to help get the word out about the 2013 Nats/USIC.
The color flyer, Black & White flyer & 1/4 page advertisement were recently uploaded to the Files, Events, 2013 USIC folder for this group.
The other three attachments listed below were already in the same location.
 
Please help us to promote this event by providing the information to your local club newsletter, web master & local hobby shops. If you need any of these attachments sent to you directly contact me thru this group or directly.
Gary Hodson
warthodson_at_aol.com
 
 
The annual AMA Indoor Free Flight "Nats"/NFFS United States Indoor Championships ("USIC") has a new location!
This year the annual combined event will be held at the University of Illinois Armory in Champaign, IL on August 7-11, 2013. The Armory is a Cat. III site.
 

The Nats/USIC schedule includes AMA, NFFS, FAC, & FAI events, so there is something for everyone.
 
You are encouraged to publish or share as much of this information as possible to help inform the Free Flight community about this unique annual opportunity to participate in a relaxed, enjoyable, seriously fun competition - no prior experience required.
  
The attachments include:
color flyer
B&W flyer
1/4 page advertisement
schedule of events
FAC schedule of events
USIC/NATS Supplemental Rules & General Information
 

The B&W flyer & 1/4 page advertisement are 300 DPI, gray scale PDFs.
Note, the quarter page ad has crops and a key line for production purposes.
Please let me know if you will need the original file for final production or in a different format.

 
The following links provide additional assistance:
 
General Information: http://www.modelaircraft.org/files/NATSINDOOR.pdf
 
Registration form for the AMA supported events: http://www.modelaircraft.org/files/2013NATSENTRY.pdf
 
Registration form for the NFFS supported events: http://www.modelaircraft.org/files/2013unofficialentryfm.pdf
 
Due to the size of the information package I may have to transmit it as attachments in two or more separate emails.
