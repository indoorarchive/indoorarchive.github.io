I just got confirmation from Walt that we have secured March 28 and 29 for the West Baden contest this year. Please let me know if you plan to attend so I can do an estimate of the entry fees. The cost will be the same as last year - $2500 for the two days. We may have a sponsor for one of the days. I hope to get that nailed down when I do the entry fee estimate.

If you want to stay at the hotel you can get the $149 per night rate by using the code 0415IMA (that's a letter i after the 5).

For the F1D flyers there are National and World Cat 3 records up for grabs. This would be a great time to try for one or both. Also F1R National Cat 3 records can be set.

If you know of people who aren't on the indoor list but may come please contact them with this info or let me know how to do it. (Loucka, etc.)
 

 Bill Gowen
