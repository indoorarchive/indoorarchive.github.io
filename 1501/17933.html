Hi Leo,

I do have prop outlines available for sale - in unfinished form.
I don't believe these will be competitive unless finish sanded by the purchaser to remove weight.
This qualifies as 'building' to me.
Similar to how I can purchase a folding prop assembly from Volare/Superior Props for several OT Rubber outdoor models - Those are even more assembled. No one would question their use.

I have also sold a couple of hub kits. These are no more than somewhat prefabricated parts and all require assembly by the purchaser.
Again, similar to Superior, they cannot be used as sold and must be 'built'.

I have also sold pre-finished prop molds. I have a far easier layup formula so the center layer requires very little sanding compared to Brett's method. It will be published in INAV soon.

I am very cognizant of the BOM rule and support it. I do not believe anything that I sell violates either the letter or intent of the BOM rule. I'm also quite open about my methods, nothing is secret about them.
