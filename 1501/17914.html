I calibrate wire bearings using a fixture and wire edm machine with 0.002 wire to get hole and pigtail perfect size and alignment. On aluminum bearings i can use samemtechnology on pigtail to slice the slot at 45 deg that is 0.0024 wide an improovement on the 0.006 thick slitting saw blade. By switching to oil diectric fluid the machine tool from water i can go down to 0.0005 wire giving a cut slot size of 0.0007 inch. I think this is a plus over the slitting saw.
