Kibbie Dome Annual 2015
  
 If we have problems, one of them sure isn’t a lack of great places to fly.
  
 Andrew Tagliafico is finalizing the dates for the 2015 Kibbie Dome Annual. He is planning for June 18th – 22nd.
  
 The deal will be the same as 2014: We need to get commitments from people who will attend. Then, we will calculate the entry fee based on the site cost (expected to be the same as recent years) divided by the number of commitments. If we get a similar number of people as previous years, the cost should be around $200 for 5 days of unlimited flying.
  
 If we get enough people to cover the cost, the contest is on. If we don’t, then it isn’t.
  
 
 This is one of the finest sites in the world – let’s keep it alive!
 

 Once Andrew gets the final dates, I will set up a poll to collect attendees.
