Alan,

Belated thanks for the note about the passing of Beth Krush. I talked with both Joe and Beth Krush a few years ago at some of the early USICs. Joe's 777 Mini-Stick was a good design that I flew and Beth illustrated the first Winning Indoor Designs book. I knew only a little about their work in art and illustration. I tried to get my son Andy to talk with them and get their advice since his interests were similar. So far as I know, he never talked with them, but he has another children's book just released anyway. Maybe advice from both Krushes would have helped him even more.

Fred Rash

Hi all,
     I have some very sad news to pass on to you all. Joe Krush's wife Beth has passed away. Until recently she attended most of the indoor flying events held at Lakehurst hanger 1 and allowed Joe to reside at the SOTS (Scale Old Timers Society) as president of the club. She was a fine artist and had provided NFFS with lots of art work, and sketches for the various news letters and digests. I speak for the club in sending my sincere condolences to Joe.
