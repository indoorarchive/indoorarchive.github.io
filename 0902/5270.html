Today we reached 500 members in Indoor_Construction. It appears no
matter how many sign up, the same 15-20 are going to be the posters. A
couple hundred have said they are interested in "getting back" into
indoor, and about 40 or so are connected via Wright Sruff. I presume
some find what they hoped to learn by searching post archives, files
and photos.

Message to newbies--the most important question you can ask here or
anywhere is "Is indoor free flight flown near me, and who are the
flyers nearest me?"
