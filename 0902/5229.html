I was also about to suggest silicone. I recently learned from an RC
flying fella whom I have been able to convert into indoor FF, something
new about those RC car silicones: the shock oils tend to be somewhat
thin (from F1B uses I prefer something like ~15,000 centitokes, about as
thick as sugar syrup, thicker than the maple syrup you get in Montreal),
but diff oil used in RC cars is much thicker, and by blending these two
you get precisely the viscosity you prefer....
