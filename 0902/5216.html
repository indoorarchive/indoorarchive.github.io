The US Indoor Championships (USIC) and AMA Indoor FF Nats will be held in the East Tennessee State University Mini-Dome in Johnson City, TN (USA) on May 27-31, 2009. The USIC has been held at this site annually for more than 20 years. Events include several FF rubber duration events, several rubber scale events, indoor FF electric duration (AMA Event 221), gliders (Standard Catapult, Unlimited Catapult, Hand launch, and Indoor Towline), rubber speed, and Race-To-Roof. A few events are mass launches. Ceiling height is approximately 116 feet.

There should be 50-75 contestants, possibly more. Come and fly with us.
