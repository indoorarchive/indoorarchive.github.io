Fred,

Some quick detail- Beth and Joe did many illustration projects
including the "Borrowers" children's series, Reader's Digest and a
host of others. Both taught at Bryn Mawr for generations and still
taught art on occaision. Beth was a humble, sweet soul who had a
great sense of humor. She was always supportive of Joe (94 yrs young)
in his flying endeavors and frequented many gatherings. She would
draw, knit or just enjoy conversation. Both Joe and Beth were
extremely fond of each other as well as the world around them.

Tom Sanders
