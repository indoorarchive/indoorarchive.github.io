Here's an update to my travel box problem/solution. The trunk that I
had on hand had a combined l+w+h of 63" which is 1" more than Delta
will take for the standard $15 checked baggage charge. I sent them an
email asking what the charge would be for a trunk measuring 63" and was
told $175!

I have on order a steel clad trunk with recessed wheels on one end that
measures exactly 62" according to the literature. This trunk costs
about $150 from Rose Trunk (model RST100) and ships free. I'll post
another update when it arrives.
