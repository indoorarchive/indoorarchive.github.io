Hi Jim,

I was given a height of 35' to the bottom of the girders in the center. They have a very low camber, so that measurement won't change much as you move outward.

The girders are tall and spaced pretty far apart, though. The ceiling above the girders is supposed to be 50' from the floor.

You might be able to transition a glider up in that space. Maybe even fly a ministick. My F1D got up there and found a vertical beam pretty quickly.
