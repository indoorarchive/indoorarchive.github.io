Hi Y'all,

I am happy to announce that the National Free Flight Society is sponsoring a
"1/2 off" offer on new 2-year memberships. If you are 19 years old or over, 2
years is just $29.00. For Junior memberships 18 years and younger, 2 years is
just $9.00. This is being made possible by a matching gift offer from free
flighter Roy Hanson.

Here is the link from our webmaster, Alan Petersen.

http://www.freeflight.org/JLF/MembershipOffer.htm

The first 25 who join, and notify me off-list that they have done so, will be
sent the December 2010 issue of the NFFS Digest, with the Tom Iacobellis 19
minute LPP Record Setter plan and article.

Any problems with the link, or if you would like to sign up by check or money
order using the mail, contact me at the addresses below.
