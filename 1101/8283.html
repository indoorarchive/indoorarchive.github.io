The EAA AirVenture museum has extended the the Model Airplane exhibit
through the 2011 AirVenture meet in July (it was previously going to be
taken down at the start of this year). That means the display will be
available during the April 1-3 Indoor FF contest.

Just one more reason to head out for this unique experience. Serious Indoor
FF Friday and Saturday evening, fun-flying during the days, lots of great
full-scale displays, and now the model aviation exhibit, too.

I know Buxton, Romash, Leo, and myself are going. Who else?

I'll need a list of events that people want to fly so I can put together a
schedule, too.

If there is enough interest, this will be an F1D team selection qualifying
contest, also.
