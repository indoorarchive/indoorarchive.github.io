Hi Phedon,

Unfortunately you are wrong.

Test components constructed with pre-preg composites for the whole screw arm
assembly and also the main yoke of an F1D hub have been made by F1D modellers
here in GB and probably elsewhere. These show that very thin pre-preg is
extremely light as well as stiff and gives slightly lighter weights in
comparison to traditional balsa or basswood versions of these components.

CIAO.

Nick.
