I made a little step in the right direction today. I recently ordered a bunch of music wire from Small Parts. Most of it came in 6' lengths, packaged in 6' long clear plastic tubes and sent in a 6' heavy duty cardboard mailing tube.

This stuff has been driving me crazy because it's so inconvenient to get at. Today I took all the wire out and cut it into 18" lengths and then cut the plastic tubes into 18" lengths to hold it. This left a lot of plastic tubing unused. I discovered that one of the long partitions in my tool box will hold 12 of these tubes cut into 12" lengths. So by clearing the junk out of 2 of these partitions I can now keep labeled and separated about 24 of these tubes to hold wire, balsa sticks, bamboo, carbon rods or any other material that will fit in one.
