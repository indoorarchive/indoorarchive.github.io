We had a very nice turn out at the hangar! Thanks to all for your support.

On
 Saturday, Stan Buddenbohm broke the indoor hand launch glider by three
seconds! There were at least two 1:52 flights and several slightly less,
 but still over the record!

On Sunday, we were joined by the
Blacksheep Club and those guys always have fun. To add to the
festivities, we shared the space with the Goodyear Blimp!

Brian was there to take his great photos and they are posted at: http://www.flickr.com/photos/44670670_at_N02/sets/72157626832462129/

Paul Love shot video of Stan's record flight, see the HPA Thread: http://www.hippocketaeronautics.com/hpa_forum/index.php?topic=7017.0

Watch for our next hangar date!

- Norm
