If you find a nice, high density (18# - 22#) balsa sheet, grab it if
you use spruce for "stronger" spars. All my balsa in the above density
range is stronger and stiffer than spruce at about 22#. Spruce breaks
at less bending.

Just an observation.
Bruce in Seattle
