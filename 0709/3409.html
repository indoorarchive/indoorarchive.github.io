Anyone got any initial thoughts about the 08 SO "twist" requirement? It
seems to me that if they really wanted to enforce the "opaque"
requirement, it would eliminate Esaki as a covering and require painting
the covering material. So, Design Floral spray onto the film?

Also, as stated, it would appear that one might approach this req't by
turning the area between the wing posts into a minimum thickness pylon
by adding two horizontal spacers between the wing posts at the base of
the wing and maybe 2/3'rds of the way from the wing to the design fuse
point to allow for incidence adjustments and then applying some sort of
colored, opaque material between a rough rectangle formed by the wing
posts, and the upper and lower spacers. Any ideas?
