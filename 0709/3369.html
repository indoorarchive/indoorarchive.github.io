great deal, nice very long poles with strong tip, but they are having
some hopefully short term delivery problems with th extra long ones...

for rescue I recommend some tip attachments- foam ball, coat hanger
forklift, box knife in a hook "pull cutter" configuration, and hook with
separate pull cord...Pulling down too hard on a telescoping pole can
damage it. Use it to send up a hook with line and pull down on the line
instead.

(chain saw would be useful too, but it might be a bit heavy for the pole
