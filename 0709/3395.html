The basics:

Monoplane
Plane minimum mass 7 g
Projected wing span 50 cm
Wing cord 7 cm
Projected stabilizer span 30 cm
Stab cord 4.5 cm
Prop single, two bladed, commercially made
Prop diameter 24 cm
Motor maximum mass 2 g

At State tournaments 20% bonus for pusher
At National tournament 30% bonus for canard

Twists: At least one vertical surface must be covered in opaque, non-
white material.
Clarifications about check-in of motors and logs.
