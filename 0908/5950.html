Indoor Free Flight Community
c/o John Kagan

Dear Indoor FF Community,

On behalf of the Academy of Model Aeronautics, Inc., I would like to thank you for the $1,100 donation toward the 2009 Indoor FF Nats, received on July 15, 2009.

We appreciate the efforts and donations of Mr. Tim Goldstein, Mr. Roy White, Mr. Ray Harlan, Mr. Jeff Hood, Mr. Tony Pavel, and the numerous other competitors and individuals that participated in the raffle. The generosity of groups and individuals are vital in our effort to develop a world-class flying site and support educational and competitive programs. The impact of these contributions as been enormous and is appreciated by the entire aeromodeling community.

Thank you again for your generous support.

Sincerely,

Jim Cherry
Executive Director
