John,

On behalf of the Academy, please accept my thanks for the generous donation of $1,100.00. Please also pass our thanks along to all of the competitors, vendors, and other participants who made the raffle a success.

I agree that this effort shows a commitment by you and many of your peers to the future of Indoor Free Flight and the National Model Airplane Championships.

It's also good to hear that the Indoor Free flight community is actively pursuing new ways to generate new and greater interest in a discipline that has its roots in the very foundation of model aviation.

Best of luck with your plans for the 2010 Indoor Free Flight Nats.

Sincerely,

Dave Mathewson
AMA President
