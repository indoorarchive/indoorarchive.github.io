I would recommend some good strong balsa perhaps with carbon fibre reinforcement for the fuselage. I believe that depron is too easily crushed and won't survive bad nosedives. Make the noseweight with plenty of plasticene or modelling clay so that it can squash on impact and absorb some of the energy.
