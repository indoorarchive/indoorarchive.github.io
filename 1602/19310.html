Today, if someone asked me how they could get started in indoor on their own, I would recommend a Wright Stuff kit from Freedom Flight models. Why? Because everything you need to build and fly the model is included, except glue and knife -- including, covering film, covering frame(!), rubber, and GREAT INSTRUCTIONS. One of the difficulties of getting started is getting all the required building materials, and these kits include everything.
 

 This is how I started in 2010. I could not have gotten started without one of these kits.
 

 I would like to see that we make an all-inclusive LPP kit available. It's main purpose is to get people started. Let's move ahead and do this.
 

 -Kang
