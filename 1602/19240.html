Hi Don.

There are four motels in Rantoul:
http://www.tripadvisor.com/Hotels-g36586-Rantoul_Illinois-Hotels.html http://www.tripadvisor.com/Hotels-g36586-Rantoul_Illinois-Hotels.html
Driving by Rantoul Motel, I wouldn't stay there.

I've stayed at the Magnuson. It was fine for me, a cheap place to spend the night.
Jeff stayed at the Super 8 and reported that it was also decent.
I've not been to the Days Inn.

Now that said, 15 minutes away there are probably a dozen hotels on the north side of Champaign/Urbana.
They also are mostly reasonably priced and decent. I haven't stayed at any down there that I wouldn't stay at again.

There is a camp ground in Rantoul. I've read it isn't tent friendly, just RVs.

Hope this helps.
