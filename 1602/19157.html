There is some evidence that they should be different diameters.
As in larger diameter for lower ceiling.
The rubber sizes would be different too, which means a very different 'engine'.
Hence a lot of spring optimization could be done too.

Think lower ceiling = larger diameter/thicker motor
High ceiling = smaller diameter/thinner motor

This based solely on observation of what others do.
