 Please go to the AMA web and down load the P-18 proposal. It is the only one in the
 Indoor category. You will see that it has been proposed as a Provisional event
 meaning that records will not be kept. This will allow the rules to be refined in the face of additional
 competition with out having the effect on records.
 

 The basic design of the P-18 has been around for many years and this proposal is designed
 to bring the a set of rules out for the total community to test and refine.
