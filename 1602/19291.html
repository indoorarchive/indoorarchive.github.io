Here are the links to the 2016 Nats forms:

Official events Nats entry form: (Note Indoor is listed as May 23-27 2012)

http://www.modelaircraft.org/files/2016OfficalNatseventsentryform.pdf

Unofficial events Nats entry form:
http://www.modelaircraft.org/files/2016UnofficialNatseventsentryform.pdf

Here are some anomalies I see besides the incorrect Indoor Nats dates:

event 204 ROG Cabin is missing on the official form but on the Nats schedule

event 214 (AROG) is listed on the official form as "ROG" and also on
unofficial form as "AROG"

event 221 "Electric Duration" is listed on the official form and
"Electric FF" on the unofficial form. Are these the same?

The unofficial form has "P18 sportsman" listed twice, "embryo endurance"
listed twice, "SciOly Glider" listed twice

"P18 Sportsman" and "P18 Pro/Am" not on schedule

I think there also needs to be a clarification sheet with the unofficial
events rules or links to rules for clarification. Such as the difference
between FAC Nocal and Nocal scale, rules for coconut scale,P-24, P-18
rules, towline glider, RTR, the difference between "Sportsman" and
"Pro/Am" (I though sportsman events were pro/ams). I will gladly assist
with this compilation of data into one sheet if it does not already exist.
