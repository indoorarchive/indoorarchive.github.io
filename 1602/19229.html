The contest is barely a month away...March 6, Saturday. Category II, 37' smooth parabolic ceiling, site of the 2015 AMA Nats.


Twelve hours of flying, 9-9, with the usual mix of AMA, FAI and FAC events. New this year: Towline Glider. Detailed flyer coming soon.


Fly into COS and our club members will give you a ride to the flying site and hotels. Ship your models to my address and I'll gladly haul them. I like six blocks from the site.


PLEASE support this contest with your entry fees or donations. The city has raised the rent on us again. It'll cost $660 for the whole day. Not terrible but pricier than we are used to. We need 17 flyers at $40 each or 20 at $33 just to break even.


Thank you in advance for your support.
