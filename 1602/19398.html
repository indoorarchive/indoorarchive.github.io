Kang can give more details. The USA proposed site is the atrium of the West Baden Springs Hotel in West Baden Springs, Indiana, USA. This an excellent Cat III site and the host to past Indoor World Championships as well as many USA contests. Many Cat III US and World records have been set in the site. For the past several years, a contest has been held each spring in the Atrium and this year's contest will be held on the weekend of March 19-20, 2016.
