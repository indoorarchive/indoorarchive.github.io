> For anyone interested, there is a new FAI Blue Book for teams in 2014 and
> beyond. http://www.modelaircraft.org/files/2014_BLUE_BOOK.pdf
>
> Some notable changes:
>
> - Funding is now a $1,000 flat amount per team member (including TM)
>
> - 100% funding requires 75 points, comprised of participation and
> performance metrics. The program gets 2 points per Team Selection Finals
> participant (3 points per Junior participant). The max performance value is
> 50 points (which we currently have achieved). This means that we need 13
> Senior participants and 5 Junior participants in the finals for full
> funding of each team. If a team has less than 75 points, but more than 36
> points, they receive 50% funding.
>
> - The Senior team consists of 3 team members and a team manager. The
> Junior team consists of just 3 team members. The FAI Executive Committee
> can approve additional members (like a Junior TM).
