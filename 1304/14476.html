Steve Brown will be part of the model aviation demonstration. Depending on air conditions inside the hangar, he will fly either F1D, F1L, or LPP (or whatever else he thinks would work).

The Harbor Soaring Society, with the support of NFFS and AMA, is working with Orange County officials to put model aviation as one of the official use activities of the new recreational center that will be built/renovated on the site of the Tustin hangars.
