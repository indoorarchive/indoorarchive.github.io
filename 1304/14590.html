I was watching this video today and thought I point out a few things:

- From 1:30 to 2:15. F1D flying near F1M (Kang's F1D, Coslick's F1M)
- 2:40 to 3:15. Leo's cool geobatic F1L. You can get a glimpse as the model passes past the lights.
- 3:15 to 3:45. Larry Coslick's Cat II record EZB. Same as Abra Ca Dabra but using a big flaring prop. Cruising at about 55 RPM.
- 3:45 to 5:00. Kang's ultra light EZB.
