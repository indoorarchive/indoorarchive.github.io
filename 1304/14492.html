I'll make this short and sweet, I was the unofficial-official provider of award plaques for the West Baden Contest. I volunteered to engrave the glass plaques and personalized them with the contestants name and engrave all of the event places to 3rd on the same plaque. If you only won one you get one plaque, win more than one, you get what I call a "composite" award with all wins on the same plaque. All record flights will receive a smaller plaque for the record. All plain and simple, too bad no one mentioned much about this offering!
 
I promised, so I will produce the awards, brought to you by "Artistry with Glass", part of SomeDay Enterprises LLC, Ron Patten, owner (CEO), graphic artist, glass artisan specializing in Flame Worked Glass, Kiln Worked Glass, and abrasive carved-engraved glass.
