> To all Southern California model aviation clubs, members and guests:
>
> The Orange County Parks Department is having its First Public Workshop on the Tustin Blimp Hangar and Regional Park, on Monday April 15, from 5 to 7:30 PM. This 85 acre facility is now in the planning stages, and this is your opportunity to guide that planning. Our goal is to allow use of the hangar for indoor model aircraft.
>
> The schedule will be facility tours both inside and outside of the North hangar from 5 to 6 PM. During this time Harbor Soaring Society will demonstrate various types of indoor model aircraft. At this time the Tustin Hangar documentary “Titans of History” will be projected on a large screen, and refreshments will be served. At 6 PM a PowerPoint presentation will be made by the Parks Department and the company contracted to perform preliminary design. At the conclusion of the presentation, the public is invited to give inputs for the features they prefer. We would like to have a massive turn-out of model enthusiasts expressing their support for both indoor and outdoor model aviation at this new regional park. Bring your family and friends for this opportunity to see the inside of this National Historic Building. Get there early if possible, to avoid traffic. Maps with directions are attached.
>
> This notice courtesy of the Tustin Blimp Hangar Action Committee, of Harbor Soaring Society.
