If you flew in the Nats...

Many of you have already sent me your feedback...thank you. Bud Layne,
NFFS Indoor Nats Chairman has formally asked me to solicit feedback on
the Arizona Nats. All of the feedback will be compiled and submitted to
Bud as he and his committee plan the 2020 Nats.

Feedback can be in any form you like and all of it will get to Bud, but
here are some ice breakers:
-How did you like the flying site?
-How did you like the event list and flying schedule?
-How did you like the town, restaurants, lunch service, etc?
-What specific suggestions for improvement can you offer?
-Would you return to Round Valley for another Nats?

Please submit your feedback by 6/20. Private message is preferred.

Thanks in Advance,
Don DeLoach
