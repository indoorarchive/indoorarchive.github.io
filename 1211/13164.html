Downloading and Reading the eDigest
In order to download the ...

NFFS eDigest you must be an active NFFS member. If you currently aren't a
member and would like to join or renew your membership go to
www.freeflight.org/Store
<http://www.facebook.com/l.php?u=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.freeflight.org%2FStore&h=D
AQEGDrh0&s=1> and using the links on the right side of the screen either
log in to your existing account or create a new account. If you have been a
member in the past you probably have an account. If you don't know, or
forgot, your username and password contact the webmaster at
info_at_freelight.org

You'll need Adobe Acrobat Reader installed on your computer. You can
download that here http://get.adobe.com/reader/ . Follow the directions for
the installation.

To download the eDigest go to
http://www.freeflight.org/shopping/newsletters.php
<http://www.facebook.com/l.php?u=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.freeflight.org%2Fshopping%
2Fnewsletters.php&h=MAQFU19Il&s=1>
This will bring up a screen for you to log in. Use your username and
password that you would use to access your account for the NFFS Online Shop.
If you don't have an account for the shop or forgot your username/password
contact the webmaster at info_at_freeflight.org.

After logging in you'll see a screen that lists the issues of the eDigest
currently available for download. Note that there are two versions of each
issue available. The one with the smaller file size, typically around 5MB,
is suitable for slower internet connections. The larger size file, between
40 and 60MB is suitable for faster internet connections. The larger file has
higher resolution graphics and is best for members who would like to print
the eDigest to read or print plans or photos.

To download the file you can either:
Click the link in the right column that says View/Download. This will
download the file and activate the Acrobat software to display the eDIgest
in your web browser. Note that in this method the controls to go from page
to page, to zoom in or out, to print, or to save the file to your computer
can be found at the bottom of the window by moving the cursor to the bottom
of the window to reveal them.

Or, Right click over the View/Download link and select "Save Link As." (in
Firefox) or "Save target as." (in Internet Explorer), or similar in other
browsers. This will open a dialog box prompting you to save the file in the
default location on your computer.
Go to the eDigest download page
