Hi,
    Anyone that is interested in the science Olympiad event- Elastic Launch Gliders, there is now a series of videos on how to build Stan Buddenbohm's SO Lit'Sweep kit on the AMAglider website (www.amaglider.com). These videos might also be useful to anyone new that is interested in building catapult gliders.
    Just after I made these videos I was told that the National Free Flight Society has a brand new DVD which shows the construction and flying of both a tip launched glider and a catapult glider. I have not seen the NFFS video but I am sure it is better than the one I produced, but I hope what is offered on the amaglider website will offer some information to the people that watch it.

Cheers,
