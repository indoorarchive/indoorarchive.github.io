Can I add my .0002$, my program (that I downloaded years ago) prints all
'foils with a 10" length. With the beauty of simplex, yes, you can cut
the trailing edge of to any length you want - it'll still be the correct
percentage.

I've made "warping" forms for several from 4% to 12%, usually use 2 or 3
lams of 1/32 wet, wiped with Elmers and clamped overnight to make
blanks, then strip width I want.
