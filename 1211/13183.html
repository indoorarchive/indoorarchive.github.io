TTOMA's monthly indoor contest for December will be held on Dec. 8 at the gym of St. Lukes Presbyterian Church in Dunwoody, GA (just north of Atlanta). The contest will run from 9am until 8pm or possibly a little later. We will be honored at this contest with the presence of the legendary Larry Coslick and the near legendary Kang Lee. It promises to be a great day of flying if any of you would like to join us.

If you're not already aware of St. Lukes it is one of the best Cat 1 sites imaginable. 4 AMA records have been set there as of this writing. One or two more might fall at the December contest.
