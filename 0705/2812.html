Sorry, sometimes we take abbreviations for granted. LPP is Limited
Pennyplane, an AMA event which some of us feel is the ideal "entry
level" class for competition indoor duration FF It's relatively
heavy and relatively easy to build at 3.1 gram minimum. (Experienced
modelers often enjoy LPP also.) IIRC, only Manhattan Cabin is
heavier, for the purely duration events, at 4 gram minimum.

To see or download plans for the classic "Banks LPP," click on our
Files Section, courtesty of Jeff Hood, or DL at Indoornews.com.
