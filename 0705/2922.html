Hi,
    If your thinking of doing outdoor DLG I highly
recommend you purchase a kit from Stan Buddenbohm.
His designs are excellent, kits are fantastic and I
assure you this is the best way to go. Information
for his kits are available at the website:
www.amaglider.vze.com
   Now there is also some plans posted on this website
for a nice DLG glider which is very similar to Stan's
Twirly Bird, if you want to go the hard way. This
model was designed and built before Stan started his
development on his DLG kits. So the design is a
little out dated, but still a decent design. There is
also a Spin up kit offered by String aeroproducts,
again a very good design(by Mark Benns) and very nice
kit.
   If you interested in outdoor DLG I also suggest you
check out the SFA forum. Lots of discussion about
outdoor DLG's on this forum.

Good Luck,

Kurt
