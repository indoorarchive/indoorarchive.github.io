Leroy,
One of the smaller drawbacks rubber lubes is that most are only available
via mail order. Should you schedule require rubber lube today instead of the
few days that mail order takes, go down to the local hobby shop and buy some
shock oil for a radio controlled car. This will not save you money, but
takes care of the schedule problem. The shock oil is 100% silicone oil. The
oils have an arbitrary rating from 5 to 100. 10 to 30 seems good to me.
