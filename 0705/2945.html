July 6, 2007 through July 10, 2007

Category IV site (145' height)

CONTEST DIRECTOR: Andrew Tagliafico; Phone: 1-503-452-0546 or:
andyruba_at_comcast.net for additional information.

Modest prizes will be awarded.

All AMA events, including 1.2 gram EZB and special events listed
below can be flown all days at East end of Dome during hours not set
aside for gliders.

F1D TEAM SELECTION FINAL will be held during the Kibbie Dome Annual
2007. F1D flying will be at the West end of the Dome during hours not
set aside for glider events.

FLYING SCALE CONTEST DIRECTOR: Dave Haught, Phone: 1-623-337-4827 for
additional information. Flying Aces Rules will be used. Static
judging prior to flying.

Times: July 6 - 9: 8:00 am to 8:00 pm
July 10: 8:00 am to 6:00 pm
Gliders: All days, 8:00 am to 9:30 am

SPECIAL EVENTS:
A-6, 35 cm, Science Olympiad, and 30 gram max. weight Electric Free
Flight (No R/C models allowed).

ENTRY FEES:
Junior and Senior - $50.00
Open - $125.00
Table and 2 chairs: $10.00. Helium: modest additional charge. There
are no additional event charges.

AMA membership required for USA fliers. Please confirm international
membership waivers. Confirm on-site AMA signup with Andrew prior to
event.

ACCOMMODATIONS: Special rates are available at the University Inn,
Moscow, Idaho, for participants, family and friends. For Reservations
call: 1-800-325-8765. Reference "Kibbie Dome Annual."

Parking: All participants with vehicles intending to park on campus
must obtain a visitor's parking pass and obtain information for which
lots are available.

Final notes:
This is a casual venue. Have fun! The timeframe of the Annual had to
be adjusted due to Dome events schedule conflicts.
