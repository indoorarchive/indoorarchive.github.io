The batches I tested are on my Cloudbusre web site, the list is only the batches I was able to obtain, I only bought rubber if the word was it was a good batch. These tests were made when I received the rubber and some batches such as 2-99 have actually improved with age. 2-99 was very soft and fragile when I tested it, I broke a lot of motors to get the data on it, I felt it was basically unusable back then but quite a few flyers are reporting good results with it these days. I mostly use 5-99 3-02 and 10-97each has its own qualities. Of course since 5-99 is now made of gold I only use it for F1D and record attempts.
