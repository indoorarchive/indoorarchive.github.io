Gentlemen,
F1 Diddler instructed me to intoduce myself, so here goes. I flew
control line back in the late 70's (stunt) and was always fascinated
by indoor models that sometimes appeared in Aeromodeller, MA and FM,
the mags I read at the time. I thought that these models were the
crown jewel of airplane modeling, I resolved as a 19 yr old to
someday build them, So at 41 yrs old I met Chralie Stiles a long
time modeller who took me aside at a hobby show when he saw i bought
some peck ROGs. That was two years ago. I have been making sporatic
progress having built some parlor mites of beformentioned Chralies
design, a Banks SO LE and a Banks PP out of Lew Gitlows book. Havn't
flown the PP at Lakehurst, which I consider an incredible place to be
period.
  
I have enjoyed great support from people like Steve Fujikawa, Ray
Harlen, Rob Romash, Tony Delassando, Joe Krush, Lew Gitlow etc. For
this year i hope to get some Hobby Shoppers complte and flying
resonably. Any comments on speeding up my progress would be
appreciated.
WEEEE enough about me.
A Happy and Peaceful New Year to all.
Fred Daddi
