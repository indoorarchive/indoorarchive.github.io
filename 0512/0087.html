"Glassine" envelopes made for stamp collecting make good bags for
motors. And they are "archival", i.e., acid free.

Google on "glassine stamp envelopes" and you'll see a host of sources.
The smallest are usually 1.75 X 2.875 or 2 X 2. But they are made in
dozens of sizes.
