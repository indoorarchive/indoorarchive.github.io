I get my 2"x3" Ziploc baggies at Wal-Mart. They are in the craft section and are about $.99 or $1.99 for 100. I use a fine point felt tipped pen to write on them. I used to use some coin envelopes that I had gotten at Office Depot but they seemed to be causing the rubber to deteriorate so I quit using them.
