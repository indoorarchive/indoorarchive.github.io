I have posted a picture of a 2010 Div C (trial event) helicopter in the SO album. This helicopter also meets the 2011 Div C rules. The helicopter flew 1:53 (2nd place) at the 2010 National competition in the University of Illinois Armory. Except for the Harlan rotor bearing (which can be made by a student, too) and the Tan Supersport rubber, all the wood and parts were purchased at the local hobby shops.
