Hopefully, to put this issue finally to bed, all Sci Oly events run at the 2010 Indoor Nats will be for straight duration times, only. There will be no bonus points awarded for reductions of wing chord.

Rules are 2009/2010 Division "B", which everyone should have in my "Rules & Such". But just in case, Leo P. has re-iterated them for us in the attachment. Please pass this on to any possible Sci Oly contestants not on this forum.

There will be an Open SO event on Saturday morning for unlimited rubber, IF there is sufficient interest. It is not on the Excel spreadsheet schedule. That is on purpose.

I will donate entry fees for those who cannot, or don't think they have to pay, but events are not free. AMA membership without the magazine is only $1 for Juniors, and the NFFS entry fee for one event is $10. I will have plenty of sign-up forms for both at the registration table.
