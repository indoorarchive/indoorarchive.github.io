I have a quick Dave Linstrum story. Back when the Cassutt Nocal was the
big thing. Dave built a red Cassutt per the plans my dad was selling and
he could not get it to fly. So one day there was a large envelope from
VTO Dave Linstrum. My dad opened it and inside was a flattened Cassutt
nocal! He folded it up and shipped the whole plane in an enevelope and
put a note in the envelope saying something along the line of "I can not
get this to fly, maybe you can figure it out. VTO" It was hilarious. We
came real close to rebuilding it and were going to take it to USIC and
give it back to him.
