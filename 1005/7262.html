F1N-150 is a rubber catapulted variant of small F1N glider with wingspan
not exceeding 150 mm. Other propositions are the same as for F1N. This class
of gliders are here very popular as by juniors as seniors, although is not in
FAI rules.

Height of hall 3 is about 22.5 meter, or Cat. 3 to FAI, and the ceiling is cupola.
