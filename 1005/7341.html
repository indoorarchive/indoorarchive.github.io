Since we need to provide the Am's with motors, perhaps we should post pictures of those, too. To start the ball rolling, I have posted a picture of one of my LPP ProAm motors.

Unlike John, I do not have a stash of 5/99 Tan II rubber but I see that Jim Richmond flew very well using using Pirelli rubber. I found some ("Pirelli" is molded right into the rubber strip) and made a motor for the ProAm event. Kang tells me that my LPP motors are not big enough so I made this one 27" x 1.75" with a weight of 140 grams. The LPP may be hard to trim with this motor but it should climb like a rocket! I hurt my wrist on my first wind but I should be okay for the ProAm event next weekend.
