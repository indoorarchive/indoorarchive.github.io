Monday wasn't a good day at the hangar. Lots of drift and the air was just so-so. The sun didn't came out in full to heat things up. (At least this is my story ...)

The highlight of the day for me was to successfully steer my EZB very high up. John Kagan gave me a needed tip -- face the direction that the model is traveling. Doing this gave me a great perspective of where the model is and where the balloon line is, which made it much easier to catch the model.

Thanks goes to Alan Mkitarian who came out to CD and time. He missed a 4th of July party on the riverfront to help us. Alan, we owe you one.

-Kang
