Hi Ken, I am a relative new comer to duration events, but I weighed every piece and rebuilt anything that was not to my specs. That is not to say that I have a great A-6. It is okay for my first and only one. It placed 3rd in the postal. The motor stick is too soft due to the fact that I do not have a good inventory of wood. I have to compensate as I fly it since the motor stick bends too much so I have to watch my launch torque very closely to keep the model from flying down into the floor at launch. My best time last season was 7:14 in our category II site. I'm not sure what kind of condenser tissue I used. It was the lightest I had on hand. I wish I had better notes of various components- I just kept rebuilding things until I got to 1.2 gr. I'm counting on you guys with more experience to help me through the rough spots. James
