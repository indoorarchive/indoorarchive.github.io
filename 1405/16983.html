A 25% reduction in flight time means roughly a 33.3% increase in the capacity of a site. Roughly speaking, this means that a given site can accommodate 33% more F1D contestants: 20 instead of 15, 16 instead of 12, 12 instead of 9, 8 instead of 6, etc.

 These are the estimates for a Cat I or Cat II site. These are the sites may be used more in the future for local, regional, national, and international contests.
