Don and All,

The ETSU Mini-Dome is getting a new (overlay) roof this summer, I think.
The large scoreboard is still there as well as basketball lights and
banners at roughly 70-80 feet. Men's basketball will be played elsewhere
in Johnson City in the fall. What is still unknown to me, and possibly
in general, is whether the scoreboard and lights will be moved or removed.

The banners are likely to change since ETSU is resuming football
starting 3 Sept 2015 and switching conferences to one that plays
football. Football will be played outside, but half the Mini-Dome floor
will have the artificial turf replaced for practice. I speculate and
have said that if punting and kick-offs are to be practiced inside, then
all the flying obstructions such as scoreboard, basketball lights, and
banners will need to be moved.

Who is now the key contact with AMA for Indoor FF Nats planning? Whom
should I try to keep informed as far as possible?

I do have high hopes of returning the AMA Indoor FF Nats to Johnson
City. Also remember that the FAI Indoor World Championships were held
there in 1988 and 1990.

Fred Rash
