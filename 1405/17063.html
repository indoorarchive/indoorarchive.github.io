Last year was my only trip to Kibbie so I have no idea what was done in
other years. Last year flying stopped fairly early in the afternoon and
there was an awards meeting for everyone still there and able to walk.

when it happened. That's why I would like to know in advance what the
plan is. Also there is the matter of where to stay the night of the 30th.

reason to have a schedule pre-arranged. One of my plaques from USIC 2013
took almost a year to get to me when it got left out of the AMA's
package that was brought to the contest.

Kang is always the last person flying and the last one out of the
building. Always.
