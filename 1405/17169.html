Walt VanGorder contacted me today and wanted me to feel out the international F1D community to see if people would come to the US for a world champs at West Baden in the future, which would be 2018 at the earliest. The owners of West Baden want to have the WC there. Walt wants to make sure that there is enough interest from overseas to have a well attended contest prior to looking into the details. I created a poll on this list, so if you are an international F1D flyer please vote.

I also realize that everyone who attended the last WC might not be on this list, such as the Czechs, Germans, Polish, Japanese and others, and that I might have to reach out to those people individually. But for now if you are an international F1D flyer, please vote or reply here as to the desire of you and your countrymen to attend a WC at West Baden.

Thanks,
Brett Sanborn
