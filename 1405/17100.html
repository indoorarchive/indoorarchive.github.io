There are 2 more votes - the Interim and the Final. This is the schedule:

Cross proposals can be submitted by any AMA member between May 31 and
July 30. The interim (semi-final) voting will be done between Aug 1 and
Aug 15. If a cross proposal is accepted it replaces the original proposal.

Final voting will be done between Aug 30 and Sept. 15. I guess this is
where you say if you really meant your first two votes.

You can (and should) lobby ALL of the board members about any rules
proposal that you have an opinion about. Some of them are near
impossible to contact except by snail mail. Hopefully that will change
someday.

In case anyone hasn't heard Kang has taken Clarence Mather's place on
the board. I believe that Stan Chilton is leaving the board soon and
possibly Gary Hodson will take his place. If this is the case I think
Gary will be an excellent board member in spite of some differences of
opinions that we have.

Bill Gowen
