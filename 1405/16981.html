Here are my best guess estimates for the new F1D flight times at various active sites. The estimated reduction in flight time varies between 22% to 30%. There will be more reduction at higher sites.

 Projected flight time for each site estimated in the following way:
 1. estimating a best 0.6 g flight time at the same site with 50% more height.
 2. Taking 2/3 (66.7%) of this estimated flight time
