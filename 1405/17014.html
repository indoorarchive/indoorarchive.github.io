 Limited Supply Offer from "Average Kits, LLC!"
 

 I have 3 F1d kits for sale, for $495, straight from my model box. Customer's choice, either "kit form" or fully assembled. Kit form means I remove the existing covering and include plenty of sheet Y2K (2004 vintage) and also dissolve rib to spar joints--it will look like a kit at that point. The AMA rules do specify that more than a few minutes "covering effort" should be exerted by the builder. To my knowledge, no F1d kits exists, so we perhaps establish what "average kit" conceivably does mean. A fully assembled F1d, however could be useful for learning to fly with little time to build, for copying your own assemblies, or just to play with and look at. You can mix and match kit vs. assembled.
 

 Features:
