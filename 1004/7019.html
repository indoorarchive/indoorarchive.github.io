I show the students how to make a "swedge ?" sizer. We drill a series
of holes from about 3/32" to 1/16" in a small steel plate, that leaves a
burr on one side. Then we just push the posts thru the holes brom the
burr side going down is size until the 1/16" (last). Easy for beginners
to control and they don't end up with odd sizes.
