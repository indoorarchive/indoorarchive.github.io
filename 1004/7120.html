I've got a really crude bearing on my torque meters and have never had a
problem. I think the key is that you need a loose fit on the bearing. The
needles on my meters vibrate while I'm winding. I take this to be a sign that
the bearing isn't interfering with the reading. I also use long torque wires.
I'm not sure what difference this makes but it seems to work well.
