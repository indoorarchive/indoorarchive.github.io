I want to make sure the Pro-Am is a worthwhile experience for the Ams, and a major part of that is making sure there are enough Pros to go around.

DeLoach has suggested various forms of advertising, and Kang has gone one better and offered to spread the word to the SO lists on which he is active.

My current inclination is to accept the Ams that are trickling in from the existing advertising, but put the kabosh on more aggressive recruiting - for this round anyway. I don't want the event collapse under the weight of its own success. I'm happy to say that it looks like it could easily head that way.

We have 19 Ams so far, and are about 5 Pros short. Sanborn is the lastest addition. Who else wants to join the fun?
