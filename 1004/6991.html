My latest LPP wing has about 10 mg of adhesive for the covering using Mark's method but with a less diluted mixture (because I was getting impatient). You should be able to do considerably better than 10 mg on your F1L wing.

I consider Duco to be nearly weightless so I don't usually even bother to figure out glue weights for frames. If you are using Duco or Ambroid for your outlines I think you can use a lot less than 10 mg there.
