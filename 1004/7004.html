There is one totally foolproof and easy way to make paper tubes. Sadly I
spent many years spoiling a fair proportion of potential tubes before

the penny dropped. The secret is to roll the tube leaving ½ inch of tissue
extending beyond the end of the mandrill. No matter how much cement

Is in contact between tissue and the surface of the mandrill the perfect
tube is simply pulled off the mandrill.. Surely I cannot be the only indoor

modeller to do this?
