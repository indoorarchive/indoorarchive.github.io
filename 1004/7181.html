I was a member of Doc Martin’s
MIAMA club in the 1990’s, and flew in the Tampa Armory a few times before 9-11
shut all of that down. I am scanning all of the issues if Doc’s Hangar Pilot
into Adobe Acrobat format, to put on one or two archive discs. I have copies of most all of the Hangar Pilot
issues from Issue 62 to the 215, thanks to J P Kish and Jim Miller, but I don’t have anything to scan before issue
62.
 
If you have any of these early
copies, would you be able to send them to me? Could you refer me to someone who
could? I would return them in few weeks, along with your postage cost, and you
would get a free Hangar Pilot Archive disc when I am done, probably by the Indoor
Champs in a month’s time.
 
As a character reference, I was
editor of Indoor News and Views from 2001 until 2005. I am Event Director for
the 2010 US Indoor Championships in Johnson City, TN, and anyone who has gone
there can vouch for me. Or you can just Google my name anytime.
 
Thanks for your time.
