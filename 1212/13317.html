I'm trying to work out a geometry calculator where you can enter in six, maybe seven measurements on your VP mechanism and it will tell you what your yoke angle and prop rotation angles would be.

The basic geometry is pretty simple - just solving for isosceles triangle angles. Solving the required spring constant needs the angular travel of the yoke between the high pitch and low pitch screws.
