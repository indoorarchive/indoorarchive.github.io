On 11.12.2012 22:50, mkirda_at_sbcglobal.net wrote:
> Can you estimate the angular difference between low and high pitch?
> Maybe 20 degrees?

Bottom pitch at 150mm radius 24 degrees = 420mm
Top pitch 42 degrees = 850mm
Angular difference in blades 18 degrees.

Yoke diameter 5mm, actuating arm length 4.5mm -> change in the spring
angle 5/4.5 * 18 degrees = 20 degrees (bingo!) Prop pitch starts to
reduce at approximately 8 to 9 g*cm.
