I’m glad to hear the Midwest props have improved. A few years ago I had to reject an order of Midwest Delta Darts due to a batch of really poor props, all molded in a red plastic. Most had misshapen blades, a few so bad they were practically flat. It was really odd. As mentioned, the prop shafts on all seems to be oversize, and made of a very soft metal. But all in all, when properly made, they’re a great copy of the North Pacific prop.
