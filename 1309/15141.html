I hope that this is self-explanatory.

You have three numbers to enter.
Low pitch = The Helical pitch form you used to make the blade.
Rotate degrees = Number of degrees off of formed pitch to the new pitch.
High Pitch = Comparison pitch.

I.E. You form the blades at 22"P and rotate them 6 degrees higher and want to compare to 28" pitch.
Low = 22
Rotate = 6
High = 28

Delta tells you the number of degrees off you are from the High Pitch either positively or negatively at each Inch station (Distance from prop shaft).
