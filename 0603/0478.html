A single strand won't be enough for stick bracing unless the stick is very
strong in which case it may not need bracing at all - OK for wings where the
loads are much lower. Give me tungsten any day; I use 1 strand of 1 thou and
bend the ends over eg the nose and glue down otherwise it will slip.
