Check out http://www.indoorduration.com/ There is a section on INAV,
which is the indoor newsletter "Indoor News And Views". Many of the
recent issues are on-line and you can get a CD of the first 100 issues
or so. Publication is ongoing although irregular.

INAV has had articles on all sorts of indoor stuff including contest
reports, testing on rubber, as well as plans and advice on building
and flying models.

While you are there check out the articles section for the "Hobby
Shopper" EZB and an update on it. It will give you great insight into
building an EZB.
