Mark, Bill and others,

Using the cheapest paint drop cloths from Menard's (or other home
improvement stores) has worked well for me. The one in front of me as I
write this is Frost King Clear Plastic .25 mil Drop Cloth.

I have attached it with thinned RC-56 canopy glue, but I think gluestick
will also work. I suggest wrapping 90 or 180 degrees for a good bond.

My experience with Saran was similar to that of Jerry Combs. It is
pretty, and might be good on heavier airframes.
