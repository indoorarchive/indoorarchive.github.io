There is a contest in Flint, Michigan on May 7, the Cloudbusters from Detroit host it. It's a good contest, we fly in a golf dome that's about 80' tall. I don't think the flyer is out yet though. We fly mostly Limited Pennyplane, ezb, and ministick at this contest. I believe they also have a competition for the current rules for SO, however they only allow kids to fly it. You should definitely come check it out.

Brett Sanborn
