I use Kevlar thread usually used for fly tying as motor stick bracing
for F1M. It is easier to see, easier to handle, and stronger than wire
which is perfect for my fumble fingers and poor eyesight. I tie it off
and also use a bit of glue on the end, sort of like belt and
suspenders.
