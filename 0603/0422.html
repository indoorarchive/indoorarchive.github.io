What is your target weight.? Ultra film and Polymicro both work well on
ornithopters (I have used both) but the weight is key. If you are
making a 3 gram model you will need the Ultrafilm strength as our flap
rate is higher and more forceful. If you are making a 1,5 gram or less,
then use Polymicro,
