It is extremely easy to make an overweight A-6. I find it easier to make an
overweight A6 than an overweight PP, or F1M. If the intent was to make an
event where a beginner could make something out of hobby shop wood, then it
is a failure. 1.2 grams is just too little.

As far as folks who invest lots of time and effort with an event, they are
the ones that do well anyway. Most of these folks like the challenge and if
they have the time try new things. Look at the record attempts. You see the
same names moving around to new areas.
