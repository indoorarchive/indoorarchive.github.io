I would like to tell you that we are going to organize the International
Indoor Fly In this year for the second time. Last year it was a great
success. The flying site is 2400 sq. meters big and just under 8 meters.
Also the flying conditions are superb with a flat ceiling.
Some British members of this group were also present last year, but it would
be great to meet more of you guys.
The contest will be flown 28/29 of March 2009.
Klasses which will be flown are most duration (F1D,M,L) and most scale
(Open-scale, peanut, Pistachio) and much more.
For more info I would like to recommend the website (under construction)
www.iifi.nl
