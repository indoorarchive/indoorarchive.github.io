I would recommend the A2Z stripper. I ultimately replaced the base on my
Harlan stripper and added a self healing mat to the top. I think the A2Z
blade holder is better (I've used both). The micrometers on the Harlan
stripper are nice and accurate, but the 1" width limitation is a problem at
times. Others would need to comment on the micrometers in the A2Z setup
because I've never used them, but I assume they're good and the 3" cutting
range is nice.
