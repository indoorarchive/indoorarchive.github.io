Hi Guys,
I made up a tissue printing master for our Phantom Flash contests (Skull and Crossbones required!).


Printing instructions:
1. Tape a piece of tissue to the top edge of a blank piece of paper. Run through the copier using the manual feed (don't put it in the paper tray!

2. Book a flight to the Tustin blimp hangar and fly in our contest!

Also attached is a prop block that works well with our PF's.

Merry Christmas everyone!
