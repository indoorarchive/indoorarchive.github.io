Hi all
I've dropped some pics in an album (Australian Team Trial 2011) in Photos of our Team Trial for the 2012 F1D World Champs. The organisers will recommend the top three finishers as team members to go to Belgrade. The morning started in good conditions, but deteriorated after midday as the wind outside increased turbulence indoors... Best two flights at the Cat 1 venue for top three were:

Tim Hayward-Brown 30:12 (18:44 + 11:28)
Max Newcombe 23:16 (12:23 + 10:53)
Jim Fullarton 17:53 (9:09 + 8:44)

F1L was won by Sean O'Connor who also came 4th in F1D.
There's a longer report on HPA for those who follow that (http://www.hippocketaeronautics.com/hpa_forum/index.php?topic=5461.msg71517#msg71517)
cheers
Tim
