I'm using the bandsaw fixture that was developed and built by Steve
Fujikawa and John Zseleczky in INAV 114. The blocks are cut from 2x2x11
balsa with a center chord width of ~2.75". I'm selling them as cut so
they'll have some saw marks that can be removed with very fine sandpaper.
 I can cut any pitch from 18" and up. I'll be asking for $20 shipped in
the lower 48 with $5 from each block sold going to the junior fund.
 Shipping outside the US will be at cost.

I've ordered some jointed and planed blocks to ensure accuracy. I won't
have that material until around Christmas so I'm planning to start selling
blocks in January. I've only ordered enough material to make 50 blocks
initially until I can determine the interest level. I'll post something
more official along with pictures when they're available for purchase.

Jake
