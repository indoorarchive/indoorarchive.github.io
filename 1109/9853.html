F1D Japan Nats was held at Yamabiko Dome in Matsumoto, Nagano on 25 Sep. It
was 6 round - 1 day contest. It was rain outdoor.
The dome is at 650 meters sea level, so the air is thin and 34.8 meters high
ceiling by FAI method but flyable max height will be about 31m and safety
height may be lower than 30m.
7 flyers entered and the results were:
1, Danjo 31:51+31:32= 63:23
2, Kihara 61:28
3, Enomoto 59:06
The same placing as '09 and '10.
Good flying, Aki, next is F2B Japan Nats in Oct
