After six rounds of competition the standings are unchanged.

The conditions were much worse today with an inconsistent and fast drift.
Brett and John were both steering frequently and at one point Brett had his
prop tuck under the wing because of wind terminating a potential record
flight.

Max, Tom, and Mark fought to get high times in the tough conditions.
Tomorrow will be a close run for the third spot on the team.

The junior team had some great flights today with tommy posting two good
times and parker breaking 26 minutes.

More to come tomorrow.
