Gentlemen,

For you nostalgia buffs I have listed my early 1960's (when I bought it) 16:1 Wilson winder on eBay. It's #260863264846.

There was a time when the Wilson was the only dedicated winder available for small to medium-size free flight rubber models, both indoor and outdoor. The early single channel rudder-only RC guys used it to wind the rubber loop that powered their escapement. The winder is all metal, including the gears, and was made by the Wilson Company of Cleveland. It's old, but in excellent shape. I'm sure it brings back memories for lots of you. Check it out!
