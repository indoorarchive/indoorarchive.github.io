A repeat of last years 2 day event in Sarasota.
Cat 1 site gym. Good floor space, I don't have the dims, but girders at
the ceiling.
FAC no-cal, p-nut, bostonian.
10 bucks entry fee
If you,re in the Tampa Bay area it's been a long while since the days
of the MIAMA Clubs great meets.
This event is sponsored by the Florida County Park and recreation Dept.
The gym is located via I-75, exit 213 . Drive right onto University
Parkway,stay of left lane for 4 1/2 miles, cross RR tracks At major
intersection,RT 301 turn left and stay in right lane, pass the fourth
traffic light(Myrtle ave.) and at the next intersecion, at 34th street
turn right and follow the street to the recreation center.
Flight times are 8 a.m. to 5 p.m..
Rubber soled shoes requested when on the wood floors.
