I have in the past couple of days, created a MSN Group to unite all who work with Kids and Free Flight activities, primarily the old balsa stick and tissue methods. Making planes out of kits or by scratch is fast becoming a lost art. So, if you work with kids in this activity, whether in school's, church's, Boy Scouts, or just a parent helping their child get into this hobby this might be just the place for you. If you wish, we can create a page just for you, that tells about you and your kids. Hopefully, we can learn from each other. It is also intended for the Kids to show off their planes and maybe even have a few internet contests just for them. The sky is the limit as to the possiblities!
