writes:



You want the stiffest pole you can get. A too-flexible steering pole is
pretty dangerous when it contacts a very fragile model like an EZB or
F1D. I have seen guys slice right through LEs with a whippy steering
pole.
 
I suggest getting a pole that is a couple feet longer than you need to
reach the ceiling comfortably, then removing the tip section.
