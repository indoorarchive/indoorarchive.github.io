Jim

It has standard eight-lane 400M track around a full-size football field, so something like 200 ft by 450 ft. Seats on both sides to the roof with no endzone seating.



The ceiling height has been quoted as anywhere from 116 to 122 ft. It is a pretty dirty ceiling with exposed steel trusses (pretty easy to hang up).



The site was Awesome until 2005 when a scoreboard was suspected from the 50 yard line, just off of center. VERY easy to hang up and it hangs down to only 50 ft above the floor.



All things considered USIC is a great meet in a nice little town. You should go!



--Don
