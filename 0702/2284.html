Good morning, Marty, I print the plan and decoration on the Dennydart
tissue, have wrinkled first but no matter how I iron the tissue is
"crinkly" and doesn't print as well. So, I iron the sheets to take our
creases, print, wrinkle and re iron.

To attach, I just rub a light coat of gluestick (bought at Dollartree)
and put the sticks right down on the paper - the MarkII is built
directly on the tissue. On the mark IV (airfoiled), i build the wing,
rub a light coat on the sticks and put the tissue down. I'd be afraid
the ink would smear if using liquid on it.

wombat - printed probably about 300, taint hard.
