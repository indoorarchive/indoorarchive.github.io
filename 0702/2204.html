Have you looked for info on the student center page? sounds like your
torque isn't high enough on launch and you have cruising torque for
early flight. also try adjusting your stab, slightly. in a negative
angle. take some of the lift off the wing and actually cause the plane
to point itself toward the ceiling. Change the stab a little at a time,
you want a slight climb and then the plane needs to cruise and then
come down with the wing and stab level. I may be wrong on someof this
but that has been working for us.
