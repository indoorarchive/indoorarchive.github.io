I finished a V-tail A-6. I started with Jerry Coombs wing (from INAV)
and the stab outline but with V-dihedral rather than tip dihedral.
Flight across the living room looks pretty good. The model is small
enough that packing should be easy. I added a tissue tube on the end
of the motorstick to allow removal of the tail boom.

I'll have to pick up some methiolate or mercurichrome for the red
coloring.

I'll report on things after the weekend, I hope to make it out to MIT
this Saturday.
