Our Rochester SO regional competition was held at a 22' high school gym two
weeks ago. Don Steeb and I were the events directors. The winning flight was
made by the team from Greece Arcadia High School (Greece is a suburb just
north and a bit west of Rochester). Their model was an original design by
Don, who had earlier mentored the team. Unlike most of the film-covered
models entered, this model used red Japanese tissue for covering and still
needed a bit of ballast to bring it up to weight. The winning flight was a
no-touch 2:38. The two next best teams were back around 1:48 or so.
