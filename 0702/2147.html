Hello BEAMS Members, Boeing Employees, Northwest Modelers, Students,
Educators and Friends,
 
BEAMS (Boeing Employees' Aerodynamic Modeling Society) is pleased to
announce our next big model airplane event, the 13th Annual "A Day in
the Hangar" to be held inside the Boeing Factory in Everett,
Washington, Saturday, March 31th, between 7:00a.m. and 6:00p.m.
Registration deadline is March 17th.
 
We are delighted that Boeing Manufacturing and Engineering has made
space for this event, in light of the busy production schedule in the
Everett Hangar. To fly indoor models in such a huge open space (10
million cubic feet) is a real treat.
 
We have made some changes to this annual event to create a more "fun
and family" atmosphere. For example, there will be four pre-event
workshops to help participants prepare their models. Complete
Registration forms and details are posted on the BEAMS website:
www.beams.ws
 
So, we invite you to be part of this traditional opportunity to
safely witness the joy of aerodynamics, inside the largest airplane
hangar in the world.
    Informal "indoor flying" for novice and experience modelers.
This year we are not having any contests...just come and enjoy the
space.
    Youth and adults flying indoor model airplanes together and
learning about aerodynamic principles.
    Flight and building demonstrations of indoor rubber powered
models and gliders
    Inspiring our next generation of modelers and future aviation
professionals
    Models will be available to be flown by novices with experienced
modelers
Please share this information with others. Hope to see you there...!
