This will be a small trial contest at the newly accessible dirigible hangar
in Tillamook Oregon. Unfortunately I need to limit entries to 8 people for
this first contest, but I'm already having discussions for a larger contest
next year. Also due to limited floor space and relatively short flying
hours (10am-5pm), this will be F1D only. Future contests will include more
events.

Entry is $5, and I'm also asking everyone attending to purchase a museum
pass to help support them. Daily passes are $10.50. Helium will be
available for a fee. Tables and chairs are not available on site, but I'll
find a solution if you're unable to bring your own.

Contest dates are 8/17-8/18. For more details and to reserve a spot, please
contact me directly at 82 dot jake at gmail dot com.
