Dear friends, We are happy to invite you to Slanic, Unirea mine for OttoHints Memorial 8-th edition November 21-23 2019
The schedule is as follows: - flying period every day 9:00 to15:00; we have to leave themine at 15:30- each competitor has to fly 3 official flights every day (no established rounds)- timing will be performed by other competitors- organizers will provide Contest Director and technicalteam for measurements of models and rubber- test flights allowed but not in the official flying area- tables and chairs provided by organizers Entry fee for the contest will be 35 euro/ competitor, andwill cover: -         trophies-         organizing costs-         one fill of helium/ competitor (refill will cost 10euro)-         tea and coffee in the mine It’s forbidden to enter with private cars in the mine. Everybodyhas to take the minibuses (first departure 8:00 from the elevator area).Minibus cost supported by competitor.
 Registration on November 21 at 18:00 in Hotel Roberto. Aurel Popa & Marian Popescu
