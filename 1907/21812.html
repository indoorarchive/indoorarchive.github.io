The City of Rantoul has decided to lease out Hanger #3 which will make it unavailable to us for the foreseeable future. We will be cancelling the events scheduled there for this fall and are returning the unused portion of the money that sponsors donated to the cause. We apologize for this inconvenience, but be assured we are already exploring other flying site opportunities in the Midwest.
