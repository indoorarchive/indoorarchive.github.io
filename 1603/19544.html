Regarding the 5 1/2 inch plastic props. All made today are way to heavy and most rules prohibit scraping etc. I have some old skeeter units i Got from jack Mcgilvray estate and are half the weight of todays midwest red props. This is important for clases like delta dart blatter 40 etc. They are also flexible plastic and do not brake and have soft shafts but that can be replaced with 1/32 piano wire. One thing about miswest props the pitch distribution looks good my light props i am mentioning look quite warped. May do 3d scan to sheck actual pitch distribution one day if get back to these clases. Meantime may be willing to part with a few few of
