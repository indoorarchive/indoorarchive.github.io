The elite indoorists of the USA gathered at the world famous West Baden Springs Hotel for a weekend of flying, great food and friendly, cutthroat competition. On Saturday the air was so turbulent that only one official flight was recorded. Sunday was much better with flyable air early in the morning and improving conditions throughout the day right up until the 5:00pm quittiing time. The following scores were recorded:
