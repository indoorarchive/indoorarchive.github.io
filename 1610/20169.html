It seems that that the FAI ID number for at least the USA senior competitors at the 2016 Indoor WC (as listed on the final results) does not match the number on their FAI Sporting License. The FAI ID number listed in the results must be assigned some other way. Mea Culpa. This makes the proposal problematic in that the numbers cannot be written on the motor stick in advance of the event, at least for new entrants. Maybe the proposal should be changed (as should the FAI ID) to match the Country designation plus the country membership number.

 Also, the Jury Report for the 2016 Indoor WC has been posted on the F1D blog:
 

 thef1dblog.wordpress.com

 

 The request for the ID marking rule change comes in the remarks section of that report.
