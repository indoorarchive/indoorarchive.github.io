required/sufficient for flying in the salt mine... There is a number of
relatively cheap lamps using Cree LM T6 LED lamp, these give out up to
1000 lumens, and in a comparison to car headlights, the light spot is
about as bright as one from H4 car light, but the beam is narrower (
e.g.
http://dx.com/p/ultrafire-sh-3aa-cree-xm-l-t6-800lm-5-mode-memory-white-led-flashlight-w-strap-3-x-aa-1-x-26650-124061
