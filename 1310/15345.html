   The formulas I use:
 
 
 max turns = motor_length * 45.67*SQRT (motor_length / motor_weight )
 
 
 
 max torque =( motor_weight / motor_length / 0.11)^1.5

 For my 7/99, the max turns is very accurate. For my 5/99, I can get a 7% more turns.
