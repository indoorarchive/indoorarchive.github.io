Dear Indoor Constructors,

This you may find interesting:

CCT recently launched a new line of composite materials: ThinSkin,
pre-cured ultra thin carbon skins.
The main use of this material can be found in making full composite wings,
but maybe someone also find this interesting in indoor construction.
Furthermore they are also able to offer the material in prepreg form (so
not cured). Both IM and HM materials are available (as thin as 18 gsm or
less than 10 mu).


If interested, take a look at their website:
www.creativecompositetechnologies.com
or Facebook:
https://www.facebook.com/CreativeCompositeTechnologies
