Indoor Nats registration is finally live! Visit
http://nats.modelaircraft.org/register/

Some notes:
1. The $50 basic registration fee is for AMA official events only. If
you pay the $50 once for Indoor, you don't have to pay it again for Outdoor.
2. If you are only flying unofficial events, you need not pay the $50 fee.
3. Standard Catapult Mass Launch is an unofficial event, $5 entry fee,
but is not listed on the paper form or the dropdown menu. Not sure why.
Send Yolanda an email and let her know you'd like to enter it.
4. As long as you pre-enter by April 30 you may add events without
incurring a late fee. Example: show up to the Nats with a model for
event you hadn't expected to fly, pay $5 for that additional event on
the spot and you will be given a time card.

I'm very sorry for the delay on this. It has been a two-month
back-and-forth with AMA to get this correct. It's now correct except for
the exclusion of that one mass launch. Hopefully AMA will get that
corrected soon.

~DD

P.S. Very important...all contestants will be required to consent to the
following Code of Conduct before receiving time cards at the Nats.
Failure to acknowledge and accept the terms of this Code will be grounds
for disqualification. If these terms are unacceptable to you please
don't bother entering the Nats.

2019 Indoor Nats Code of Conduct

1. Contestants shall not direct any complaints complaints or questions
to Round Valley staff, including but not limited to hung up models, open
doors or windows, lighting etc. The Nats Event Director (Tom Gaylor) and
the Indoor Nats Manager (Don DeLoach) are the only individuals who shall
talk about our flying activities with the Round Valley staff.

2. Contestants will treat each other with respect at all times.
Unsportsmanlike conduct by contestants or by guests of a contestant will
not be tolerated and at the discretion of the Event Director or Nats
Manager will be subject to disciplinary actions as outlined per AMA
General Rules Section 17, "Disqualification/Exclusion". Such infractions
will be documented and included in the Event Director's Report to the
AMA following the conclusion of the Nats.
