Indoor Nats in Springerville, Arizona, May 29-June 2.

The pre-entry deadline has been extended to May 20th. This gives you
plenty of time to get your entry in. You can do it online or the pdf
forms are attached for printing and mailing. The CDs greatly appreciate
you pre-entering--it saves a lot of work for us. And, remember, as long
as you pre-enter you can add events on site for $5 each.

Also attached is the information on the Italiano Cup and the District
Cash award. First place district earns $1500, second $1000, third $500.
All flyers within a district fly as a team. Team entry is automatic and
FREE.

Hotels: Rode Inn, El Jo, Best Western have special discounts. A couple
of flyers stayed at Reed's Lodge recently and like it a lot.

--Don DeLoach
for the Nats Committee
