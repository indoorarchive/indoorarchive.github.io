(Re-sending this as I had a typo in the results. Attached files are
revised)

Many thanks to all who came to the Kent contest this past weekend. It
was one of the best turnouts in the past 5 years. F1D was the most
popular event with 10 entrants (twice as many as LPP). As usual the air
inside was good despite the wind and rain outside. We had seven new AMA
records set!

*New AMA Cat II Records:*
F1D (Adult): Kang Lee 25:11
F1D (Youth Senior): Spencer Tyson 14:06
F1R (Adult): Nick Ray 30:39
A6 (Adult): Don Slusarczyk 8:17
Unlimited Cat Glider (Adult): Kurt Krempetz: 134.3 seconds
Std Cat Glider (Youth Senior) : 95.2 seconds
Unlimited Cat Glider (Youth Senior) : 95.2 seconds

I have attached the results in PDF and jpg format to this email.

I hope everyone had a great time. Many thanks to all who were able to
spend their Easter weekend flying model airplanes. Looking forward to
the 2020 contest!

Thanks! 
-- 
Don Slusarczyk
