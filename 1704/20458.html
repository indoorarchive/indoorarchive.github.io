Dear All,

I am trying to create an archive of all current AMA Indoor National records. The intent being if a person wants to see the current record design for any age class or event for any ceiling height it will be available to view online. As of today I count 219 existing records.

The AMA has all of these drawings but they want me to pay $5 each so $5 x 219 = $1095 to make the copies. So instead I am proposing to the indoor community that if you have a record (no matter how old) that is currently on this list as of today: http://www.modelaircraft.org/files/NationalRecords.pdf http://www.modelaircraft.org/files/NationalRecords.pdf

And you are willing to share your 3 View drawing publicly please email me at don_at_slusarczyk.com with a link to your 3 view, or a PDF of your 3 view, or even mail me a photocopy and I will scan in it. I have discussed this with Nick Ray and we would like to make this available on the Indoor News and Views website. I still have some of my old 3 views I had from when I was a Junior, hopefully some of you guys have your old records as well. I want to get as many as possible. I think it would be a great resource for the indoor community. As new records are set I will be updating them as I approve them. The hard task now is to populate all the existing records so that is why I am looking for help. I had hoped the AMA would have been more on board with this as in reality it is maybe a couple hours of work to copy all the drawings, and I think $1095 is an excessive amount to have that done.

Thanks,
Don Slusarczyk
