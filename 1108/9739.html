I have been using one for many years now. Once I threw out what appeared to be some redundant items, it has performed very well. Good tests were the cold conditions in Slanic and hot conditions in Belgrade. I have increased accuracy for very small weights with use of a 10:1 extension arm which is in flatpack form for transport ( no, it didn't come from IKEA).
