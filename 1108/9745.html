In order to use Stan's hook system here's what I learned from Fred Terzian.
 Use a 1/2 in dia or larger dowel for a stick where you can drill a 1/4 in
hole vertical from the top and 1/4 in hole from the side.The rubber loop now
comes out from the top of the stick.
