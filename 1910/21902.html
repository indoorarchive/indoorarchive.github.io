I agree with Lincoln's notion of moving Indoor_Construction to
GROUPS.IO. We moved the RC Soaring Digest Yahoo! Group to GROUPS.IO a
couple of years ago. It's very similar to the Yahoo! Groups format,
free, and there have been no hiccups. GROUPS.IO moved everything -
posts, attachments, files, etc. - from Yahoo! to GROUPS.IO (which took a
few days) and then sent out invitations to all of our nearly 3,000 group
members. The transition went very smoothly and it's worked extremely
well for us. If Yahoo! has been fulfilling your needs and you're simply
looking for a similar free service, check out GROUPS.IO.
