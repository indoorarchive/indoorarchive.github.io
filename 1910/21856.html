About 6 months ago I purchased a fairly rare 10:1 Wilder winder from Jon
Putnam who was a WMC member that we recently lost to cancer. I intended to
use it in place of the 20:1 winder I've owned for more than 20 years. Now
that Geauga winders have become available again, I decided to order one.
So now it's time to pass this 10:1 Wilder on to someone else.

One odd quirk to note about this winder is the counter. Bob never had
special faceplates made for his 10:1 winders, so it shows the same 0-500
turns on the face as the 20:1. This is wrong as each full turn of the
counter is actually 250 winds.

I'm just looking to get my original investment back out of it, so the first
person willing to part with $235 plus shipping will get it. As a bonus I
will also include a Wilder torque meter that can be mounted to the winder,
and a 3D printed brake for it.

If you're interested please contact me directly at 82 dot jake at gmail dot
com. I can send photos if desired.
