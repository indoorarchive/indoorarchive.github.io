While I haven't tried all of the strippers (only Harlan's and the one
from Poland), I can say that the best stripper is Harlan's. I can say
this because Ray is local to me, about a 20 minute drive, so if I need
it worked on I can call him and maybe drive down or meet him at a
local flying session.

Seriously, it works well, and Ray's service is great. Folks seek out
the Oppegard and Leeson strippers, so all three work well for flyers
at all levels. You can't go wrong with any of them.

One problem with the Harlan stripper is that you can easily wrap your
hand it in such a way to cut your fingers if you are not careful. If
you are careful though, no problems.
