I have used the Harlan and the Polish strippers and both are very good; I
bought the Polish one, made by Edward Ciapala. I think the choice may often
be influenced by where you live and the exchange rate with different
countries at the time.

On the Small Flying Arts web site, in the Indoor section, there is a thread
headed ‘Polish Rubber Stripper’ which deals with several different types and
quite a bit of related information and opinion.
