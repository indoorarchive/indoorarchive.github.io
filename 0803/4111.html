I poured some a year ago with Ray Harlan. Picked up gold and silver
44" long frames as well as 30" frames that were all purple, silver,
and gold. I've had it curing since then.. I tried covering with it
last summer but didn't have the best of luck on my first attempt.
Hasn't slacked on my frames, you mean covering frames or wing/stab
frames? Pretty dry I think, not like the wet stuff I've seen on other
planes, so it's not as sticky. I bought the 600mL bottle, should last
a long time.
