I got the Treet blades last night and tried them out.

I suspect when used for basic cutting work for indoor, they will work fine.
However they do not easily snap to a point.
There is bending that occurs before the metal snaps.
A pair of snips might make a sharp point, but pliers to not.

So overall I do not recommend them.
