I looked through my collection of AMA Model Aircraft Regulations, dating back to 1960, for the Multiple Records section. There is a gap in my collection between 1959-1960 and 1969. The section is not in the 1959-1960 book, but is in the 1969 book. Does anyone have copies of the regulations in this gap, and if so could they identify the year the section was first included?
 

 At any rate, the Multiple Records section has been with us at least 49 years. If there is some hue and cry about Outdoor Free Flight having problems with multiple records, why doesn't Jerry Murphy introduce a new rule for this discipline only, rather than causing a big stir among all of the other record keeping disciplines?
