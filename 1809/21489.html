      This is for anyone that is interested in joining us for some great Free Flight indoor flying at Hanger #3 in Rantoul, Il on October 5th, 6th and 7th 2018.

Information about the event and Hanger #3 can be found at:
https://bongeagles.org/rantoul-proposal-for-new-midwestern-indoor-flying-site/ https://bongeagles.org/rantoul-proposal-for-new-midwestern-indoor-flying-site/

Its an AMA sanctioned event, where you can flying any AMA Indoor Freeflight event. Its a great group of people who fly there and a pretty informal event, where many different indoor models are flown, like F1D, Penny Plane, A6, FAC,Peanut Scale,Science Olympiad, gliders, etc.
Spectaculars and others interested in Indoor Free Flight Modeling are also welcome.
If you have any questions about the this event, please contact me.

Cheers,

Kurt
