That expo center looks interesting. It's probably Cat 3, and it looks like
they can set it up with virtually no ceiling obstructions. They list a
daily rate of $2500, but they also offer move-in and move-out days at half
that rate as long as you don't need air conditioning. I wonder if we could
get 3 or 4 days at half price since we wouldn't want AC the entire time.
At the moment they don't have any events listed for the weekends of June
20-21, or June 27-28.

Of course it's also somewhat remote with the closest major airport an hour
away in Austin.
