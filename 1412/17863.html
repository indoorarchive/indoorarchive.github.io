I also prefer Kibbie but I will go wherever it turns out to be.

My reservation about Colorado Springs is the prospect of a lot of models
scrubbing the ceiling at the same time. Mid-airs are not a huge problem in
a high site but having a lot of models competing for the top 12 inches of
air space sounds like a problem to me.

OTOH flying gliders at a Colorado Springs USIC is a very attractive idea!
