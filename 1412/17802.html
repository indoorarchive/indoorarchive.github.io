The low times are in part due to the dome being located 7000 feet above sea
level.

 

The location is in Eagar Arizona which is right next to Springerville
Arizona.

Some of the confusion on the location comes from the contest being hosted by
the Phoenix Model

Airplane Club but the C.D. (me) lives in Albuquerque NM.

We realize it's a bit remote but our arrangement with the school keeps the
cost way down and we

get the dome for 3 full days.

In a couple of weeks I'll be sending out an information packet with maps,
motel info, a list of events, and

some of the other activities we have planned for the weekend.

My email is steveriley at cableone dot net

 

Please do not refer to it as the Ensphere. The school prefers that we call
it the Round Valley Dome.

The word ensphere is a transient verb and not a noun. Somebody way back when
thought it was a cool word.

Cheers and we hope to see you in the dome,

Steve Riley
