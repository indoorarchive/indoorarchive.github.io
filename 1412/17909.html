  In terms of the flying site, or the church itself? If the latter, well, it's pretty big. If the former, it's a regulation size gymnasium. Really a fantastic flying site, usually with extremely good air up near the ceiling. There are a few spots to stay out of, but if you're not flying F1D or F1R, you'll never notice any turbulence. It's got easily 35 ft of available height for ministick and F1R.
