> Anyway, the bottle is a square glass one a bit bigger than the size of a old Testors paint bottle, a little taller.
>
> Inside is a tapered steel pin which is used to dip drops of glue out of the bottle- no hypodermic needle or such.
>
> It has a removable center section which has the pin which is held by an inverted white nylon(?) lipped cone which serves to make the glue drip down the pin if you get glue up there, the cone part continues up as a hexagonal knob/handle thru the cap and is removable from the cap...so one can let the bottle sit, stoppered by the cone and not have to unscrew the cap every time since the threaded cap comes off separately. Simple but very well thought out and a joy to use. As a side bonus the steel rusts in the presence of ambroid clone glue giving it the same nice yellow tinge :). I'm sure it would do the same for Duco.
