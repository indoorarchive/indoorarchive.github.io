The Kent State Indoor contest is quickly approaching! The dates are
April 20-21 (Easter Weekend) at the Kent State Fieldhouse in Kent , Ohio.

The contest flyer and Mashup plan are on www.indoorfreefight.com

A direct link to the contest flyer is here:
http://indoorfreeflight.com/Kent_2019_ContestV1.pdf

*IMPORTANT PLEASE NOTE:*
I have just added a "Pilot Briefing" guideline and "Code of Conduct" for
the contest that I am attaching to this email to the group. A direct
link is below:
http://indoorfreeflight.com/Kent_State_Pilot%20Guidlines.pdf

I am posting this guide ahead of time since most contestants are from
out of state and I want all to have an opportunity to read the
guidelines ahead of time before they make their trip. These guidelines
will be posted at the contest and also be handed out to each contestant
at the time of registration. If you have prepaid for the contest, I will
email a copy of these guidelines to your paypal payment email address
shortly.

See you in Kent!
Don Slusarczyk
CD for the Kent State Indoor Contest.
