LeRoy,

 The roll I have weighs 2.2g/m2 and came from IMS years ago. This is the same as regular Ultrafilm. It is easy to use, but heavy for all except maybe LPP. It is what I used on my LPP and is fine for that. It crinkles up really well, if you like to do that before you cover (I do). As a comparison Super Ultrafilm weighs 1.2g/m2 and O-S film weighs .8g/m2.
