I posted a couple videos from the Flight Fest on YouTube.
The first is a neat full lenght video of John Kagan, Rob Romash and myslef doing some standard catapult mass launches. This was a real 'blast' The gliders were so closely matched it was unreal. One launch all three gliders touched down at the exact same time. This one is not that close, but still close.
http://youtu.be/sl0guo_8_uY

The next is a compilation from the rubber mass launch events. These were great, with Rob and I giving John some grief fo his bad luck. The Ross Flyer is a really neat little airplane. John decided to spend two weeks building one to the insanely low (indoor ruining) weight of three grams. Rob and I decided to build one the night before the event back in the lodge. This model was completed in less than 90 minutes, start to finish. John did help, by 'building' the wheels, which took him the full 90 minutes I think.
http://youtu.be/UOWI5gfL2dA

There was also some unauthorized borrowing of airplanes involved. Many stories from such a short event. Another great time.
