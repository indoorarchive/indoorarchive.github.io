I've been using 1/32" A grain sheet I bought from Specialized Balsa a few
years ago. It's not the best wood, but the sheets were all in the weight
range I asked for and I've made some decent spars with it. If I need
anything thinner I sand it down. Some day I'm going to try cutting my own
spar wood on my table saw.

For all of my C grain wood I'm now buying exclusively from Nick Aikman. As
Kang said he has excellent communication and the wood I received was
exactly what I wanted.
