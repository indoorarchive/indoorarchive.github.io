Some possible good new in regards to helium availability. After ~18
months of waiting I was able to get my tank exchanged today. Only one
place locally had helium for sale and up until recently they were
getting rationed amounts of helium and only selling to companies under
contract. As of today they are selling again to the general public but
that can change again at anytime. The cost has gone way up, ~$115 with
all taxes and fees for a 80 cubic foot tank refill, it use to be $35-$55
previously. So I wanted to let anyone who owns a tank and has been
having issues getting a fill to call around again and see as you may be
able to get a fill now.
