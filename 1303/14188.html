I'm getting excited as the contest season begins. We have another F1D

Master Calendar http://www.freeflight.org/Competition/Calendar.htm

Pontiac, in May. This year, in lieu of USIC, I will probably go to Lakehurst

attend one day -- the Peach State Indoor Championship. I probably won't make
that this year.

After the Kibbie, I will likely miss USIC this year to attend to non-indoor
obligations. I hate to miss it, though. I hope to round out the year with a
Labor Day trip to Lakehurst.

So, what are your plans?
