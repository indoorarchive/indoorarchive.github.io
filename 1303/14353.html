In order to make the 2013 Indoor Nats a success, we are looking for people to volunteer to work at the scoring and processing table. This 5 day event covers a total of 71 hours of which 100 volunteer- hours are required. So that this task isn't put on just a few individuals, I have divided up the work schedule into 25 volunteer segments ranging from just 3 to 5 hours. The job will not be difficult as training and processing materials needed ie. score cards, score sheets, scales, rules, and measures will be provided by the CD or event organizers. Therefore I would like to highly encourage all contestants or interested parties to volunteer for a minimum of 1 time slot over this event. This is your Nats, come out and help make this event a success for all of us, by volunteering. Refer to posted schedule under Events, 2013 USIC for time slots.

Thanks,
Jeff Annis
