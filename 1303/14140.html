Ya, that's a problem I've been annoyed by for years with the Banks/Brown style. A spar seems to have a limit for how many times it can be tweaked to different positions. Come to think, I have no more working OPP VPs due to mangling the tubes on my one and only nice, tight Kevlar hinge VP (style now sworn off for good.)
