To get 1/16” post, I just sand the edges of 1/16” sq wood with a sanding block.
Strictly speaking, sanding blockS, #240-#320 or so. I use Great Planes Easy-Touch Hand Sander 5.5” on a cutting mat.
No special technique needed.
Good flying, Aki
