Chuck Markos has introduced produce bag film to the Bong Eagles. He has
also showed how to use Aleene's FunCraft Foam Glue from Michael's Craft
Stores to attach it. That glue is water thinned and dries to a rubbery
film like a contact cement. I've covered the wing of an Aerodactyl with
it. Chuck used it on indoor tow line gliders.
