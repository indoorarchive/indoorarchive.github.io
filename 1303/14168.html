Sebastian Perez spent about 6 hours today flying his two WIF-SO gliders. Near the end of the day he had posted a best flight of 32.8 seconds with each glider so I suggested he try to raise his record times. The result was new Cat 1 Sr. records of 63.1 seconds for UCLG and SCLG. Here are the times for his 9 flights:

27.5, 30.6, 24.2, 28.9, 30.0, 28.1, 26.3, 32.5, and 30.4.

He says that his summer project is going to be the Sr. Cat 1 HLG record.
