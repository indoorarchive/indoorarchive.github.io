Good morning: For the "kid builds" I don't ask that they shrink tissue
after building, and don't on the instruction sheet packed with the
kits. Think that's kinda too far ahead for "tweens.

On all the tissue, I spritz the sheet, then iron it with a regular
clothes iron set on "cotton". I also suggest wadding the plan into a
ball, then smoothing out. The DD's are built directly on the tissue and
don't have too many wrinkles so that seems to work.

Have you ever looked at the film bags used at the grocery store produce
dept?
Around here that's fairly light.
