Rob Romash and Jeff Annis were also there. Attendance was light this year by flyers unfortunately. We had enough for F1D flyers for a contest, drift was a bit high.

Highlights:
Getting mid-aired by mini-sticks THREE times on one of my new light F1Ds.
Watching (I think Buxton was behind the controls) one of Romash's R/C biplanes flying in formation with a penny plane take out another penny plane in a great mid-air. IIRC, it was Romash's LPP.
Watching Kagan Catapult glider end up in the diorama three times.

I'll let someone else post the winners. Jeff and I were deep in conversation when the awards were being handed out and I only know my own and maybe one of Jeff's.
