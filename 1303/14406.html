I thought some of you would be interested in the open results of some of the West Baden events back in 1983:


HLG: Bernie Boehm, 136.0 (1:07.6 + 1:08.4) Wally Simmers competed, finishing 7th of 11 with 82.4)

Easy B: Chuck Markos, 18:46. (38 contestants; models were still using condenser paper.)

Paper Stick: Jim Richmond, 26.57

ROG Cabin: Rich Doig, 25:12

F1D: Larry Calliau, 73:23 (35:32, 37:51)

Pennyplane: Dennis Jaeks, 16:00

Manhattan Cabin: Walt VanGorder, 9:50
