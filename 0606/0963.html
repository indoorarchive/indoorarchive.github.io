I just uploaded a file "Cement_applicator.pdf" into file sections.
Is a small article (almost all pictures) on how to build in very easy
way an inexpensive and practical cement aplicator.

The only strange material need is a tip used in medicene. I bought the
in a vet lab. They give me 20 tips per 1 peso (30 dollar cents).

Besides that, you need a plastic bottle and a shot piece of music
wire, that wil act as a sealer or to remove solid cement from tip.

Article is not very extensive on explanations, but images speaks by
themselves.

Hope you like it. This aplicator is almost indestructible and last
long time.
