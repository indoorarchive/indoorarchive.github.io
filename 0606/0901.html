I would like to put in proposals for A6 and F1M to become official AMA
events. Is there any support for this idea?

At USIC this year there were 14 people making official A6 flights and
9 making official F1M flights. Participants in both events included
many of the best indoor fliers in the USA. I think this indicates a
high level of interest in these events. F1M seems to be attracting one
or two new participants each year and the comments that I've heard
from the new people have been very positive.
