My question is the best way to get it to circle. Does the direction matter, since there is no
prop? On my powered planes, I use a combination of left thrust, stab tilt, and angle
between the motorstick and tailboom, which results in a nice circle with a diameter that is
pretty constant throughout the flight.

Any suggestions concerning how to best induce a turn with a glider will be appreciated.
