> Would ministick wing posts be strong enough for AROG?(.065 x .034 7.5 lb.)
> Or with boron on that?

Overkill! No need for boron. I'm living proof that you can get an AROG down to 300 mg without boron or even a rolled tube fuse (and still have it strong as all get-out). For your wingposts, I'd go with something around .04 sq. You could get away with less, but there comes more danger of breakage during handling.

Good flying,
