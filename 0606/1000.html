--"John Kagan" wrote:
> that site looks great. do you know how tall the
> ceiling is? i'm guessing about 35 feet?

The ceiling is 14 (46 ft) meters tall in center. And gets lower to the
sides, in a cupola form. The name of the place is "La cupula", and you
can take the risk to touch a couple of times, but not more than that.

--"Lori" wrote:
> Where were the Nationals held at? It looks like a
> very interesting building....and very cool site.

Our indoor nationals usually are held at Embalse, Cordoba, at the same
time the outdoor events. The same city where the World FF '05 contest
was held.

Unfortunelly the nationals are almost in winter,and the temperature
are low, arround 19C (66F). The place is cool, but is far from what
you guys have in USA :). The air quiality is not good, as words from
the F1d fliers. Still is a nice place.

standart booms.
