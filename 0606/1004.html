My LPP's have a dropped tail. I don't know if it really does anything
for such a short configuration, and the weight savings with LPP aren't
that critical (easy to build to weight), but the models certainly do
stand out in the air. LPP's tend to have squared off flying surfaces
with tip dihedral and it is easy to lose sight of your model when a
bunch of LPP's are in the air. The drooping tail was really easy to
spot.
