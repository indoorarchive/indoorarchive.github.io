T-shirts for to help support the 2018 USA F1D are now available online
for purchase. Please note I have changed the way I was going to do this.
Initially I was collecting info on what size shirts people wanted to
order but then found out that Customink will do a fundraiser format
which means they take care of shipping shirts and collecting payment etc.

I have set up a Custom Ink Fundraiser website and made it 4 weeks long
so people have time to order shirts.

https://www.customink.com/fundraising/2018-usa-f1d-shirt

T-shirts as well as long sleeve shirts are available for purchase in
sizes up to 4XL. Please note there are two shipping options when you
place your order. The default option is to have the shirt delivered to
your home address once printed. The second option is for pickup at the
World Championships in West Baden. Only chose this option if you will be
coming to the F1D world champs and can pick up the shirt or have someone
pick it up for you. This option is great if you are an F1D team member
from outside the USA and want to buy a shirt. You can buy it now from
the website and pick it up at the World Championships. Shirts which are
prepaid and unclaimed at the end of the WC will not be refunded.

Thanks to John Warren for the shirt design!
