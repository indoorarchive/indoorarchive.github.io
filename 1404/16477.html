It seems to be impossible to get definite word on this years flying times. I'm going to go out on a limb and say that the flying times will be the same as last year:

Saturday, April 5 - 9:00am until 6:00pm with last launch at 5:30pm
Sunday, April 6 - 9:00am until 5:00pm with last launch at 4:30pm

A good number of world class indoor fliers will be in attendance at this event even without the USA team - which is tied up in Romania at the World Championships. Everyone is welcome to come to either participate or hang around and watch high level indoor flying.

There is a world record sanction in place for the 2 days of the event.

I'm not sure if the hotel has discounted rooms still available. If they do you must give them the code 0414IND when calling.

See you there!
