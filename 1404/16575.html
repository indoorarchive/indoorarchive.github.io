I've approved the entry forms, and they should be mailed out soon.
 The majority of the contest will look and feel the same as the usual Kibbie Dome Annual.
 

 However, there are some additional tasks that will require volunteers. Who is available to help with these?
 

 - Scale judging
 - FAC events
 - Processing (this will be more spread-out than at a time-slot contest)
 - Scoring (same as above)
 

 Thanks,
 John
 

 p.s. A few people have asked me who is receiving the entry fees. The apparent concern is over who might profit from the contest. However, there is no profit to be had. The AMA and NFFS are going to put up the money up front, and the entry fees are designed to cover the costs. The only difference is that we aren't collecting commitments in advance, like we have in recent years, to make sure that we didn't incur a loss.
 

 I expect at least as many participants as the usual Kibbie Annual, with hopefully a few more because it is the Nats/USIC. Please don't be the one that says they are skipping the contest because of some variation on, "the AMA is running it". First, we, the Indoor community, are running it - this is our event. Second, I'll personally drive over to your house and give you a wet willy for being ridiculous.
