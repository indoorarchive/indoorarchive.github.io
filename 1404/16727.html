I work for a bureaucracy. I know how they function.

 First issue: Do they have an appeals process? This is most critical. No process, no chance.
 If not, you need to address this first.
 

 Second, you need to reach out to the F1D fliers in the countries that voted to pass this.
 They will need to find a leader, find out the local consensus and communicate this to their FAI rep.
 If the rep finds out the entire local F1D community is against the vote, we might have a chance. There will be communities that believe this change is positive.
 

 Third: You have to embrace the fact that you still might lose. In fact, I think it likely.
