Andrew Barron, an FAI towline glider fliers, sent me this message Tueday. Seems to me this opportunity is a good one for those near enough to get there.


Subject: Indoor Flying - New Scotland Armory - April 26


We have a unique opportunity to try the New Scotland Armory in Albany for
indoor model flying (FF & R/C) on Saturday, April 26th. See attached
Flyer.

The better the turnout, the better the chance of being able to book it for
future dates. This is a trial run for Sage College (new owner of the
Armory) and for us modelers.

Please help spread the word by distributing the attached flyer as widely as
  possible to people on your lists who might be interested in the Armory
flying. The nearby Swap meet may help make it worth the drive for those with
R/C interests.

Thanks
Andrew Barron
