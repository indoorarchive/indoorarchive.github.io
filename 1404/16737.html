It's pretty interesting to me how concerned people are about the FAI rules when no such discussion has taken place about the rules process for AMA rules. Here are the people who decide the fate of rules change proposals for AMA indoor f/f rules and the districts they represent:
I: Ray Harlan
 II: Mary Jane Barber
 III: Walt VanGorder (chair)
 IV: John Diebolt
 V: Bill Gowen
 VI: Larry Coslick
 VII: Dennis Tyson
 VIII: Jesse Shepherd
 IX: H. Stan Chilton
 X: Clarence Mather
 XI: Andy Tagliafico
There are several people on this list that either have never been indoor competitors or are no longer indoor competitors. Several can only be reached by snail mail. When a highly technical rules change proposal is submitted it would be desirable to have active, experienced indoor competition fliers deciding on whether to accept or reject the proposal.
