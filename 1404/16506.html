To all going to West Baden I got a call this morning about 8:45 that the town is closed due to flooding although the hotel is fine but you need to come in from the east on 56 from the south-west. The lady said that the town floods a couple time a year when they get rain for several days. The hotel although never is closed or flooded when this occurs. You need to come in on 56 east and turn off on bear cave rd. (route 1000) just before you get to French Lick. Bear Creek Rd. goes past the Donald Ross Golf course and turn on Baden Ave where the hotel is located.
 

 Jeff Annis
