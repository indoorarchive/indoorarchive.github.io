Greetings.

I have submitted the Kickstarter project. It may take up to a week to get it approved they say.

Once the site goes live, I will post the link here.

One thing I was planning to add is a storage box kit for the meter. Being loose isn't good on the torque arm as it can bend. It was meant only as a way to hold the meter for storage. I was planning on 1/8" Baltic Birch plywood, laser cut.

Originally I was thinking the top should just fit on the box and I'd wrap it with a rubber band to secure.
Kang suggested doing something a bit more classy. Magnets or clasps. That makes things more fiddly for the end user and increases costs.

I would love to hear what folks would like to see in a storage box for the torque meters, or if this matters to you at all.
