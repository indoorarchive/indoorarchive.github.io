When I researched a commercial supplier for stripper blades I looked as similar companies. Great technical details on the subject, but the minimum diameter was in the 2+ inch range. Not sure about this one.

 Another thing to be aware of is on the Oppergard stripper there was never a "standard" blade. They were frequently marked with diameters on them and seems like different combinations may have been used to tune the stripper when made. Point being, not all sets of even original blades may interchange.
 

 I know Ray Harlan has found a commercial source to make his blades. Maybe he would share?
