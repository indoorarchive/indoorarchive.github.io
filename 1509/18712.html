Re-posting a comment from HPA, because the Juniors did a great job:
 

 - Joseph Szczur is on the steep part of the learning curve, and showed a lot of improvement. He also got in a little F3P practice each morning down on the R/C end of the hanger. He is on the F3P World Championship team, and is the reigning F3A Junior World Champion!

- Wyatt Wear edged up into the 18 minute range, and is in the beginning stages of refining his new models. His first F1Ds (only several months ago) featured solid motorsticks and prop blades. His latest models have the normal rolled tubes and built-up props.

- David Yang is the real deal. He put up several 22+ flights that compared favorably with Senior competitors. But, more than that, he has a calm, studied approach and a proficiency that makes it seem like he's been flying F1D for years. David is already into the nuances of F1D.
