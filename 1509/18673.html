Vlad,
         3M spray thinned with TCE (trichloroethylene) is my preferred method. Use a fine brush to apply the thinned 3M every .25 to .5 inches along the spars and ribs. (No need to coat the entire spar or rib) When dry lay your outline on a frame covered with film. Use TCE to paint around the outline and ribs the same way you would use water or saliva when you covered with microfilm.
