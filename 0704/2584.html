Two students and their coach (Mayfield City School) were flying for practice
next to me during the Kent indoor
contest last Saturday. I chatted off and on with the coach. One of the
students, Brant Frederickson, was flying a pusher. It was a standard wing
forward layout with a low-slung tail boom to allow clearance for the
propeller. It flew very well at 7.07 grams. Best flight was 4:04. I was
impressed and took a photo of the model which I'll put in my picture album.

The coach, Brant Frederickson, mentioned that the site at Ohio State was in
the height range of 60-65 ft.
