I know a lot of us like to wait until the last minute (me included),
but "late" deadline of April 27th is fast approaching for USIC entry
forms.

AMA has only received 27 so far and, as much fun as it is to make them
nervous, we probably want to keep everyone reassured that the great
attendance we've enjoyed of late will continue.

So, now that your taxes are out of the way, save yourself $15 to $25
and get your entry in right away!

Here are the form links (you may need to paste them back together if
the list splits them):

http://www.modelaircraft.org/events/Entry%20Forms/07natsentryfrm.pdf

http://www.modelaircraft.org/events/Entry%
