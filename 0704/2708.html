I had the pleasure of spending last Saturday judging Wright Stuff at
the Illinois State tournament held in the University of Illinois
Armory. Here's a brief recap.

37 team competed, 1 team attempted the pusher bonus
9 teams had one or more construction violations
7 teams had incomplete logs
3 teams had no log

16 flights under 10 seconds
3 flights under 1 minute
6 flights under 2 minutes
7 flights under 3 minutes
2 flights under 4 minutes
3 flights over 4 minutes

The top three teams were a joy to watch. During testing they each had
flights over 5 minutes, but no one was able to put together an
official flight over 5 minutes.
