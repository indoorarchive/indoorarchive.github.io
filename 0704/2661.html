We are putting together an F1D Regional and Cat I record trial on May
27th in Willoughby Ohio.

There are two connected sites. One is very close to the Cat I limit,
with open girders (not too scrubbable, but good for no-touch). The
other is about 18' with a flat ceiling.

This will probably be the last opportunity to qualify for the F1D
finals in this region. So, if you want to go but haven't qualified
yet, you want to boost up your regional points, or you are gunning for
one of the Cat I records, come on down.

Please let me or Wayne Johnson know if you are considering it, as we
are trying to get a headcount to cover expenses for the guard.

P.S. The primary focus will be on getting F1D juniors qualified. If
you know of any area juniors who want in on the action, get 'em over
here.
