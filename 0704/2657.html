One thing that I've noticed, and something that I'm certainly guilty
of, is the tendancy to use full motors for testing in low ceiling
flying. I guess since flights are generally pretty short there is a
tendancy to fly on full motors. We really should be doing more testing
with partial motors. This should keep the models from bashing into the
ceiling and allow safer testing.
