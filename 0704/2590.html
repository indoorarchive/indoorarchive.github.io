Hi Bob,

Thank you for writing about our model. May I make a few additions to your
text?

Two students(Saul Fredrickson and Tian You) and their coach(Brant
Fredrickson) (Mayfield City School) were flying for practice

next to me during the Kent indoor

contest last Saturday. I chatted off and on with the coach. One of the

students, Saul Fredrickson, was flying a pusher. It was a standard wing

forward layout with a low-slung tail boom to allow clearance for the

propeller. It flew very well at 7.07 grams. Best flight was 4:04. I was

impressed and took a photo of the model which I'll put in my picture album.

 

The coach, Brant Fredrickson, mentioned that the site at Ohio State was in

the height range of 60-65 ft. I believe the French Field House is ceiling is
a little lower than Kent. If Kent is 48 ft. Ohio State is about 42 ft.

In addition Chardon had a pusher at Kent with a similar concept, but
different design and construction. Thank you again,

Brant
