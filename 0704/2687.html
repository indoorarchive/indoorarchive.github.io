On 27 April will start Argentina national aeromodeling championships.
It will be held at Embalse, Cordoba and as many years the competitions
will include about 60 classes from indoor, outdoor free flight, radio,
control line.

This will be our 61st NATS, hoping a big general attendance as in past
years, but cant same the same for indoor classes. Still, we usually
have some important assistance from neighboard countries (Uruguay and
Chile)

Ok, I will try to pick some pictures, videos and informatio to share
with all you.


I have two videos taken in our 59th NATS (2005). First one is an
ornitopter, built by Alejandro Garcia (Uruguay team). The second one
is a film of two F1D in flight, From Sergio del Patto and Raul Cuello,
both from Argentina.
