A general rule of thumb that seems to work well is to schedule major
regional contests no closer to each other than every other weekend. The
outdoor FF community does a pretty good job of this. Indoor meets would
benefit from this approach as well.

For 2008 I have already booked the Colorado Springs 2-day for the same
weekend, March 25-26. It would be great if the Kent State, WNYFFS and MW
States Champs CDs would plan accordingly and not be on consecutive (or
worse--the same) weekends.

MW, Flint, Peach, WNYFFS and Kent CDs, will you please contact me? I can
still move our March 08 dates if needed, but I have to do it now. Otherwise
MMM will keep March 25-26.

Thx
Don DeLoach
