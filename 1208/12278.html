Thank you Mark for the invite.

 

For those in Florida, the Central Florida Indoor Free flight Club flies
every third weekend of the month, except in July and August (way too hot in
the hangars), so we build and meet in an air-conditioned room.

 

The site is at the Sun'n'Fun air grounds in Lakeland, Fl., Hangar E and
there are planes up in the air by 9am and we've been going up to about 5pm.

 

Everyone is welcome to attend.

 

For more information or if you have questions, please contact Ren Monllor
