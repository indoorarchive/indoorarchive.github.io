Ron's book was given to me by Dr. Hacker, and I learned almost everything
from that book before passing it along to another young flier. Before I got
help from people at actual competitions, this book was the only way to
learn all the skills needed. It took off where Lew's book left, adding more
advanced techniques.

I would buy an updated edition with some articles with some of the new
technologies, such as VPs, boron and carbon construction, steering, and
some of the new classes.

Who would buy an updated edition? Who would want to contribute to it?
