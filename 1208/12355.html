OK...here is the secret decoder ring...having been the target of hacks
because I have a very public email address.
 
Change your password ASAP and frequently (!) and do not open any links when
 you get a note from somebody you know and they have only one or four
other "sent to's" on the email. What the deal is your password has been
grabbed when you open that link and your address book is grabbed too. They use
your login to just send spam. That is the good news. Wait until you whole
system is destroyed sometime and you will get the message.
 
In the meantime...notifying folks and CHANGING YOUR PERSONAL PASSWORD ASAP
OR FREQUENTLY is very important.
 
Just some advice...Rick
