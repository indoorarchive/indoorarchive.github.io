This is what I have read: the competition title is according the name of one Belgrade local community in the downtown. It is organized by a local Belgrade aeromodeling club.

It is separate from the World Championships. For example, in 2014, the World Championship will be in Slanic, Romania, but the Dorcol cup will not. Also, the Dorcol Cup is held every year. Since 2007, it has coincided with either the World Championship or the European Championship.
