--- In Indoor_Construction_at_yahoogroups.com, Dwight Brown <freepistol@...> wrote:
d Is there anything to choose from between Ambroid or Duco? Would I be far off to just mix either one of them 50/50 with acetone?>>

No, you won't be far off with either one of them thinned 50/50. From there you can experiment with (preferably) thinner to get a feel for what works for you. Even the viscosities out of the tube have changed a bit through the years, so ratios can be misleading. (I remember when Ambroid used to be thicker, browner, and more fragrant.) What kind of model are you building? Some milligrams overweight on glue won't be critical on your first models anyhow. Ya gotta start somewhere.
