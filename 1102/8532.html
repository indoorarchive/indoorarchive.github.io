Congratulations on F1M flight. As regards boron, I always degrease it
before applying and I'm sure this helps. However, if Ambroid is the
adhesive, it's still all too easy for local separation to occur. I now
use Duco or similar since it has more contact strength and dry joints
which cause separation areless likely to occur. I also test each
component eg a wing spar by bending locallyto compress the boron and
correct as necessary. Once the component passes the test without fault,
it's ready for use.

Bob
