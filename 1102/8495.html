Some people are saying that the newer batches of Tan SS are good. I haven't done much testing so don't really know. If it's really good then it's a huge bargain.

Otherwise it's 5/99. 3/02, 7/97, 10/97, 2/99 in pretty close to that order. How to get some? You have to have awfully good friends or be awfully lucky. If you see any for sale at any price you need to be ready to write a check. If you spend a few hours thinking about it you'll be waiting for the next batch to be available.
