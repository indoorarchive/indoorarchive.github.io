Entry forms have just started arriving by snail mail for the U.S. Indoor
Championships at Johnson City, May 25 to 29, and the U.S. Outdoor Free Flight
Nationals at Muncie, June 18 to 22. If you haven't attended either in the last
few years and didn't get anything in the mail, you can download free forms and
information at:

http://www.modelaircraft.org/events/nats/entryforms.aspx

Remember, to fly both AMA and FAC / NFFS non-AMA events, you need to fill out
two forms. Deadlines to avoid late fees are May 1 for Indoor, and June 1 for
Outdoor.

Our Indoor Event Director this year is Abram Van Dover, 757-877-2830,
vandover_at_cox.net.

AMA event questions and information can be found at
http://www.modelaircraft.org/events/
or directed to Headquarters at 800-435-9262
JoEllen Eppards, ext. 298
Randa Coats, ext. 232
Yolanda Jones, ext. 261
Colleen Pierce, ext. 252

Remember to give copies to a friends who may not be digital like you, but who
might like to attend, and help make this the best Nats ever. Notify me off-list,
and I'll do it for you.
