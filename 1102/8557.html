To follow up John's note, it's critical that we receive your commitment to attend the contest no later than Friday, March 25. To date I've received only four entry forms. (Okay five, myself included…)

We have a limited staff to set up the event, and we also have to plan for lunches, etc. We want to accommodate as many free-flighters as possible, and tucking in the tables amongst all the aircraft and displays in the hangar can be challenging. So the more time we have to do so, the better. Help us make this a special event for us. Flying in the Eagle hangar is great fun – ask John!
