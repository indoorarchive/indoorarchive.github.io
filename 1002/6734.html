LPP PRO/AM EVENT

This is a new event and unclear to many. We will be flying the Pro/Am part Saturday, and the Sportsman flyoff of the amateurs on Sunday. Those of you who keep your issues of Model Aviation, check the Indoor column in October 2009, page 144. John describes his ideas in detail. Pre-entry will be included in the East Tenn State University Dorm Reservation form in the Feb issue of FF Digest.

A-6 KITS

There is a very good A-6 kit out there in www.shortysbasement.com. Click on Indoor Kits. I have built two, and have gotten over 4 minutes so far. Construction time is under 2 hours.


Product Information

This
is a flying indoor model kit - Class A6. Wingspan 12 1/2 inches. Kit
includes ready-made prop, motor stick, ribs, spars, full sized plan,

non-expert flyers. Suitable for age 7 years and up.
