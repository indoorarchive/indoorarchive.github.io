Greg, I got a couple of pounds of it and tried it out last week at our
Racine indoor session. It seems to be pretty good, on my LPP I turned
consistantly high times (for me) and even did a personal best on my
MiniStick. I would recommend it. They have it in both 1/8 and 1/4 inch
widths (possibly others - I had been led to believe it was only available
in the 1/8" width, but not so) and reasonably priced at around $25 a
pound plus shipping. I found that it didn't take quite the turns that
some of my Tan II would and it is a bit "torquey" but I think it's a
keeper. When I called the lady said they were getting low on their
supply so I wouldn't hesitate. On their web site (Not a great one) they
do have a secure order form and that's the only way they take orders - no
phone orders. What I got was Novenber 2009
Check them out at faimodelsupply.com
