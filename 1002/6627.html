I'd vote but I don't even know what the present rules are or where to find them.

I inquired about an A6 article written some time ago. so as to get a bit of a handle on this topic; as it seems to be an important topic, and I only received one response from Bill (I think it was), and he couldn't lead me any closer to the information I was looking for.

It's kind of tough to make an informed decision when you don't have all the information.... Kind of like a first grader trying to do 8th grade math.

Then again guys, I'm a real new-commer so, please take this from where it's comming.
