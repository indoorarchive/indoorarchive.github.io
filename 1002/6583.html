Handicapping is just a form of social engineering to level the playing
field. Why must the playing field be leveled in the first place? My 10
year old son golfs not for his score compared to other but because he
likes to play golf, that is his motivation. If he wants to elevate his
game then he decides if he wants to devote more time to practice or not.
Same applies to indoor, each person finds their motivation. Some like to
sport fly some like to competitively fly.
