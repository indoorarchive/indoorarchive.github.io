The purpose of the short tubes placed over motors near the rear hook & prop hook is to prevent the knots formed during unwinding in these two areas from bunching up and stopping the prop from turning or preventing the motor from fully unwinding. I don't believe that violates the rule. If it does violate the rule then so does bunching of knots & they too should be illegal. I submit that is another example of the unintended consequences of rules &/or rules changes.
Gary Hodson
