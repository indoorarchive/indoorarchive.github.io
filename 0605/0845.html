Will it be hot? I hope so! Hot is good. Cool and rainy is bad.

There are plenty of fast food places close by. Like Nick said, I don't usually seem to have time to go out until the end of the day. Others (like Kagan and Romash) will take naps, go out for food, etc. Maybe someday I'll be that relaxed and confidant but I don't think it will be any time soon.

If you come on Tuesday night the loading door will be open for bringing stuff in. Sometimes you can park your car just inside the building to load or unload. There are one or two large 4 wheeled carts that people use to take their stuff from the loading area to where they are set up. The demand for these carts is pretty high.
