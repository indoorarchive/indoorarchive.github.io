My standard model I built for Flint weighs in a 4.2 grams. My standard
flight times were 1:07 and 1:10, I then flew it in Unlimited and got
1:10 and 1:10. Actually all three on my 1:10 flights were one after
another. I was not 100% happy with the roll out on those flights, they
were good, but rolled out a little too fast as the glider flew a little
fast for about 5 or 6 seconds before settling to normal glide. I wish
they flew glider a little longer to like 10am.

HLG was another story! I got first and last place as I was the only
entrant, my flight total was a whopping 51 seconds I believe. I still
and will always stink at HLG. I did manage to get one flight to roll out
and not stall but it flew ito the wall and slide down the wall. I think
I may have been able to break 40 seconds on that one!
