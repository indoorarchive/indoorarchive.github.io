A reminder to you all that our 8th Empire State Indoor Championships will be
held this Sunday at the Buffalo Bills fieldhouse in Orchard Park, NY, a
Buffalo suburb. Doors open at 8 AM and the meet starts at 9, ending at 5 PM.
The $30 entry fee flies all FF events. For anyone interested, there will be
informal radio control flying in the fieldhouse from 6-10 PM. I can send a
contest flyer to anyone interested. Remember- you'll have a floor area of
200 x 400 ft. plus an absolute ceiling height of 128 ft.

The official catering service for the building informed me Monday morning
that they will not be selling food or beverages in the building during the
contest, so bring your own or plan to take a break and find a bite nearby.
NOTE: No food or beverages can be brought into the building. This is a Bills
rule.

I hope to see a bunch of you there.


Bob Clemens
