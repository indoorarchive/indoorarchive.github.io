Hi,
     I just got home from the Flint, Mi indoor contest. As usual the
contest was very well run and things seemed to be going smoothly.
Early the air was very rough but around 9:30 the air was getting
good. Unfortunately, the glider event went until 9:45.
     Don Slusarczyk really cleaned up winning all three glider
events. The most impressive thing was he beat many large gliders in
unlimited with his standard glider. Congratulations to Don.

There were about 10 people flying in each of the catapult events.
Here are the unoffical results:
SCLG
1) Don Slusarczyk
2) Richard Thompson
3) Kurt Krempetz

UCLG
1) Don Slusarczyk
2) Kurt Krempetz
3) Bob Warmann

HLG
1) Don Sluzarczyk

I had a great time!

Kurt
