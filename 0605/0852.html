Gary Hodson asked, "I heard that there is a dime scale Hughes Racer by
Guillow... If not by Guillow, did anyone make a dime scale Hughes racer?"

If Guillow did have a Hughes Racer, I've never seen or heard of the plan.
But then, how many people have seen or heard of their dime plan (from a
1930's kit) for a Fairchild F45? Indeed, how many people even know of the
F45 (not many, but I just finished one). So, there very well could have
been a Hughes kit and plan. And maybe others of interest to Dime Scale fans.
Guillow dime plans seem to be comparatively obscure.

How about inquiring at Guillow? They must have some sort of record of past
kits.
