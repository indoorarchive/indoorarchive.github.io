Somehow my stiffness coefficient program has disappeared. Probably when I bought a new computer a couple of years ago - shows how much building I've been doing lately.
 Anyway, it was a downloadable program that could reside on the desktop and opened with a mouse click (or two) and showed a neat little grey panel that had windows to put in thickness, width and length dimensions, weight and buckling load.  I have had it so long, I don't recall where it came from. Searches for stiffness coefficient programs lead to Don Slusarczyk's website and program.
