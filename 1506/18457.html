I just uploaded version 27 of the pstiffness for calculating the density and stiffness of balsa sheets. Version 27 and version 25 give significantly different stiffness coefficient results for certain ranges of input. The calculated SC 's from version 27 will often be lower than those from version 25, often by nearly 20% for those particular ranges. The results for version 27 are the correct results. I would suggest using this version at all times.
