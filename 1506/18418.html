2015 AMA Indoor Free Flight Nationals reminder
When: Monday, 08 June 2015 05:00 PM to 01:30 AM
    (GMT) Greenwich Mean Time - Dublin / Edinburgh / Lisbon / London

Where: Colorado Springs CO. City Auditorium

Notes: This is a site much like that available to the majority of flyers, a smooth 37 foot ceiling. The site is located in down town Colorado Springs with hotels and dining spots all with in easy walking distance. Colorado Springs is a great vacation venue which means your family will enjoy the trip as well. Full details available at the AMA.
