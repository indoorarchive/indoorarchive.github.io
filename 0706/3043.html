Re: glue syringes ... I purchase hypodermic syringes at the local hobby shop that have unsharpened needles that are wonderful for very controlled glue application. They last about a month and a half before the solvents start to get to the rubber plungers, but they only cost a dollar each, so it's not a big issue.
