  Perhaps John Kagan will again post USIC/AMA Indoor FF Nats results at http://www.modelaircraft.org/events/NatsNews07/0530.pdf

  I think that Kurt Krempetz won both cat glider events, but I did not take any notes. Kurt's Dad is sick and they were headed home.

  The standard cat glider challenge was won by Kenny Krempetz. My unofficial time on his flight was 82 seconds. I think that 14 flyers flew in this. After mild browbeating by the officials, I think everyone who still had a plane flew in this. <Grin>

  I saw many discus launches for HLG.

  Chuck Markos won Race To Roof narrowly edging out Ray Harlan. Both their times were under 10 seconds.

  The P-24 Mass Launch was won by Tem Johnson with a time of just under 6 minutes. This required one "good hit" followed by one trip downward THROUGH the scoreboard followed by more flying.

  Some lightweight events are underway now.
