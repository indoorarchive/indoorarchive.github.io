I got pretty good reviews of the wood that I took to USIC, and am
getting ready to cut some more... I have quite a bit of 4.5# to 5.5#
blocks, and if anyone has any particular needs, or has suggestions on
what sizes/weights would be good, feel free to email me...

The one comment that I consistantly got was that the sheets were in
odd size/weight combinations... so I don't want to make the same
mistakes again... I'm planning on cutting quite a bit of .032 -> .036
of 5#+ for spars, and should be able to get a bunch of good C from the
mid 4# sheets in .012 -> .015 for motor sticks...

So if anyone wants to email me a cutting list, let me know before it
is all cut up... next run will probably be 500+ sheets, and I can do
custom orders if I know what they are...

Thanks to all that gave advice at USIC, and anyone that got sheets can
feel free to email me with comments/suggestions...
