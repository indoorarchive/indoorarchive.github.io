My question may have be moot. Unless the regulation is buried
elsewhere, the AMA Indoor rules (which have been revised in the last
couple years) don't address what I seem to recall being "assisted
launching". The concern was that a container might be interpreted as an
assistance, and; therefore, not allowed. I've had trouble with ROG's
before; both in getting them to the launch area in one piece, then
preventing them from getting snagged in rough flooring.

I found nothing about homesteading or tap dancing. A shame. No one
would be crazy enough to chance having their equipment destroyed by a
Mr. MaGoo. They might be crazy, however. The odds get better in a crowd.
