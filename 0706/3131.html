 Rude is walking under someone else's plane coming down washing it out. Rude is setting your launch in circle where another modeler's plane is coming down or just about ready too launch. Rude is running, fast walks instead of walking slowly. Basically rude is disturbing air of another modeler's plane or distracting the flyer during process of launching. If everyone took their winding and hook up on floor at World Champs I have attended there would have been plenty of time to do it in an orderly respectful fashion.
