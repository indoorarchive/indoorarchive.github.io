A year or so ago I bought 200 of the 1/2 oz bottles below. I think they
were around $.30 each then. Right now they are on sale for $.20 each
including flat caps. This company doesn't seem to have the caps that
accept needles but caps that I got with other bottles will work on
these bottles. I've used them for Duco, acetone, and CA as well as a
handy place to store bearings, Teflon washers, o-rings, etc. I've even
cut the bottoms off of them to use for small epoxy mixing cups.
