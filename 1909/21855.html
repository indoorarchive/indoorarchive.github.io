The National Free Flight Society is soliciting nominations for the
Presidency and Central District Vice Presidency. Elections will take
place via printed and electronic ballot in December 2019. The President
and Central VP assume their respective offices on 1/1/20 for terms of
four years.

Any NFFS member may run for President. For Central VP, candidates must
reside in South Dakota, Colorado, Nebraska, Kansas, New Mexico,
Oklahoma, Iowa, Wyoming, Minnesota, North Dakota or Texas.

If you are interested in either position please contact NFFS President
John Lorbiecki at <lorbieckie_at_sbcglobal.net>. Self-nominations are
acceptable. Candidates who wish to have 200-word campaign statements
published in NFFS Digest prior to the election must submit them to me on
or before 10/1/19.


Thanks and Thermals,

Don DeLoach, NFFS Editor and Election Judge
