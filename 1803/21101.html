Tea at West Baden!


The West Baden (and French Lick Resort) holds an afternoon tea event on
Saturdays in the spring and we are reserving seats for the Indoor Free
Flight World Championship group for Saturday, March 17th. This is not an
official event for the World Championships, but it is an activity that the
hotel hosts that our guests might enjoy. We currently have reserved a block
of seats for the 3pm tea, but we can divide our group between the 3pm and
2pm slots if that proves to be more convenient. The tea service features
finger sandwiches and small desserts, as well as a variety of teas. The
cost is $28 USD per person + gratuity.
