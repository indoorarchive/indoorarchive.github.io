The official results of the 2018 Jim Richmond Open have been posted on the AMA's 2018 Indoor World Championship website at:
 

 http://www.modelaircraft.org/files/events/jro2018_results_all.pdf http://www.modelaircraft.org/files/events/jro2018_results_all.pdf

 

 Although times for 41 pilots are shown, there were well over 50 entries, as some pilots did not post times. This was a large number of F1D pilots. The longest official time was Brett Sanborn's 26:49, one second more than Corneliu Mangalea's 26:48.
