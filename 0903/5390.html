Symes site, it's made from foam grocery trays and flys nicely.

If you want to get into simple stick and tissue, I can sell you some
Dennydart kits, easy and dependable flyers.

I may have posted some hints on making "pigtails" in the photo's
section, don't remember, but the way I do it isn't hard and only needs a
pair of pliers, cutters and grinder.
