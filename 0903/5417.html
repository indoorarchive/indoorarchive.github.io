I agree with everything Larry had to say about the PPCC. Many thanks to Don Deloach for CD'ing a great meet and also many thanks to the MMM club members who came to officiate and time flights. This is the only place I've ever flown where you can walk out on the floor with a wound model at any time and have someone ready and willing to time you.

The glider guys in Colorado Springs are just tremendous and there are a LOT of them! In the Atlanta area I've almost never had anyone to fly against in HLG. At the PPCC I had a couple of everything I have left in my arm throws with decent results (all things considered) and wound up in SEVENTH place.

I hope that the PPCC can become a permanent event for the indoor community.
