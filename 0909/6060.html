Guys,

Keep in mind that the intent of this event is to expose newcomers who might otherwise be too intimidated / busy (in addition to proving your indoorist mettle by winning via proxy).

Start spreading the word to anyone who has seen or heard about your planes, but hasn't taken the leap yet. I didn't want to start the push too early, lest the enthusiasm wane before the event. But it is getting about time.

Good targets might include: scale FF guys, r/c pals, glider flyers, sailboaters, Science Olympiad grads, and enthusiasts of any other oddball discipline.
