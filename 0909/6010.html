Ebay. I bought a pretty cheap lifetime supply about 8 years ago. But after anything catches on, the price goes up, of course. I ended up with various brands frome .003" up to .007" thick, and all seem useful for different tasks. Hadn't heard SS blades aren't being made, if that's what you're saying.

BTW, .005" Gillette Blues are pretty common, and pretty useful indoor. The .007 Star is great for balsa strippers, very stiff. You can also stiffen modern stainless steel blades with a thin sliver of bass or heavy balsa, just above the edge. Only a few applications where that isn't suitable.
