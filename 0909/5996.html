Use "foam safe" CA, or UHU, or epoxy. If you use foam safe CA you may want to have the foam safe CA excellerator as the CA dries real slow on foam. UHU is a strong fexible contact glue. Epoxy is just that, epoxy...rather heavy, but very strong. You can email me directly if you wish, I do alot of foam!
