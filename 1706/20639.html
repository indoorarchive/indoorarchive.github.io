Tem was a great guy. Always greeted me with a smile and had time to chat about gliders. He really loved indoor HLG. The day after hand launch he always came over and asked me why my right arm was so much longer and we would share laugh. He also sent me a nice packet about the history of indoor HLG with many hand written notes that made it clear he was an engineer and probably did quite a bit of drawing on a manual board over the years...his lettering was perfect.

 I will miss him, he was one of the many good guys, that we are losing all to soon these day.
 ~Jim Buxton
