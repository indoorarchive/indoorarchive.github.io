I use this size mostly now. Both are for prop spars and are single wall.
One is for VP prop hubs. The other is for hubs for LPP or F1L or such. Anything where I can insert the prop spar and glue it in place. If I need to adjust it, it is far easier to do so than with tissue tubes.

The one thing about it is that you need to coat the inside with some thin CA in order for the Duco or Ambroid to have something to stick to.
