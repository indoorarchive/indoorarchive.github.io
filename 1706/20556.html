 Rantoul contest, May 19/20/21st
  
 Upon arrival on Friday, there was a large storm blowing through the area. The hangar doors were rattling loudly. Most of the insulation that was stuffed into the gaps in the doors had fallen, the hangar was somewhat dirtier than we left it, however all the rest of the improvements were still in place.
 Rain continued through Friday and part of Saturday. Around noon on Saturday, the weather cleared up. Flying in the afternoon was nice with drift only right by the ceiling. Sunday was more of the same, with great flying weather inside despite the 15-20MPH winds outside.
