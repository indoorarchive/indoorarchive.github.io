Top 10 Reasons to Fly at Tustin in December

10. Sunny southern California. Beach, Mexican food, and Disneyland.

9. 5 days of indoor flying at a world class indoor flying site. 155' to the ceiling. Floor is HUGE and completely clean and unobstructed.

8. Home to countless "hall of famers" in indoor flying: Steve Brown, Bob Randolph, Cezar Banks, Larry Cailliau, Ron Whittman, Lee Hines, Stan Buddenbohm

7. The air in winter is very good. Not as good as summer, but Tustin in winter still yields longer flight times than Kibbie Dome and Johnson City

6. Challenge Kang Lee in F1L and try to beat his butt.

5. Do the same to Leo Pilachowski in LPP.

4. Come and challenge Gary Hodson in A6 (if he comes :-) )

3. Fly with Steve Brown.

2. Come set a record

1. Watch Stan Buddenbohm set a new HLG record
