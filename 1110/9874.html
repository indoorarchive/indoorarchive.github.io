I've uploaded two tables of 30-minute EZB flights. The link is here:

http://www.hippocketaeronautics.com/downloads/EZB_30_minute_flights.pdf

The first table contains all known 30-minute EZB flights, and the second table lists only those flights at USIC, Johnson City. Those who have flown 30 minutes at USIC deserve special recognition.

Official flights are those flown at sanctioned contests and record trials, and timed by one or two timers other than the flier. Unofficial flights are listed, however, but are shown below the official ones.

I've included additional information for each flight: model weight, prop specification, motor used. A lot of the information have not been previously published. Permission to post all information have been granted. I hope this information is useful to the indoor community.

Recall the Forty Minute Club list that Steve Brown has kept. In that list, only the best flight of a flier is itemized. That's the big difference with this list -- in this list, all 30-minute flights are captured. For now, it is still a manageable short list. Hopefully, more fliers achieve 30-minutes in EZB, and we one day list only each flier's best EZB flight.

Hope this helps. Please let me know if you have any questions.
