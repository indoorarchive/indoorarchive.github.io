Hi all

A team trial for Australian team selection for the 2012 F1D W/Champs will be held in Melbourne at the Manningham Indoor Sports Centre, Donvale this year on 18 Dec.

The site is a Cat 1 hall and the trial is being run in conjunction with the F1D and F1L Victorian State Championships, by the Victorian Free Flight Society (VFFS). Practice will be from 9.30am to 11.00am. Competition flights will be taken from 11.00am to 4.30pm. Entry fee is $10.00 plus a fee of $7.00 per event entered.

If there is anyone from OZ on this list who would like more info contact Contest Director Darien Cassidy via the VFFS (http://members.optushome.com.au/vffs/) or I can provide his contact details.

If Australia sends representation to the 2012 W/Chs it will be the first time since Felstead, Blackam and Kerr went to Nagoya in 1984!

Cheers
Tim
