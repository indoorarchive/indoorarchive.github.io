Services for Doc will be Wednesday December 12 at 11:00 at The Christian
Assembly, 25595 Chardon Road, Richmond Heights 44143
(216-261-4214)….if coming from the east it is on Chardon Road past

probably be packed.

Donations should be sent to

Dr. Vern Hacker Scholarship Fund

c/o National City Bank

117 South Street

Chardon, Ohio 44024



The hopes of this fund will be to award scholarships to the National
