What a privilege it has been to have known Vernon. He was a sweet and
kind, true gentleman…who gave so much to the students of whom he
mentored over the years! Building and flying will never be the same
without his emails of advice, encouragement and even amusement. I hope
he now knows how many people he has touched the lives of and how much he
has meant to all those people.



Vernon always had a positive attitude, even when the going got
rough...whether it was flying related or in real life. My daughter
advised me today that Hack could now watch her and her brother fly
anytime he wanted. I'd like to think he'll be watching over them, as
well as others, still enjoying his passion for flight.



With very sad hearts…our family will miss Vernon's friendship
terribly!

Hugs and God Speed Hack!



Lori, Jon, Ashby and Kelsey

Daugherty
