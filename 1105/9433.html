I saw this on hippocket - from Wout.

At the IIFI, the 21st and 22nd May Nijmegen, a new world record has been set for F1D models! Lutz Schramm (Germany) has made a flight of 32 minutes and 7 seconds, which is a new record for halls of less than 8 metres height. Before this record is official the FAI will have to investigate and confirm this achievement.
The previous record of 31:13 was from 2006, also by Lutz Schramm.

Take a look at the picture. Gone is the elliptical wing dihedral.
