FWIW, a good way to scrape props is to use a "furniture" scraper, not a
razor blade or knife. It's a thin steel plate with the edge rolled into
a "cutting" shape. Works great, cuts fast and can be made with a curve
if you want a cambered blade.

Probably find directions by googling.
