You can click on either of two new apps in the left column of the web message board. One is "RSVP" which may prove no better than posting the usual "roll call" about any upcoming contest.

But the other app may prove useful--a members map. Please add yourself to the map--it will only require filling in City, State, Country, and whatever name you wish to use. I was hoping for a world map, but it defaults to regional maps. Click on box containing person's name and data, and that should take you to a map with lil' flags stuck in it showing member'a locations per city. Don't click on the individual city as appears data banner or that will just take you to regular Yahoo Maps for that city.

Give it a try, please, if you are willing to let other members know where you are in the world.
