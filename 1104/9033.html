I am fully aware of the AMA rules in regards to the CD not being able to time
that is the reason for getting more alternate CDs. Due to the limited number of
people more than just one alternate CD can be added to the existing sanction so
that if the designated CD is going to fly or wants to time then he can designate
another to take his place while he is flying or timing, however the person they
designate must also be listed as an alternated CD on the sanction.  That is why
the more CDs the better. This is the only way to "pass the CD hat" when the
people trying for the records are also the only CDs available.
