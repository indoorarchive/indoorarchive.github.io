On 4/2/2011 3:54 PM, Phedon Tsiknopoulos wrote:
> A CD cannot time. All his timed records will be thrown out, that is if
> he is the one that applied for the sanction. Plainly stated only CDs
> who have nothing top do with the sanction can time. You still need a
> RECORDS SANCTION from the AMA. The RECORDS SANCTION CD can time only
> if he has stated beforehand in the sanction papers that he wishes to
> time in which case there must be a second CD available. Bothe names
> must be stated. Ciao, Phedon.

I am fully aware of the AMA rules in regards to the CD not being able to
time that is the reason for getting more alternate CDs. Due to the
limited number of people more than just one alternate CD can be added to
the existing sanction so that if the designated CD is going to fly or
wants to time then he can designate another to take his place while he
is flying or timing, however the person they designate must also be
listed as an alternated CD on the sanction. That is why the more CDs
the better. This is the only way to "pass the CD hat" when the people
trying for the records are also the only CDs available.
