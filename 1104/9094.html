So now the Wally Miller one-design min weight it 1.7 grams?

Per http://www.modelaircraft.org/files/2011IndoorFFNFFSEventsRules.pdf:

Wally Miller One Design Early EZ-B Event
1.Model must be built to specifications, to include wood sizes, as shown on plans published in Indoor News and Views Copy #125. For plans contact Abram VanDover
2.Covering must be mylar.
3.Rubber motor to be TAN II
4.Weight will be 1.7 grams
5.Event Director has final word.
