rule goes down narrowly in defeat 45% to 55%.

 

The proposal will go to the Contest Board reflecting these parameters.

 

--Don D.

 

P.S. Only one of the respondents (that I know of-A. Danjo) is not an AMA
member. He voted yes on 50% and no on plastic.
