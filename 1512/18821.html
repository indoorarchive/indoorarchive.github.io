Star have been my favorite, fortunately I found a new box of 100 on ebay
about a three weeks ago and I snagged it. Luckily all blades are in good
shape and not rusty. I had lots of rusty NIB star blades off ebay over
the years. Glad to hear something is available. I have so many brands I
have tried looking a good blade to compare to the star. The closest I
have come is Gilere Carbon Steel Blue, they are about .0045" and will
snap pretty clean if you use needle nose pliers.

Don
