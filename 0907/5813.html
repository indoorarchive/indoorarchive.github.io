The following is a letter sent to AMA prez, Dave Mathewson accompanying the donation from the 2009 USIC / AMA Nats raffle proceeds:

July 6, 2009

Dave Mathewson
AMA President
5161 E. Memorial Dr.
Muncie IN 47302

Dear Dave:

During the recent 2009 Indoor Free Flight Nats, we once again held a raffle using products and funds donated by Tim Goldstein of A2Z Corp., Roy White of Y2K Films, Ray Harlan of Indoor Specialties, Jeff Hood, Tony Pavel, and various other competitors.

In demonstration of our commitment to the Nats, and Indoor Free Flight in general, over a thousand dollars of proceeds were collected and have been allocated as a donation to support the event. Please find a check for $1,100 enclosed.

Thank you for your continued support of Indoor Free Flight. It has a long history and many current enthusiastic participants. We are also actively working on ways to bring new people into the fold – keep an eye out for the "Erv Rodemsky Limited Pennyplane Pro / Am" we are developing for the 2010 Indoor FF Nats!

Sincerely,
John Kagan,
and the Indoor Free Flight Community
