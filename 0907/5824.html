I use a mylar balloon that is 1meter in diameter. My fishing reel weighs about 625grammes and just rests on the ground when the balloon is inflated. This is the minimum lift I would use for a category 4 ceiling.

The length of the rod can be shorter for lower ceilings but requires still requires good judgment of the height of the model relative to the balloon.
