takes about a few degrees positive in the front canard and the back

the cg has to be way father towards the front canard than you would
first expect. Oh your plane should be turning left i think if you are
just reversing one of the standard ikera props. Jury is still out on
if it is truly worth the 30% bonus in a low ceiling. Hope to see some
of you at nats.
