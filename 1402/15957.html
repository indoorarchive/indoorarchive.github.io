Just read with interest Bob Brown's latest salvo in his war on FAI competition in the most recent Model Aviation.

 - Cut FAI team funding
 - Initiated processes to eliminate teams that fall below increasingly challenging performance and participation levels
 - And now, is pressuring the FAI to "sunset" events "of little participation outside of Europe"
 

 I wonder which classes he is talking about.
