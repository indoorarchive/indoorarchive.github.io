Greetings.

This fiber has been sold out at all carbon fiber suppliers for six months. Every one of them has no idea when they will get it back in stock.

As I have been learning how to mold the prop blades, I have gone through most of the fiber I had gotten previously.

I went back to the source company and spoke to the US Sales rep, a very nice Mr. Suzuki, who informed me that they do not supply carry the fiber tow in the US. I would have to order from Japan directly, with a 5kg. minimum order. The cost could buy me a cheap new car.

Mr. Suzuki was sympathetic to me and put me in touch with two companies that recently imported the tow, one a prepreger, one a weaver. The prepreger never responded to my inquiries. The weaver did.

We've been going back and forth and he is offering a minimum way under his normal 'minimum order'.

This is three yards of fabric, 19x19 weave, 42" wide at a total cost of $1000 plus shipping.

Another US flier (Kang Lee) and I are willing to put up the cash to purchase it as long as we can get enough interested buyers.
If my math is correct, $50 would get ~100 pieces 42" long plus ~35 or so 3 yards long. (That is a LOT of carbon!)

We would need at least 10 people interested.

Please contact Kang or myself directly.
