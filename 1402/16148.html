I think it has become generally accepted to do whatever gets you the highest time on the stopwatch.

My best LPP times so far use helical pitch props at 24" pitch.
F1D I've done well with the Treger pitch blocks.

I think there is a lot of room left for experimentation.
i.e. Make the same outline and size, but try 26"/31"/35" pitch distributions and see what works best for you.
