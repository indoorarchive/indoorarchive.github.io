I see some innovation in the last 14 years, but I wouldn't attribute any of
it to the rubber limit. I would also point out that Steve did 50 minutes
at the Kibbie Dome with 8/93 rubber. I think the best 55cm fight at Kibbie
is 38-39, and it was done with 5/99.

As for longing for the days of yore, all I can say is HL stick is looking
more and more interesting to me. I would say the opposite about F1D right
now.
