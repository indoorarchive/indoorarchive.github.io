It is with great sadness that I post to the list that my father passed
away on Monday Morning. Many of you knew my dad as my constant travel
campanion to every indoor contest since 1987.

He battled Parkinsons Disease very successfully for almost 20 years,
and attended his last contest in Kent Ohio last March. He began
having serious Parkinsons complications this August, and passed
peacefully with his family at his side.

I have had several E-mails from people about different matters in the
past week. I will return those E-mails and publish the final National
Cup points next week sometime.

Thank you,
