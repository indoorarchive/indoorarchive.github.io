 To keep a prop in re pitch make a cut on top and bottom of spars.Cut
depth 1/4 to 1/3 though spar. Then re pitch, fill cuts with Ambroid it
has held up fairly well for us and can be dissolved with actone for
pitching adjustments. Once your certain of pitch desired or making
several props with various pitches CA glue has proved not to vary over
time.
