I just built a "super simple" model to SO specs, see if my students
can get it to fly dependably. They seem to be having trouble
understanding effects of decalage. Fixed 2 degree negative stab
incidence, zero wing incidence with a slidable wing to adjust cg. (no
wing posts)
General format is same as Dennydart mark IV - I do like rounded
laminated tips - they seem to live longer where we fly. Be
interesting to see what happens tuesday.
