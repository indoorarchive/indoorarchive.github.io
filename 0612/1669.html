David,
" Has anyone else seen this problem with repitched plastic props? If so, any
suggestions as how to prevent it?"
 It seems the closer you get the plastic to the 265-275 degree temperature
that Ray Harlan quoted in his post, the better the plastic stays where you
want it. Harbor Freight has a IR thermometer which was on sale for about
$10. We use it to monitor the plastic temperature. If you start to
experiment with these temperatures, make sure you have spare props at hand.
