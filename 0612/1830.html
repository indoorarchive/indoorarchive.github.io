I just joined the group so I guess I'll introduce myself. My name is
Jake Palmer and I got started in indoor about 15 years ago. I saw
pictures of F1D's in Model Aviation in the early 90's and had to find
out more. I eventually contacted Ed Whitten who then put me in touch
with Andy Tagliafico. From there I got hooked and even worked for Lew
Gitlow for a number of years.

For several years now all my stuff has been in storage but now I have
some space to build. So I'm just here to see what's new in the indoor
world and see if I can figure out these 55cm F1D's (I actually
competed as a junior in the '96 world champs with the 65cm models).

Regards,
Jake
