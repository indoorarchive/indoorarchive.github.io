I realize that there may be some people on the list that get tired of
seeing my name pop up. My only motive in posting as much as I do is to
try to pass on to newcomers things that have been taught to me by the
people I consider the greats in our sport. These lessons have come from
in person teaching, from personal observation and from reading. If I'm
trying to foist off on others an actual original idea, I try to
identify it as such as a way of cautioning the reader that he/she needs
to do some critical thinking about whether they want to believe it or
not.

So my apologies to those who don't need to hear all the basics
discussed over and over. And to the peolple trying to learn, keep on
asking and I'll keep on answering as long as I know the answer or can
point you to someone who does.
