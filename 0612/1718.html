Fred Rash has spent several years perfecting an Excel program for the
design of cone or can props. It makes the whole process very easy and
gives you the actual pitches at various stations along the prop blade.
You can use a cone or can that you have on hand or you can design your
own. It's called Bucket68.xls and it's in the Files section.
