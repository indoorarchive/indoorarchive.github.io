The results are all in for this years National Cup Season.

We had 37 glider flingers and 59 gumband twisters competing in the
Indoor National Cup events this year.

We also increased the number of events, with 10 regional contest in
addition to the two exempt contests of USIC and Kibbie.

Be sure to get your contest on the 2007 calendar soon!
