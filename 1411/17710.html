Greetings Free Flighters,


Please note that the National Free Flight Society (NFFS) Western
District Vice Presidency has a vacancy for which we are commencing an
election today. Ballots (attached) must be postmarked by 12/31/14, or
you can vote online at the following link:


https://www.surveymonkey.com/s/W828KGT


Vote only if you are a current NFFS member living in Alaska, Idaho,
Utah, Arizona, Montana, Washington, California, Nevada, Hawaii or Oregon.


Not a NFFS member (tsk, tsk)? First-time members join for half price at
www.freeflight.org. With membership comes a full-color magazine, 240
pages per year, devoted 100% to Free Flight.


Thermals,
