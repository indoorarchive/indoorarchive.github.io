I just drew up a sketch of the wing and stab, printed them out and build
using 1/6" medium weight balsa and tissue covering, motor stick is 12"
of 1/8" x 3/8" 10# with a 1/8" sq tailboom, prop is a bucket prop (if
you sue a regular plastic prop shouldn't be any problem making weight
:-\ . I'd make the wing adjustable on the motor stick ( look at
Dennydart plan) so you can play with adjustment. Use a pigtail prop
hanger, easy to make and lets you change props and thrust angles.

enjoy, wombat
