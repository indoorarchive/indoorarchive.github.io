Got my more or less SO model up to test stage, hat has to be the ugliest
plane I've ever built:-P . About max size, weighs in at 8 gms, flies
across living room kinda decent, maybe get a chance to try in gym next
week. Be interesting as there are ceiling fans that blow Dennydarts
around, probably blow the wing off if it gets directly under one at
ceiling height :-D .

Prop is can formed blades mounted to a bit of 1/4' sq balsa, blades set
at 45 deg angle at hub - about 1" x 4" paddles. Gonna start with 1 gm of
3/32 SS.
