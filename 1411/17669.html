Google has finally signed a 60 year lease with NASA for Moffett that includes rehabilitation of the hangars. It sounds like they have plans to use the hangars for research and development which might not bode well for access to the sites for flying. It would be unfortunate if Hangar One was forever lost (or at least for 60 years).
