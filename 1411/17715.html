Where do the rules require a "non transparent" area and "opaque patch"on
the covering for the bonus? The rules I have read as follows:

"10% of the flight time will be added to flight time of the airplane
that has a colored panel on the wing that is at least the length of the
wing chord and at least between 2 wing ribs."


Additionally how are Ikara props allowed because rule 3a prohibits
pre-glued joints. Ikara props are preglued from plastic pieces. Rule 3e
does say props assemblies may be bought pre-assembled but that to me
means rules 3a still applies to the purchased assembly. So a one piece
molded plastic prop can come preasembled with a prop shaft and nose
bearing, like the Sig 7" prop with nose piece.
