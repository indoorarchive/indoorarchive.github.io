Ninth annual Pikes Peak Ceiling Climb will be Sunday, March 8, 2015. Cat
II, 36 feet, Colorado Springs Auditorium.


This is the site of Bill Gowen's epic 49-second SCLG flight in 2011.


Can you set records here? Yes, site records are kept for all events,
since 2001. And they are very difficult to top. An elite group of about
five guys has ever topped 20 minutes at our 6100 foot elevation.


Air is very stable and Colorado is beautiful in the springtime.


Please support our contest. Flyer coming soon


DD
