Attached is a photo of the Freedom Flight kits that six of the students I am coaching built last weekend. The kits are excellent and include a 30-page instruction book with detailed description (and pictures) of construction steps along with details regarding adjusting, trimming, aerodynamics and modification. 17' of three thicknesses of Tan SS are included along with every thing else you need except a building board and glue. The kits constructed to about 6.8 grams which allowed positioning clay for a good starting point CG and SSM. The test version of this kit that I constructed easily did three minutes using only about 20' of a 23.5' ceiling site (stock propeller). Sorry about the picture quality from my phone.
