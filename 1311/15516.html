 Midwest Basswood is often sold in art stores (even here in Malaysia, I've found it next to the balsa and plywood in art stores). Problem is you still have to strip/sand it to size, I suppose. If you're looking for another wood heavier than balsa there's also Paulownia - Daiso (Japanese "100 Yen" store chain - they're all over the world now) carries sheets of it.
