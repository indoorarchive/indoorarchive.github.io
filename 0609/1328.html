On Thu, 21 Sep 2006, Nick Aikman wrote:

> We're all building 'Russian doll' type card/foam/bubble wrap
> contraptions, fully expecting to go in the hold - we also looked into
> driving and going by train but both are impractical.

Styrofoam makes good protective cases. I've used a box out of 25mm (1 in)
blue foam around my F1B case for several years while flying. It has taken
quite a few dints, but the box inside and the models have survived. Does
not weight too much, just around 2kg with corners taped in silver tape. It
makes the box quite a lot bigger, though.
