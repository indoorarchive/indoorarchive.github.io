We held an official cat4 record trial here in Japan today. It was held at
Kumagaya Dome. The ceiling height is 33.5 meters.
M, Ishii did 1min 30.7sec (= 90.7 sec) in F1N.
I did 20min 25sec in F1M.
It was very very difficult condition because of terrible drifts and
turburence, especialy near the ceiling. My model climbed only 25m or so when
did the best time.

Good flying, Aki
