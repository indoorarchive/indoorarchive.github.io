Art,

My guess is for $4 you are using first class parcel from USPS. For the 8
years I ran my RC website USPS Priority mail had very few problems
compared to USPS first class parcels. Eventually I stopped offering
first class parcel and went to Priority Mail only. Just passing my
experience along.
