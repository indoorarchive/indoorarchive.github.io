At the World Champs last March, I found a wrapper from an Accusplit A601XBU. On the front it said Quartz 0.04 sec/hr. On the back it said Quartz Crystal Accuracy 0.1 sec/hr.
 At the WCh, the issue of accuracy was brought up and Andras Ree, a member of the FAI Jury, said he had never seen an electronic watch that wasn't accurate enough for timing indoor FF flights. A timers' watch calibration was immediately scrapped!
