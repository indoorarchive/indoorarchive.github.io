Best to have the rubber broken in at least a day or two ahead of time.
I like to have 10 motors that are the same that I can cycle through, giving them time to rest.
If you push the same band to >90% max on successive winds without rest, the band will break sooner and suffer from some power loss. Some with running a band that has not been broken in.
