 The tea is a British style, afternoon, tea that the hotel runs on weekends. Last year the tea reservations were all filled up so the "others" went for a lunch at one of the hotels eateries. The fliers are flying (prime flying time!) so the past tea and lunches have been for the significant others, etc. In the past years, the tea (or lunch) has been an opportunity for the "others" to mingle all together and for a break from the action (or lack thereof) on the flying floor.
 

 The tea reservations are needs to be made in advance. The lunch is not so much of a concern as there are several restaurants and eateries in the hotel.
