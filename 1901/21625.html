Don,

I'm not condoning Leo going around you and the organizing committee.
However, the fact that he did it is an indication that he didn't feel
heard. When people don't feel heard and respected, they don't show up to
contests. I'm sure he was in similar situations at various points while he
helped organize the World Champs, although he never publicly threatened to
ban anyone for talking to the hotel, jury, or sponsors.

As an AMA AVP, I'm concerned the Round Valley Dome event is going to be a
regional contest, even if it is well attended by locals. Based on the posts
here from several top level competitors, I think that is a valid concern.
Kibbie has a better track record of drawing high quality indoor competitors
from around the country. Back in the days of Johnson City, the best
competitors in the country went head to head each year. I don't feel like
we've had that level of participation in several years. I think a key
ingredient for that is a world class flying site, with ample time to
compete, that leads those most involved with our hobby to drop what they
are doing and come. So, I'm simply asking whether or not we have gotten to
a point where the needs of the sport fliers, and the needs of the "Indoor
Elitists" are too far apart to be met by the same venue. If that is where
we are, let's own it and move forward while there is still plenty of time.
I'd rather see both groups separate than continue watching this game of who
can boycott the other's event most thoroughly.

Best regards,

Nick Ray
