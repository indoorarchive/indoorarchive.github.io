Further information for your consideration:

At this point we are proceeding on the assumption that night flying will
not be possible. The school district is not willing to turn on the
lights, and we are respecting that answer. We are investigating
alternative lighting options, but we are not sure we can get enough
floodlights, spotlights, and extension cords to illuminate this huge
site adequately for a National Championship, not to mention the
complexity and expense involved with alternate lighting. We intend to
test a few lights at the April RV Dome meet, but no promises.

-- DD, for the Nats Committee

> Dear Indoor Nats survey respondents,
>
> In response to a few complaints your Indoor Nats committee is
> considering minor changes to the flying schedule.
>
> See attached for the schedule as it currently stands. For reference
> daylight hours in Eagar in late May are approximately 6am-8pm. Currently
> there are three late flying nights scheduled until 10 pm which is about
> 2 hours after twilight.
>
> The school district has said we cannot use the stadium lights. Should we
> end flying at dusk on 6/30-6/1 and add yellow hours elsewhere? Should we
> stick with night flying and extend the yellow and blue windows even
> later, like midnight or 1 am? This is entirely possible since we will
> have the keys to the Dome. Should we add a few hours of official flying
> to the afternoon of 6/29? That is also our entirely possible, but the
> Dome won't be available prior to 6/29 at 4pm. More generally, does the
> allocation of flying time for each color look about right given the
> responses to our Fall 2018 event survey (note the number in parentheses
> next to each event)?
>
> Now, we need your well-reasoned feedback. The public comment period open
> from 1/19 and lasts until through Saturday 1/26. This is your chance to
> make your feelings known. Comments must be in writing and addressed to
> any of the committee members (listed below). Commentary on social media
> or email lists will not be considered. Your comments must be addressed
> to a committee member privately or as a group. The Committee will
> discuss all feedback received the week of 1/27 and announce any schedule
> changes.
