Hi all,

Here are some comments from Canada:

Order of the events:
Jake brought up important point - steering!
All the long endurance classes are scheduled for late afternoon - evening.
Thus, steering and handling of delicate models would be quite challenging.
At the same time, events where flight time is measured in seconds and no
steering required got early mornings and mid-day time.

Time allocated:
There are 4 entries in Electric (average time would be about 5 min) got 13
hrs of flight time in day 3 and 4.
The 13 entries in F1D (average time would be over 20 min) got only 12 hrs
total.
FAC scale, 11 entries got 5+5+8+6 = 24 hrs total!

Myself, and at least one more guy (Hi, Jo!) will fly F1D, HLS, Orni, and
Heli. It will be hard to do well in all of these in just 12 hrs.

So, if my opinion counts, I propose:
1. Extend yellow section to Saturday and Saturday.
2. Reorder time slots through the days to allow every group of fliers to
get fair share of mornings, mid-day lights, and darker evenings.

Cheers,
=Bill

P.S. I am coming anyway!
