I wanted to notify everyone of a major security concern on the new NFFS
website. For some reason they didn't implement a security certificate or
any encryption for the new store. If you enter a credit card number on the
site, you risk having that information stolen. I've notified the webmaster
of this massive oversight, but I would strongly urge people not to order
with a credit card from NFFS until this is fixed.

As a side note, they've also been unable to process my PayPal payment, so
the only safe method of payment right now is mailing a check.
