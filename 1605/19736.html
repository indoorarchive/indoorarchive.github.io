I posted this earlier on the USAF1D group. I'm re-posting it here for the more general audience.
 

 ========
 Since the Team Selection contest is likely to be at the Kibbie Dome next summer, I hope many of the team selection competitors are making the trip this summer.
 

 To further add to your F1D experience this year, we're thinking of adding a couple of clinics on F1D. The first is a clinic/seminar on how Brett makes his carbon props. Brett has written extensively on this, but you might want to see and hear directly from the source (Brett). The second is a "hands-on" flying clinic on VP flying and adjustments, aimed for all beginner to advanced fliers. This could directly help you with your rubber and VP settings for the team selection contest.
 

 We would love to hear some feedback on what would interest you most. If not these topics, please suggest others that might be more attractive.
 

 
 -Kang
