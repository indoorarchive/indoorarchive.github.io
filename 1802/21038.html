As of today, AMA event 228 (F1N) is now adopted by the AMA and can be
flown for USA national records. Please READ the FAI rules on scoring and
timing for F1N as it is not the same as other USA glider events. I have
been informed that the event will be listed on the AMA online sanction
portal in about one week.
