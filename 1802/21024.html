> There appears to be some confusion with respect to the space for the FAI
> unique ID numbers on the entry form for the 2018 Indoor World Championship
> at West Baden. The FAI/CIAM instituted an off cycle rules change that took
> effect on January 1, 2018. That rule change added the following to section
> 3.4.2 ofthe F1D rules (actually to the rules for all FAI indoor free flight
> classes):
>
> "The model shall carry the FAI unique ID number of the competitor on the
> motorstick written with permanent marker or other non-removable means."
