I regret to inform the indoor community that one of the great indoor
flyers, Lt. Col. Bob Randolph, died on February 2nd. Bob was a member
of numerous US F1d teams of the 1980's and 1990's, a team manager and
at one time holder of the absolute world records for Hand Launched
Stick in all four ceiling categories at the same time.

Bob was a true friend and he will be missed.
