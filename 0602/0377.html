Those of us who attend the USIC each year know how much fun the event is, so
I am curious why so few scale flyers attend.
Do most of you fly outdoor only?
Do those of you that fly indoor avoid the USIC for some reason?
Perhaps you think because it is a contest that it is too serious & therefore
not fun?
The FAC biannual event in New York is also a contest, yet it is fun & well
attended.
The USIC (Johnson City, TN) is May 31 thru June 4, with the scale events on
June 1. 2 & 3. It is a great site for scale flying. Something like 116' high!
Give some thought to attending this year or offer some suggestions on what
might inspire you to attend.
Thanks,
