Just a reminder that one of the first NFFS National Cup contests of 2006 is
coming up in Colorado Springs. The contest is at a nice Cat II site , 37 ft
smooth tiled ceiling. All NC events-rubber and glider-will be featured.

 

For those who think indoor models don't fly well at 6000' altitude check
this out. At our last meet Bill Leppard did 10:42 with his tandem
Pennyplane. And John Kagan has done over 15:00 with his F1D.

 

E-mail me off list for a flyer. COS airport is only 20 minutes away and MMM
members are happy to provide transportation to and from the site/hotel.
