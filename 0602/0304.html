I use the cheapest jap tissue I can find, reusing it as long as
physically possible. The more wear, and loss of binders, the less
adhesion to very flimsy balsa and easier removal - I precut and lap my
joints a few thousandths. For heavy stuff like F1d, it's disposable
domestic tissue, or first runs jap. The cost of gampi has never made it
a consideration for such waste.

Bruce in Seattle
