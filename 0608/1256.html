Everyone may already know this, but I felt the need to solidify my
status as a building nerd. Tonight I spilled indoor glue (Amboride,
DOPE ect) on a new wing that I had just finished Covering with OS
film. As the Glue contracted it wrecked the wing, so I was getting
ready to toss it into the trash when I decided to play with it, I took
a low density balsa stick and started gently poking the film trying to
make it vibrate, After about 30seconds of this the glue popped off and
the wing was good as new.
