Ikara also has a similar prop hanger, but made of a little harder
plastic and therefore more sturdy. The only source that I've found is a
small spanish home-entrepreneur selling CL (!) and Ikara stuff. See
VLAI008 at this page: http://www.jpaeromodels.com/en/ikara/02.php

We have a national beginners class quite similar to SO, where many
people use Ikara props. They are not mandatory, but as their performance
is almost similar to balsa props, they are quite popular. For the
laser-cut kit that I provide
[http://www.kotikone.fi/tapio.linkosalo/tintti/DSCN1090_small.jpg], I
also add the plastic prop with the VLAI008 hanger, and instruct people
to replace the flimsy original prop hanger with the sturdier one.
