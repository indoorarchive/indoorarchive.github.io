10/97 is a tough and torquefull one and I like it. It has very good cruising
torque and works well for low ceiling flights.
My ministick have done some 11 mins+ flights at a 9m ceiling gym and 8 mins+
flight at 3m ceiling room with it. My F1L did 21 mins+ at a 18.8m gym and
the flight was a previous cat3 world record.
I do not have 7/99.
Good flying, Aki
