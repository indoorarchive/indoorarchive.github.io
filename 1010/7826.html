I don't fly minis or EZBs as a rule but one of the principal design priorities for the meter has been to make it usable for any indoor duration model. This has also been the toughest to solve but I believe that the meter will be accurate down to .01 in-oz or less. Testing for this capability is not easy. I'm offering the meters with a satisfaction guaranteed warranty so that if it doesn't suit your purpose you can return it.

Also for anyone interested, these meters are not going to be a work of art like something Ray Harlan would make. My primary goal is function.
