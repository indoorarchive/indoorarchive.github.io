The www.indoornewsandviews.com website has all the issues through 123, and in
color. These can be saved and printed.


Van Dover came out with a 10 page Summer Issue 124, postmarked July 2010 with
the USIC results.
Then there is a 10 page Fall Issue 125, which came around September, with the
Wally Miller EZB, Lou Young's Crooked Penny, and the FAI World Champ results.

I have scanned them in Adobe PDF format, and will send to Tapio, and anyone else
who contacts me off-list. I do not believe Van Dover has a scanner, but he lists
me as a co-editor, so I ought to be doing something for the honor. BTW, he has
implemented something we discussed two or three USIC's ago, that is, to put out
regular issues, no matter how small or large, rather than waiting for material
to accumulate for a full 40 pages. Often it never does.
