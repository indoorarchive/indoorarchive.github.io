First of all, let me introduce myself, my name Dwidahana, from Jakarta - Indonesia. A long time ago when i was kid, the aeromodelling i now is just control line, OHLG n R/C. Internet getting through my country about mid '90. My dream come true because I can find a lot of knowledge about building the models.

On that era, the people who works in there, are very special, instead there is one thing fundamental they forgot to do.
They never build enthusiasm, the kids, young generations, to play and introduce about aeroplane.
is that related ?
Yes, Rick told us, in his letter. We forgot to carry the parents, when we introduce the kids to play with. Without the parents involved, they never know about they kids dreams. Because they only heard what they child do. They never feels the emotions.

For right now, It's only me in Jakarta still build n play rubber power.
The others more attract to R/C. But I still believe the rubber is still better in my perspective.
So, we better join to do what we believe ?
