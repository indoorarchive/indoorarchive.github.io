Thanks, Tim san,
'10 F1D Nats was held at Matsumoto Dome, 34m ceiling, on 3 Oct.
It was cloudy and windy outdoor, and room temp was 23 - 24 degC.
At the 1st and 2nd round, there were heavy drifts and it was impossible to
get good time without balloon steering.
I did not have a proxy steerer and did not steer at all.
From 3rd round, the air became better and at 5&6 round the air was good.
I lost my #1 model at the 1st round on the dome side wall, so I had to use
#2 model.
The result was
1, Danjo 32:58 + 31:28 = 64:26
2, Kihara 63:04
3, Enomoto 57:56
The same placing as the '09 Nats. Kihara was the leader till 5 round, but I
could win a losing game at the last flight. 8 competitors entered.
Got a good luck, Aki
