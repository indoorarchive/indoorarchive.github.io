> First, and most important, what's your secret ? :-)

> OK, more questions. I notice that you have very long wing posts. With
> the height of the wing tips, it seems that the propeller has full
> clearance and never touches the ceiling. Question: what are the reasons
> that you have such long wing posts?

Clean wash for tail. The post in not so long, just normal length.
Sometimes the wing tip touched the ceiling, but the prop touched much much
more.

> At launch, your model flies extremely flat. How is this achieved?

Hi pitch setting.
First touch to the ceiling time was between 1min30 and 11min30.
Yes, it took 11:30 to touch the 7.4m ceiling.

> Do you also use downthrust to counteract the pitch-up moment from the
> propeller thrust? Do you use motor stick bowing under high rubber tension

No and No.
No downthrust. No stick bow. The bracing wire is very tight.

> Finally, on the VP. Does the pitch change gradually over time?

Yes.
