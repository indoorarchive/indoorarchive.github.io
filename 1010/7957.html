Did you guys know that Jeff Hood had put together hardcover books of old INAV issues? They are available for purchase at lulu.com

http://www.lulu.com/browse/search.php?fListingClass=0&fSearch=INAV

There are 6 volumes in all and go all the way back to INAV's beginnings in the 1960s.

They're a little pricey, but that's the cost of self publication.

I go through my electronic copies of old INAV issues all the time. They are an *amazing* source of information. Unfortunately, old contest results, like from the 1990's, are also shown; it is evident that participation at national events like USIC is way down compared to the present.
