The thing is: This is so intended for R/C that it is very clear that FF was never considered in any of this.

Put a Lanzo Stick into the prop of the full sized Taylorcraft and tell me if it poses any real threat to the operator of the full sized craft. Or your proverbial paper airplane. It would cut both the shreds.

Now put a large fiberglass dual turbine jet that weighs 75 pounds, you have a real chance of loosing both aircraft.
