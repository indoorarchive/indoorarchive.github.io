David Aronstein has stepped up to lead the FAC events at the Nats (Thanks David!). Here is a recent communique regarding the schedule:
 

 "On the entry form, the events are not broken out by day. I assume that’s because we now have essentially two flying sites.
  
 So you can fly any ‘light’ event any or all days on the West end, and any ‘heavy’ event any or all days on the East end.
  
 If this is the case - I like it! It lets each flier give time to the events they really want to concentrate on.
  
 So the only thing we really need to schedule are the mass launches.
  
 I propose the following:
 · Thursday
 o 12:00 Noon - P-24 Mass Launch
 o 1:00 PM – WWI Mass Launch
 · Friday
 o 12:00 Noon – Phantom Flash Mass Launch
 o 1:00 PM – Combined Racer Mass Launch
 · Saturday
 o 12:00 Noon – WWII Mass Launch
 o 1:00 PM – Coconut Scale Mass Launch
 · Sunday
 o 12:00 Noon – Flying Horde Mass Launch
  
 Open to suggestion, but this gives us something to start the discussion J.
  
 -David
