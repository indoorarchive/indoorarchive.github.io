On 10.5.2012 9:36, jos_melis wrote:
> What kind of sidecutter should I buy for cutting the boron? The one
> I'm using at the moment is not a good one I think.Mainly used for
> cutting propshafts etc..

I use scissors. Mostly small ones I bought from a fly-tackle shop. I
take a short piece of office tape, fold it onto the boron to the place
where I want to cut it, and then cut through the tape. Pull the fibres
out of the folded tape and put the tape to trash.
