Bill regularly attends the local MMM indoor meets and holds several site
records. He's as good at indoor as ever. But the real answer <cue the angry,
torch-bearing mob> is since moving to Colorado Bill has been bitten by the
outdoor FF bug in a big way. He flies Coupe, P-30, Old Time Rubber, some AMA
and Nos gas, and lately E-36. We still call him "Beginner Bill"--a jest, of
course. Bill is one of the finest Free Flighters in our club, and we have
lots of good ones.
