For me, as an A6 spectator, 2012 has been a great year. A recap:

Bill Gowen sets Cat IV record at Tustin in December 2011, narrowly beating out Gary Hodson.

Bill sets new Cat I record in Atlanta in Jan 2012.

The same Bill sets a new Cat II record at Kent in April 2012, narrowly beating out Tome Sova. We missed the Kansas City gang at this contest.

Tom sets Cat III record in Pontiac in May 2012, narrowly beating out Bill.

Aki flies an incredible 11+ minutes in Japan.

There are two more A6 shootouts. First at USIC. Who is going to challenge the A6 experts? Do we want to see Bill, Tom, Gary, or Tem win yet again? I believe that Gary and Tem won't attend USIC this year.

Sanborn and Don S.: Are you guys up for the challenge?

I hope the final A6 shootout will take place at Lakehurst over Labor Day weekend.
