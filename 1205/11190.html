Hank Nystrom and I met at the ETSU Mini-Dome today and soon went to lunch. The banners were taken down and stored yesterday and will be rehung after the USIC. While we were there, the facilities director Tom Trent talked with someone from Physical Plant (aka Utilities) about turning off the HVAC and blowers. On Monday, the scoreboard bottom will be removed and the scoreboard will be raised up to the H-Frame which is the upper limit of its movement.

I believe that Elmer Bryant will be opening the dome in the mornings and Mike Rhea will be the daytime person. Tom Trent will be vacating as he frequently does at this time. He assumes that we will not cause problems and will behave ourselves. <Grin>

All seems to be progressing okay. I look forward to seeing many of you again.

Thanks to everyone who resolved the helium supply concerns.
