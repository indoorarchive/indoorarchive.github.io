This is the data of my A6 at Shellcom Sendai on 6 May.
Rubber 0.88 grms, 470mm long loop, Tan2 May99
Wound 3900, backed off 5,
Time 11mins 08secs
Climb about 40m, Dead sticked (rubber twine around prop shaft) at about 12m,
maybe over 100 turns left
Please do not blame me for using precious rubber (May99) for A6, because the
rubber had been slit over 10 years ago for EZB. ; )

109+107= 3mins 36secs StdCLG is the famous Buddenbohm/Ray Lit'l Sweep. The
plan is just published in the newest NSSF Digest Mar-Apr '12 issue.
I built 4 and my best model was a modified one that had slightly
undercambered wing.
Good flying, Aki
