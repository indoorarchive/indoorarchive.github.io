> Art I haven't seen much of your winders lately have you released a parts list?

There aren't all that many of them (25?) and they're kinda spread thin around the country and the world. If you're going to USIC there'll be plenty there.

There isn't a detailed parts list because the device has been under constant development. I'm just now getting to where I'm satisfied with it, and most of the recent changes have been only to make fabrication easier and more accurate.

The core is 4 part nos. from Servo City, a counter from Trumeter, a reed switch from your local burglar alarm guy and a radial button magnet from K&J. Everything else is from the ole reliable LHS, Radio Shack and Home Despot.

What a kit does is eliminate a lot of individual S&H charges, and having to buy a foot of an oddball K&S tube just to get an inch. Or a square foot of lexan when you only need 25 sq in. Wait a few days until Shadow posts the build on HPA and you'll get a better take on how it works.

If you want, the plans, templates and assembly instructions are free. Just send your address. I don't want to scan and e-mail the plan because I don't trust the size to come out right.
