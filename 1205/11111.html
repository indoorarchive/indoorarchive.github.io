That's because the indoor Nats is two weeks away and the outdoor FF is two months away. Those numbers WILL change.

Outdoor FF is the largest Nats of all. It has the most events (about 70) the most contestants (175-250) and the most event entries (something like 800 on average).

Another interesting fact: the outdoor FF Nats has been growing steadily for about fifteen years. Over that same period the indoor Nats has of course been in decline.
