Aki-san is correct. The Jim Jones rib slicing tool permits adjusting the
distance the rib template moves downward between slicing cuts by moving a
triangular (~30 deg triangle) indexing plate to the right. The horizontal
movement is 2-4 time the vertical movement of the template. My ability to
describe this clearly is not so great.

I wish that I had bought another one of these and another two or more torque
meters while Jim Jones was still able to make and sell them. Many good tools
came from him.

Fred Rash
