*-- FEMA's inventory:* FEMA distribution centers have an overall inventory
of more than 5 million liters of water, 3 million meals, 900,000 blankets
and 100,000 cots. FEMA and the Department of Defense established Incident
Support Bases in Westover, Mass. and Lakehurst, New Jersey to pre-position
supplies including water, meals, blankets and other resources closer to
potentially impacted areas, should they be needed and requested by states.
As of this morning, FEMA has moved roughly 245,000 liters of water, more
than 174,000 meals and thousands of blankets and cots to Westover Air
Reserve Base; and more than 400,000 liters of water and more than 390,000
meals and thousands of cots to Lakehurst Naval Air Station in Lakehurst,
New Jersey, and more commodities are en route, as weather conditions permit.
