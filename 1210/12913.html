From H.G. Frautschy:

Hi folks,

Great news - Yesterday I spoke with Bob Campbell of EAA and was happily informed that Family Flightfest is a go for 2013!

The dates will be March 1-3, 2013. We'll use the same general format we've used in the past. If you have any specific requests or suggestions, please let me know. Since I'm not working in the building any more, I need a bit more time to get things organized. I'm still in Wisconsin, but I expect I'll be in Connecticut by then, and will have to do some of the planning from a distance. It is my plan right now to come back to participate. You bet!

No final word on the cost - I suspect it will be a bit more than it was, but that means it will still be a bargain.
So feel free to pass the word (yeah, if it can make it, in your column, John). Whoooo hoo!

If there are folks who express interest, feel free to pass along this e-mail address and have them let me know they're interested in participating. John - let's do the same process we did last year, with entry forms coming to you, if that works for you.

Oh, and before anyone asks, no, it turns out this decision was made before yesterday's big news at EAA HQ was known to the staff, so it has nothing to do with the change at the top of EAA.
