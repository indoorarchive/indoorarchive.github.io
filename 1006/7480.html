Being another devil, I'd say that, since the CG is closer to the wing than the canard, that most of the lift comes from the wing and not the canard. The canard is a strong contributor, unlike the rear stab on a conventional flapper, which is at such a high negative angle to counter the wing pitching moment that is has a net downward lift.
