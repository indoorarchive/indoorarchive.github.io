I believe I am right in stating that UK fliers and most if not all European
fliers use grams per metre to specify motors. 100mm lengths give a good
approximation and it is useful that one metre

Lengths provide two full F1D motors. F1d motors average out at around 1.4
grams per metre. I hope this is helpful.
