Yes, I also enjoyed Billie's teaching and conversation. He built a prop for my LPP by wetting some really thin balsa blades in the water fountain, layered them between several sheets of paper towel from the bathroom, and used several feet of some old Tan II rubber to wrap them around a piece of PVC sticking out of those giant rolls of mats or *whatever* stacked along the walls of the dome!! The next morning, I had two very nicely formed prop blades. So, Billie if you're out there, thank you again! Now I know you can make props just about anywhere in a pinch...
