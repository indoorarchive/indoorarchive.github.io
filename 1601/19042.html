To the post in regard to Kibbie dome, I am compelled to share a few thoughts. The event has for many years been a big event and I feel an important in the recent history of the indoor flying. It has regularly been attended internationally incuding by fliers from europe. This site was the host of a wc event as well. Today with so many few events and flying sites I feel it is important to preserve the continuation of this event. The concept is very velcomong at kibbie dome annual fly what you want. I think Andy Tagliafico needs special recognition for keeping it going for so many years. Also i feel that August is the bestntime for the event per existing flying schedules. I look forward to attending the event this year, and am also willing to donate to keep the event going and preserve and continue the use of this flying site.
