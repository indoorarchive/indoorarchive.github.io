As Ray stated the window to introduce cross proposals is May 31 thru June
30.

Here's what the rule book says about cross proposals:

Cross proposals may be filed by any open class AMA member (as outlined
above). Cross proposals are alternate means of accomplishing the objectives
of a basic proposal which has passed the initial ballot (see Contest Board
Voting section). While there is latitude in allowing alternative proposals,
the original objective shall be retained. Cross proposals will not be used
to introduce new rules changes or to reintroduce proposals that were
defeated in the Initial Ballot. The cross proposal phase takes place in
accordance with the schedule. The validity of a cross proposal shall be
determined by the Chairperson and/or the Contest Board.
