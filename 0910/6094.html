I ran across this on the AMA website while searching for plastic props. Ha! 12 to 15 seconds?? I fly these things out-of-sight.

http://www.modelaircraft.org/shopama/dept.aspx?id=DA090A0104E24ABFA8C167BCE9633365

"The Flying Machine is a Rubber Powered Balsa Motorplane from Guillow's. Suitable for ages 10 and older. Easy to fly and assemble! Hand launched flights of 12-15 seconds and from 250-300 feet in distance. Rise-off-ground flights can be made from a smooth flying surface, including a 4' x 8' sheet of material. Glides to a smooth landing after the rubber motor bands unwind. Landing gear with red plastic wheels red plastic propeller. Balsa construction pre-decorated with red print and graphics. Complete assembly instructions provided . Flight instructions are printed on the bottom of the wing. Assembly required. Wingspan: 17"
