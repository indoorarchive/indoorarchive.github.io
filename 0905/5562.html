I talked to Tim B.and Jimmybux right after the comp.



Jimmy won HLG by a hair over Tim. Tim blew up his three best gliders on the scoreboard and other hazards! A day of carnage for him but missed first place by only 0.6 sec. Not bad considering his last indoor contest was in 1967.



Jimmy was humble about the win. He had never flown indoor tip launch until yesterday AM! A champion glider flinger, that Buxton. This is his fourth or fifth Nats win in IHLG.



Stan B had what sounded like a bittersweet day. He won UCLG and had the best single HLG time (82.8) but could not get a good second flight in HLG and was out of the to placers.



According to JB Ralph put on a real clinic in Standard Cat! I'm sure Kurt K. was impressed to have been beaten for the first time in ? years.



Sounded like a great contest.
