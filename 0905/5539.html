Word on the grapevine is that Peck-Polymers / AtoZ is going to send another ultra-generous donation for the USIC raffle. This is not confirmed yet, but if it does happen there will be some more great stuff to win.

Mark B had a great idea to open the raffle up to people who can't attend USIC. After pondering the logistics a bit, I think maybe the best way to do that is to use the "buddy system". As in: enlist the help of a buddy who is going.

This would take care of:
- Sending money (buddy drives it down)
- Buying tickets (buddy buys yours along with his)
- Identifying a winning ticket (buddy checks your tickets along with his)
- Prize selection (trust your buddy to do right by you)
- Shipping (buddy drives it back)

So, go to USIC! But if you can't, get a buddy to win you some raffle booty.
