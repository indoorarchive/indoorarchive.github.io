Having used the McCombs formulae for years I can tell you this doesn't much
matter in the practical sense. The resulting starting CG postions are always
very close to optimum regardless of whether you measure the tail moment from
LE to LE, quarter chord to quarter chord or CG to quarter chord.

 

Then again McCombs was not concerned with indoor duration and their
extremely aft CGs. He devised his formulae to address indoor and outdoor FF
scale and outdoor duration, mainly.
