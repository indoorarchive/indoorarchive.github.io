Ignacio,

Yes, to answer your question.

 

The basic requirement is that the blades be flat (no warping for helical
pitch), of balsa, 0,81mm minimum thick, and not have any rounding of edge
faces, meaning 90-degree corners and all faces flat. Think simple. Your
imagination is the limit after these rules are met.
