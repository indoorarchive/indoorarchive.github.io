Hey all, just wanted to send a quick update and give some highlights for
the day. We have a big contingent from Georgia attending the contest. Al
Mac also made a showing.

Bill Gowen had a great day flying F1M, he broke the US record with a time
of 22:05. Bill also has the world record currently and 22:05 is the second
highest time ever done with an F1M. Congrats Bill!

Here are the rest of the results:

F1M
Bill Gowen: 18:10, 20:33, 22:05

F1D 1.2g
Kang Lee: 34:27

F1D 1.4g

Brett Sanborn: 30:25

HLS

Nick Ray: 35:44

F1L
Hope Finn: 14:32
Leo Pilachowski: 21:30
