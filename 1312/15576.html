11/25/2013














Attention Contest Directors:
 In an effort to save on postage related expenses, we have recently launched the "on-line portal" for sanctioning. Our next step in this effort is to send all completed event documents to the CD's electronically for downloading. Effective immediately; All flying and Non flying documents will be sent electronically to the e-mail address contained within your membership record. For those without an active email account, we will continue to mail your information.
 If you do not have a web account, registering just takes a moment. Please visit www.modelaircraft.org and click on the "sign in" button on the top right corner of the page. Next you will need to select "Create a new web account now" and follow the prompts. Once you create this account you will then need to "link" by selecting your name next to the "sign in" field and "link". Our Contest Directors Portal can be located under Members and Clubs, Members Only , CD Portal.
 If you experience problems with your login, please contact me directly.
