 BONG EAGLES FREE FLIGHT MODEL AIRPLANE CLUB
 Rantoul Hangar 3 Fall Indoor contest
  
 CATEGORY II (44.5’) site – AMA Sanctioned event 18/6861.
  
 FRIDAY, SATURDAY and SUNDAY, Oct. 5/6/7 2018
 9:00 AM TO 9:00 PM Friday/Saturday, 8:00 AM TO 4:00 PM Sunday
 SPONSOR: BONG EAGLES - www.bongeagles.org
 ENTRY FEE COVERS ALL EVENTS
 SENIOR & OPEN: $25.00 for one day, $50 for the weekend.
 Juniors $5.00/day or included with paying adult.
  
 CONTEST DIRECTOR: Kurt Krempetz and Mike Kirda
 6048 W. Eddy, Chicago, IL 60634 P: 312-907-3831
  
 All Indoor Free flight AMA Events available to fly.
 FAC and Science Olympiad flyers welcomed. Bring your kids!
  
 Additional events: Phantom Flash, Mashup.
 Mashup plan here: http://www.hippocketaeronautics.com/hpa_plans/details.php?image_id=8199 http://www.hippocketaeronautics.com/hpa_plans/details.php?image_id=8199
  
  
 Hangar 3 was part of the shuttered Chanute AFB. The floor space is 200x240’, with 200’x200’ flyable. Porta-potties will be available. Electricity available. No water is available on-site. The CD will have bottled water available on-site.
  
 Must have AMA license and sign Village of Rantoul waiver to fly. Tables and chairs extremely limited, be prepared to bring your own. Record attempts will be given precedence. We ask that folks use common sense here to avoid fouling up an attempt. Contest will be West Baden format, self-service in a binder. Processing to be done for records or when requested.
  
 Bring it, fly it and most of all, HAVE FUN!
