Just a quick note to the Indoor community that reads the Indoor column in Model Aviation magazine. The column was being published 4 times a year, January, April, July, and October. It has been reduced to 3 times s year. So will now only appear January, May, and September. So getting contest announcement in will become more difficult unless you know the dates way in advance. Each column has to be submitted 3 months before it's published. So for example to appear in the May column , I would need the info no later than the beginning of February .

The reduction from 4 columns per year down to 3 also effects the other Free Flight columns appearing in Model Aviation.
