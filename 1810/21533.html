Aki, the only things that can break our sport are loss of flying sites, loss of participants, and ever changing rules (F1D, I'm looking at you). Mikhail's kits not only have the potential to draw in new participants into free flight here in the US, but already have a proven track record of attracting unprecedented numbers of youth into the indoor scene in Russia. I regularly see videos surfacing with as many as 100 youth flying gliders in their contests. Mikhail's design is the most popular, but many teenagers are campaigning their own designs. If that's what it looks like to "break our sport," then please, break it some more!
