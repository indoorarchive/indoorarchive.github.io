Long-time Southern California indoor flyer Warren Williams has been
elected to the AMA Hall of Fame for 2006.

Warren has flown every indoor class as well as outdoor rubber and
glider. He is a well known expert in Ornithopters. 92 years young,
he's still building and flying.

Congratulations, Warren!
