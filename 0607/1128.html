> Now, what is the advantage?
>
> Isn't a smooth surface better because less drag?

Wrinkled film has a nice uniform appearance (no lines from being
rolled up) and a slight give.

As for the second question-you should be covering tighter if the
surface isn't smooth. The way I do it, the film is balled up somewhat
tightly and has a fine texture, almost like that of skin.
