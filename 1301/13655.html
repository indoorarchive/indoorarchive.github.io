John,

I don't care what version of Nocal rules are being flown just want to
know which version of the rules will be flown so I know what model to
bring. I (as well as my dad an Loucka and I am sure manyothers) have
models which fit either version (no weight and 6.2gr limit). As to which
version is chosen for the USIC schedule it is really up to the CD to
decide. The basic difference is the USIC nocal is best 1 of 5 flights
with a 6.2 gram min weight. FAC Nocal has no weight limit and is scored
as 3 of 3 flight total i.e.all three flights are added together. I have
my personal preference but it is not my decision.
