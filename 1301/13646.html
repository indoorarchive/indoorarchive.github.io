I know it's short notice but we will also be having a contest in
Catonsville, Maryland at CCBC (Community College of Baltimore County) that
should be added to the list:

https://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&client=firefox-a&ie=UTF-8&q=community+college+of+baltimore+county&fb=1&gl=us&hq=community+college+of+baltimore+county&cid=0,0,13630616210130790231&ei=3q36UK2_Huu30AHw-4CICA&ved=0CKcBEPwSMAA

What: Class AA contest
Where: CCBC's 45' dome in Catonsville, MD
When: Friday, February 1, 2013, 9 PM-12 or 1 AM

The contest is relatively short because this is the only available time we
have the dome. The contest is sanctioned for records and F1D team selection
points etc.

So far our preliminary attendees are Kang, Tony Pavel, Steve Fujikawa, John
Zelescky, a few other Maxecuters, and myself.
