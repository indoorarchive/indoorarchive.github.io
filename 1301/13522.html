While I have USIC on my mind, the parking situation needs a little description.

The parking meters around the armory are in effect during the week until 5pm. There is a small area that we can use to drop off boxes on Tuesday.

I have arranged free parking about a 10 minute walk away. For those that need to be closer, the school can "bag" a meter in front of the armory for $19/day.

I envision people who need to be close parking in the free lot and then car-pooling to one of the meters, and sharing the cost.

After 5 and on the weekend we can park anywhere.
