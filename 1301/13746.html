Folks, I've uploaded a spreadsheet of the proposed FAC schedule at USIC. It's in the USIC 2013 folder under Indoor Events in the files section. Please have a look and tell me what you think. Two sets of eyes have already looked over it to check for schedule conflicts, but I'd like more eyes to take a peek. It's a tight schedule, but that is the norm with FAC events in my experience. It's part of the fun of FAC.

Please note that I've listed a proposed time for scale judging based on conversations with Tim Lavender, who is bringing my judging crew provided that someone else has not already picked up that item. If I'm stepping on someone's toes, please say so. It's just that there has so far been no mention of scale judges.
