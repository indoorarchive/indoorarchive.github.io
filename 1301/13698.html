Hi Pete,

Thank you for your support!

The best way to support the program, and to get your T-shirt, is to join the US F1D Team Selection program.

Ideally, everyone who joins will also fly F1D and compete for a spot on the team, which will in turn will make better teams.

However, simply joining the program, even if you don't fly F1D or are not eligible to compete for a spot on the US team, will help support the program, both monetarily and by boosting our numbers.

The AMA funds its FAI teams based on program membership and performance. Showing that a large number of people are interested in the continued well being of F1D will help ensure our continued funding.

You can find the program membership form here:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/Indoor_Construction/files/Indoor%20Events/2013%20U\S%20F1D%20Team%20Selection/F1D%20Indoor%20Team%20Selection%20Program%20Participa\tion%20Form%202013.doc
