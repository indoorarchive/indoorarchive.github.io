The University has notified us that construction on the classrooms outside of Armory will be taking place during our event.

Most of the work will be in other areas of the building. The part in our area has been described as "opening doors to the hallway behind the bleachers" and "working on the 4th floor mezzanine", which is the space part way up the long sides. The is no talk of doing anything that should significantly disturb the air.

We do not believe that this work will have any impact on our event, other than some noise, but we want everyone to be aware.

We will still have safe and easy access to the building through a set of "buffered" doors to keep the air nice.
