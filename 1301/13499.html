Depends on schedules events and what is flown with what. I have certain
events I personally like, Int Stick is actually my personal favorite
indoor event. As you know I have new EZBs in process (on the hippocket
forum), all well as new ministick in process (I will be posting photos
of that as well on hippocket). Realistically I have to rebuild many
models as they have degraded over the years or were lost or damaged
beyond effective repair. 35cm, HLS, F1L, to name a few probably a new
Open Pennyplane and Manhattan Cabin (assuming the event is being flown)
. Generally when I go to USIC I like to fly as many events as possible
but it really depends on scheduling. I look at the schedule then pick my
favorite events in each time slot and make sure they are built first
then start to add others in to maximize the fun!
