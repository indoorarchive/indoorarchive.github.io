Here is a plan with printwood. Of particular interest is the sheet prop
blank, creased and bent to make the blade angles. No wonder there are reports
that people couldn't get them to fly. ;-) It looks to be about 6&1/8"
diameter, leaving about 1/16" of ground clearance. Note that the landing gear
strut on the plan and printwood are not exactly the same size.
