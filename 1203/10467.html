Another neat site feature to point out: it is difficult to get hung up.

The wooden structure is flush with the ceiling, so there aren't many open places to get caught on. The only hang-up I heard about was a glider on one of the side speakers.

And speaking of speakers - there is a LOT of room between the speakers hanging on the sides.
