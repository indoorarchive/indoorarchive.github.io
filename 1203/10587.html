John,
I opened my Wilder many years ago & as I recall, the only difficulty was getting all the various shafts to re-align with their corresponding bearings upon reassembly, but apparently it is not to difficult since I was able to do it. I used some of the Dow Corning 3M grease that I was using for rubber lube at the time. I would think a white Lithium grease would be fine. I applied it to the gear teeth & the bearings. It did seem to smooth the operation of the winder, but it will get squished out from between the teeth so I don't know how long the benefit lasts.
Gary Hodson
