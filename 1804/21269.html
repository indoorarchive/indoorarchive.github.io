The goal of F1D is nice, but a better goal is retention in the hobby, regardless of flavor.

Wright Stuff exposes a lot of folks to the hobby, but it hasn't translated into more indoor fliers.I wish we could understand why that is.
I suspect though that I know why: A lack of a local support group, aka club, and places to fly.I live in a large city and have to drive to Wisconsin or downstate to have a good place to fly.
