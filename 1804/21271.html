 It looks like the F1D competitors (85%) have spoken. I have rethought this and I agree.
The jury for the 2018 indoor WC made a very wise decision.
**************************************************************...While the VP is an important part of the model in case the available height of the site is limited, a purchased VP is not in RTF state. The usage of such VP needs expertise, measurements and test flights to harmonize its setting with the size and setting of the prop and the rest of the model, rubber, available height and actual conditions in the site. Therefore, the jury agrees to continue the practice applied at several previous championships, i. e. not banning the usage of purchased VP hubs."

**************************************************************
A competitor can now use a purchased VP hub to compete as he learns about it and later build his own if he chooses.
