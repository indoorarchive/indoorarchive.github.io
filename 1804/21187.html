Let us know when it's locked in.

Everyone should make the effort to go to Kibbie. It's a great site that
consistently has good air and good weather, tons of floor space, and no
spectators wandering around. It reminds me of the good ole days at Johnson
City.

The travel really isn't that bad. Fly Spokane on Southwest (check your bags
for free), rent a car (or plan to carpool with a friend like I am), drive
to Moscow, split a hotel room, and get five days of excellent flying. You
don't even have to lug a table out there. While once regarded by some as a
culinary wasteland, the food options in Moscow have vastly improved over
the past few years.

Brett
