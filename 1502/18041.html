As a strictly sport flyer, my results with the Re2000 foil lack the credibility of those by the engineering types who can generate and keep accurate record of things. I made alternative wings for a Minislick, a Gowan A-6 and a Danjo A-6 last March. It turned the Minislick from borderline unmanageable to outright unmanageable, so I scrapped that wing. The A-6's were a different story. They both dropped airspeed and climbed faster on thinner rubber than with the original arced airfoil. At least they appeared to me to do that. The Danjo was very fast, and now it's more like a real indoor model. I'm happy with the Re2000, on A-6's anyway. But I'm only a living room sport flier.
