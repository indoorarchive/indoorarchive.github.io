Yes thanks for posting this. We launched the project on Monday and we are
hoping to reach our goal before the deadline of July 4th.

I am building custom kits for Mini Sticks, Penny Planes, and F1D's as some
of the possible rewards for donating. The kits will include access to
digital plans and notes, access to a video for construction, and of course
the necessary balsa, wire, covering materials and miscellany and printed
plans in a mailing tube. Tools are not included. Payment is through Amazon
Payments, accepting most major credit cards. http://kck.st/KKfQsg

The prices are as follows for backers:
$30 Mini Stick Kit
$50 LPP Kit
$150 MiniStick + LPP + Custom "Saks" 10:1 Winder
$300 F1D Kit

I know how much you all care about Indoor, and every small amount helps the
cause. I want to see Float finished, so please help in any way you can. You
can share the link to the project on your Facebook Wall, or Email it to
your friends, family or coworkers. It is a great way to show people the
true beauty of Indoor.

Thanks for your continued support!

Ben Saks
