This is what I'm thinking ... Instead of a survey, let's have a competition.

A few indoor competitive zealots should fly coupe and P30 against DeLoach and his buddies. Forget the stupid maxes and thermal aided flights. We go straight into early morning flyoffs. When the indoor guys clock the outdoor guys, then will realize that it's not the difference in outdoor vs. indoor rules but the difference in the outdoor vs. indoor flier/builder.
