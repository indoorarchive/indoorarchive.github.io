Welcome, Steve - where are you located ?? You could look for an Ikara
Super Bulldog (or Bulldog) which is an indoor ARF Pennyplane and a good
starter. I gave a couple of them to some guys in my R/C club who were
flying indoor with us and one of them, who stuck with it, did his first 4
minute + flight last Monday. It flies nicely on 1/8" rubber. I don't
think that the Super Bulldog is made anymore but the Bulldog is the same
plane covered with tissue (the Super is a clear film covered model).
FAI model supplies still shows them both at
http://www.faimodelsupply.com/fai-ikara.htm - for the extra 2 bucks I
would get the Superdog as they call it.
