No, not directly. He makes three sizes (I'm sure he will correct me if I am
wrong) SO, PP, and F1D/EZB. Use the smallest size for ministick. It will be
heavier than a wire bearing, but it is so easy to use that I don't use
anything else. You can carefully file away excess material to make it
lighter.
