I stumbled across these old Plymouth promo films on YouTube. Very high
production value and some terrific aerial footage in here!

The first installment is slow, with little flying activity. Then there's
lots of footage toward the end of film 2. Look for indoor flying with some
familiar faces in the 3rd film: Jim Richmond and Tony D'Alessandro receiving
giant trophies in a packed banquet hall!
