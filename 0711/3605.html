> for props? I know the glider guys use them for curing bagged

Have you thought about an infra red bulb in a spotlight lamp fixture? I used
one to dry out my wife's cell phone after my son took it swimming. I used a
$10 laser temperature qauge from Harbor Freight to set the distance at about
two feet between the cell phone and the infra red lamp to make sure the cell
phone didn't get above 150 degrees F. I believe 200 degrees could be
reached by closing the distance between the light and the prop.
