We just received a text message congratulating the US Organizing Committee and the AMA on being awarded the 2018 Indoor World Championship. The contest is to be held in the Atrium of the West Baden Springs Hotel in West Baden Springs, Indiana, USA. The proposed dates are April 18-23, 2018.
