Last year 25 P-18 kits were ordered for the build and fly for the Nats. It was a sellout. There were almost as many P-18 novice flyers as there were regular AMA expert flyers, and P-18 was the best attended event, by far.


This year for the Nats the order has been doubled to 50 kits.


What if P-18 becomes the most popular event at the Indoor Nats in the same year the Indoor Contest Board rejects it as an AMA event? (I think that'd be considered *ironic*).
