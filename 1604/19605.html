Re: message 9. I'd draw a rectangle 8.125 long and .325 (4%) high, put pins in the two lower corners, and draw a french curve across the pins until the curve runs tangent to the top line. If the tangent point is too far from 50% chord, I'd find a different french curve. They're cheap and I have a lot of them.
