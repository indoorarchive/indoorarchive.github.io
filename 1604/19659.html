So, occasionally, I have ideas. Most of them are worthless but every now
and then I have one I think is good enough to pass along.

I've been using these small plastic bottles for about fifteen years. IIRC I
got them way back when A2Z was in its early heyday. As glue dispensers they
are invaluable. A month or two ago it occurred to me that I could fill a
small one with acetone for much more precise method of application. I have
found this to work great!
