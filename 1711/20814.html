 Here is a video for the AMA Alpha-
 

 https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OZklUNtIu-c&feature=youtu.be https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OZklUNtIu-c&feature=youtu.be

 

 The airplane will be the subject of a new Science Olympiad Trial event AMA Aerial Scramble and the AMA is planning on sponsoring a new Postal Event using the Alpha.
 

 Note, we came close to a "shake-the-box-and-out-flies-a-winner" type kit but max performance still takes some classic scientific method stuff and solid trim practice.
 

 Note that the model can fly on the 3/32 FAI Tan outdoors but to make the model go OOS, use 1/8 FAI rubber.
