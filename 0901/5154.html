When measuring items on my AccuLab as light as your individual prop
parts, I include a heavier "helper" mass and record the addition over
the known weight of the helper.

You can also play with the extreme resolution by adding .00x g
weights and getting an estimate (over/under) .xx5 gram. The lighter
weights were inherited from my early homemade beam balance that
measured out 3 digits.

Bruce
