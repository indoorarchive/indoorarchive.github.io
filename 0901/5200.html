The tips are vertical. The inward tip of the outboard rib is to provide camber to the covering on the tip plate. The maximum wing span is 40cm. I don't have a lot of experience with different size motors yet but it looks like with good quality Tan II rubber something around .090" works well. With Super Sport I would probably go a little heavier than that.
