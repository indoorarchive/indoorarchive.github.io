and resolve a number of questions. The helium issue is a big one. In
addition to no Std Cat on Wed, there is no High Wing Mono on Thur, no

no Golden Age on Sat, and no A6 on Sat. WWI ML is listed twice, Fri,
Sat. Probably Sat is WWII ML.
