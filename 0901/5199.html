Larry and All,

The Indoor_Construction files section has a listing of hotels that Hank Nystrom compiled about 3 years ago and I posted in the files section. Airline choices will not be numerous at TRI and include US Airways, Delta, Northwest, and Allegiant.

Nearby larger cities are Knoxville, Asheville, Charlotte, Atlanta, and Nashville. Asheville is 1.5 hr away by car and due south but may or may not offer better choices. Knoxville is about 2 hr away, Charlotte about 3 hr, Atlanta about 5 hr, Nashville 4-5 hr. TRI is about 20-30 minutes to the Mini-Dome at ETSU. I'm a local so my perspective is somewhat limited.

Others should also advise you, Larry.
