Don't thank me, thank Dieter Siebanmann and Thedo Andre for designing
the model and making such detailed building instructions.

My first F1M was built along the lines of Moustique. Having built a
couple of national adaptation of the pennyplane, I did some
modifications, increased wing chord by a cm or two, moved fins to the
end of the stab, used tubes for fuselage. Even though my model is now -
if I recall right - 13 years old, is is still going stung, and a great
model to fly for show purposes, when the conditions are too turbulent to
take the more extreme current models out of the box.
