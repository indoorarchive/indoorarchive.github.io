Last year I bought an Acculab Vic-123 scale. As far as I can tell it has no drawbacks except for a fair amount of drift when you first turn it on. It is stable after a few minutes. It measures to milligrams, has a 120g capacity and is a lot less money than the scales Calgoddard was talking about. The best price I've seen lately is about $240. This is probably less than the total I've invested in junk scales since I started in indoor.
