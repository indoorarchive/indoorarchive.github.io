Hi to all you indoor flying enthusiasts.
 
The Black Hawk RC Pilots have decided to invite the free flight flyers
to join us in the UNI-Dome in Cedar Falls , Iowa during our indoor fly-
in. This will take place Saturday and Sunday, Feb. 21 & 22, 2009 all
day both days. There are no official events for ff scheduled but will
allow people to organize and run any ff events they wish or just fly
for fun under a 130 ft ceiling in an indoor football stadium. Details
at the bhrcp web site or contact bobsrc_at_zumatel.net.
 
Cheers,
