Dear all,

with wishing all the best for 2009 I have posted results in Files
section( CZE&Svk indoor scene) & photos in photo section from our
Year end flying in Bystrice nad Perstejnem in Czech republic at 28
december 2008 . At the end it was real international competition in
F1D , beacuse we have had the pleasure to host Our friends Lutz
Schramm from Germany, Jan Dhim & Eduard Ciapala from Poland and
laughing about Our internationality coming to Czech from Slovakia as
former Czechoslovak citizen myself.
Even when outside was -7 °C in the morning,inside normal 18 to 19 °C
and normal flying condition with only small need for steering. With
baloon training from Belgrade was no problem to steer at 10 m long
carbon rod, and everybody mastered it Super. Results are not so
important, even when Lutz flew 15:15 with 1/2 motor, more interesting
was to see evening before Lutz & Edek playing biliard or all others
trying with bowling ball or squash.
Top of the event was to see whole Klimes family playing real country
band and You can guess who has played on contrabass & banjo ???
Really - small 12 y old Peter contrabass & 14 y old Jirko - banjo .

Not saying, that they both presented new carbon propeler outline, not
so much behind that from Lutz Schramm .

Extra class band...

Another report from 30 dec flying in Sala in Slovakia follows .
