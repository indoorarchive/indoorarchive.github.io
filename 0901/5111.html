I had a half dozen, those with the plastic hanger for 1/8 x3/8 sticks,
weights from 4.5 gms to 4.7 gms. We scraped a few for the SO practice
stuff, IIRC, 2.5 gms was do-able and still tougher than the Ikara's.
(anyway, I never thought much of Ikara's, always hoped someone would
make a decent prop for the SO.)

I looked at a couple 9" gray props, one high pitch, one Peck-Polymers,
both about 7 gms.

If you do scrape, find and use a sharpened woodworkers scraper, a few
minutes with that, a few hours with a razor blade.
