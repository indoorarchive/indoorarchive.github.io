I don't know whether you're interested in the original or the Lite. Just bear in mind that the balance point seems to be at 50-60% of the forward wing. If you build the Lite, all that lumber and paper that brings the weight aft won't be there any more.

Saturday at the church we were kicking around the idea of a double or triple one for outdoors.

I didn't have any 3/16 stock on hand so I used medium hard 1/8 x 1/4. It bears up okay under the 1/16 rubber. Eventually, we'll nail down the right size, which will probably be more like .050.
