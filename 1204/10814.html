In preparation for the upcoming FAI program massacre (anticipated), I'd like our F1D team selection program to more accurately reflect the level of interest that exists.

Announcing a new promotion: With your $40 F1D team selection program entry fee, you will now receive two free F1D team t-shirts. The entry fees go directly to the F1D team fund (just like the previous t-shirt fund raisers we've had), and more importantly your participation in the program helps demonstrate that F1D is one of the FAI programs that should retain funding from the AMA.

Note that your program entry counts even if you don't get to the point of entering a qualifying event (i.e. any F1D contest) - although I certainly hope you do, even it is with an F1L and a .6gm motor.

You can find the program entry form here: http://groups.yahoo.com/group/USAF1D/files/2013%20Program/F1D%20Indoor%20Team%20\
Selection%20Program%20Participation%20Form%202013.doc

Email me your T-shirt sizes after you send in your form.

So what does the team t-shirt look like? Well that's a good question, and it brings me to part II: we need a design. Any artists out there interested in proposing something?

Here are some previous designs: http://groups.yahoo.com/group/Indoor_Construction/files/T-shirt%20designs/
