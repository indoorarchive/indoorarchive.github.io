This the finished pigtail, note the tight front bearing and the "curly"
rear support. To install a prop and shaft, the shaft has to be a
slightly loose fit in the bearing hole, "hook" the shaft thru the front
bearing, then snap it into the rear support. easy to make and use - and
the rear support can be moved around for thrust adjustments.

FWIW, I may have posed the whole series of steps in the photo's section.
