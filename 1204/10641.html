I spoke with Emil this evening. As of this point we have...4 entrants.

We need about 30 people to make this self-sustaining. Let's make this happen, indoorists! If you are anywhere within driving distance, consider coming out. Donations are great, but flying in a site like this is a big part of what our activity is about.

Let's also make sure that everyone knows that, along with being one of the best places in the world to fly serious Indoor duration planes, indoor planes of any type are welcome. Denny Darts, Dandiflyers, and P-24s will be a blast to fly under 145', too.

Ask your flying buddies if they are going. If they aren't, find out why. It would be interesting to get a list of the reasons and see if there is anything we could do to address them.

Do they think they aren't "expert" enough to compete in a big contest?

Is it too expensive?

Do they have another commitment?

Do they think it wouldn't be fun?
