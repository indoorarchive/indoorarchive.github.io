Theses 11" and 23" are available now to ship, 15" is of my personal one.

On the 11" and 15" one, vertical line is 1/2" radius. The 20" (not pictured) and 23" line is 1" radius.

I can make pretty much any pitch required. The jig is 10" long. Depending on what you are looking for, I can make the inner radius line anything, then add 10" for maximum diameter. The center line can be made for symmetrical or off-center for flaring prop designs.

Currently only one camber is available, however other deeper cambers will become available soon.
