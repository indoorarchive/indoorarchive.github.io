The MMM is proud to announce the addition of an F1D regional qualifier to
our March 25th, 2007 contest. This meet will feature all NFFS National Cup
events as well. The site is the Colorado Springs City Auditorium, 37' peak
height with mostly smooth ceiling.

 

More details will be sent to this list, www.indoornews.com
<http://www.indoornews.com/> , and INAV as they become available.

 

And don't forget the upcoming 6th Annual MMM Fall Indoor Champs at Colorado
Springs, November 19th, 2006, 9am-5pm. The Last NFFS National Cup event of
2006! If you would like a contest flyer send me an email off list.
