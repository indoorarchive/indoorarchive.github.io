I take a model each day i go to school for a "mentor" session that we
fly in the gym while classes are changing, today I took a Dennydart mark
IV, on 300 turns it climbed to about 5 feet from the ceiling and
cruised. One lad asked "how to you make it go that close to the ceiling
and then fly around ?" Told hi by how many turns you put in. SO, they
had to wind to 900 turns, up and a bounce then into a fan bracket,
luckily it fell out, recovered and did a few low level turns. fun.
