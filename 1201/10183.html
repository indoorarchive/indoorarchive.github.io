I can’t believe this will be the SIXTH time we’ve done this! Seems like only
yesterday the first PPCC was hatched…

 

After some back and forth we’ve decided to go back to the two day format
this year. I trust most folks will be happy with that. The launch windows
are the same on both days, and you can fly either/both days without
finishing on the same day. No pizza party this year, instead we’ll have a
semi-official sit-down meal following Sunday’s flying. Plenty of good
restaurants within ½ mile of the site.

 

Entry fee is $30 for two days (16 hours) of flying. That means we need 20
flyers to break even. Last year the club lost about $100.

 

See you in 3-1/2 weeks!

Don DeLoach
