Kinda OT, but a few comments.

I buy "lots" of 1/16 or 1/8 sheet from various suppliers for the
Dennydart kits. Git maybe 25 sheets, scale them looking for 6# to 8#
stock. part of the time when i cut the sheet into 12" lengths and start
stripping it turns out to be "guillows" on one edge and almost punk on
the other. Large PITA but I just live with it.

Haven't found any bulk suppliers that don't have that problem yet.
