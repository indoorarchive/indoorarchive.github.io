Greetings.

I've been working on a fixture for cutting prop blocks.

Here is the Excel function required:

=ATAN((2*PI()*U7)/S14)*180/PI()

U7 here equals c in Fujikawa and Zseleczky's paper (See page 6.) I think it would be called moment arm typically, this is the distance from the center of the pivot to the angle the pivot arm rests upon.

S14 here equals pitch.
