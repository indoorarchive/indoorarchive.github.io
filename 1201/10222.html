My favorite stop watch is a Casio DataBank wristwatch. It's out of the way and unlike a Timex, the start/stop is the same button. If you fly outdoors, especially gas powered, a swinging lanyard watch can be REAL exciting!

Back in the day, timing Steve Brown, you also needed a watch that would go over an hour!
