Leroy, each different diameter on the prop will have a different angle,
at least if you have helical pitch, some people do use "flat paddles"
where the pitch is the same all along its length.

You need to select a diameter, plot how far it should travel ahead in
one turn and calculate the angle that needs.At the shaft end it will be
very steep, at the tip much less angle.
