The Master Free Flight Calendar brings together all the contests and other
happenings in the Free Flight world onto a single calendar. The MFFC is posted
on www.freeflight.org and printed periodically in Free Flight Digest for all to
reference. All Free Flight activity in North America is open for submission:
indoor, outdoor, scale, swap meets, symposia, etc.
 
The 2011 MFFC is being finalized at this time. Please send all firm event dates,
site/city/state, sponsoring club, CD phone/email, to me at ddeloach_at_comcast.net.
Please send your event flyers along as well and I’ll gladly link them at
www.freeflight.org.
 
Please spread this message far and wide to all Club leaders on your lists.
 
Thanks in Advance!
