Hello Club leaders and Contest Directors,

 

As the year winds down and 2011 approaches please submit your Free Flight
events for inclusion in the National Free Flight Society (NFFS) Master Free
Flight Calendar (MFFC). The MFFC has a singular purpose: To collect and
disseminate dates of any and all Free Flight activity in North America.
Indoor, outdoor, scale, duration, contests and fun flys-any and all events
that would interest Free Flighters are welcome for submission. The cost is
FREE and the benefits are many:

 

--Thousands of people interested in Free Flight look at the NFFS website
every month, giving your event a web presence for interested folks in your
area

--NFFS will link your contest flyer for FREE on the MFFC, giving attendees a
place to print it out

--The MFFC is reprinted in every issue of Free Flight Digest, which gives
events extra exposure to the 1400+ NFFS members

 

Information needed

- Date(s) of event

- Name of event

- Type of event: (Indoor, AMA, FAC, FAI, swap meet, trade show, meeting,
etc)

- Cup(s): World, Americas and/or National Cup

- Sponsoring club name

- Flying site: City and State

- Event contact: name, phone, email, web URL

- Optional: pdf or jpg scan of your event flyer
