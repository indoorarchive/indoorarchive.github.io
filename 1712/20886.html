Announcement of the opening of additional timekeepers positions to teams from countries other than the USA
 

 We are opening some host provided timekeeper positions to timekeepers from countries other than the USA. This is permitted by the FAI rules. These positions are not listed and should not be put on the Final Entry Form first page. Instead, we ask that an email be sent to the email address below listing the names of the extra timekeepers from the country. Additionally, these extra timekeepers names should be listed on the second accommodations page of the Entry Form so that rooms can be reserved. The organizers will provide the same accommodations (4 nights shared, double occupancy lodging, 3 lunches, the banquet, and $150 for meals) that are given to the US timekeepers. This is one less day of lodging and meals than will be provided to the one team nominated timekeeper. However, we think this is a good offer to those teams desiring to bring additional timekeepers to the 2016 Indoor World Championship at West Baden.
 

 Please respond with the additional timekeepers' names (and email address) to:
 

 leop_at_lyradev.com
 

 Regards,
 

 Leo Pilachowski, for the organizers
