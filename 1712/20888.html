 About slipping knots - this website:
https://www.freeflightsupplies.co.uk/index.php/products/rubber
sells colloidal (or fumed) silica. Apparently it keeps knots from slipping when silicone lube gets into the knot. Haven't tried it myself, for one thing I don't use silica grease or oil (just Armor-all) and haven't had much trouble with slipping knots, but I do have a canister of it - it's usually used as an additive for epoxy to make it thicker and sold by West System as Number 406:
https://www.amazon.com/West-System-COLLOIDAL-SILICA-5-5/dp/B0006JJATW
If you have friends who build RC pylon racers or speedboats they might have some on hand.
