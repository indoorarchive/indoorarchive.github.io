Hi Kang,

       My point was not that one can't fly gliders in this small space or below the flags, my point was it will discourage people from coming. This hobby is frustrating enough to get a model to fly great, adding obstacles just makes it more frustrating and discourages more people not to attend. Gliders have different characteristics than light indoor rubber models, they are almost impossible to steer with a steer pole and when they hit an obstacle the model is typically seriously damage.

Cheers,
Kurt
