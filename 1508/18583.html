F1D World Championships Team Trial Australia

A Team Trial will be held in Adelaide by the Adelaide Aeromodellers Club, with support from Model Aerosport South Australia, to select team members for the 2016 F1D World Championships to be held in the Slanic Salt Mines in Romania.

A new Boyd Felstead perpetual trophy will be awarded to the winner.

To find out more about competing, or to register, please contact the Contest Director preferably by email at tim.haywardbrown (AT) gmail.com

Rubber-powered free flight F1D models are the slowest recognised fixed wing aircraft in the world. An F1L (EZB) competition will be held concurrently. Come along and see a unique and unusual side of the hobby. Visitors are welcome and admission is free. Those living overseas, of course, need to pay your own airfares etc first. But admission to the event is free and you are very welcome

Date: Sunday 29 November 2015 (3pm to 8pm)
Venue: The Gardens Recreation Centre, Parafield, South Australia.
