Hi LeRoy,

 

I glue the tissue tubes to the motor stick, then insert the wing posts into
the tubes.

 

When making ribs, I first make a template out of very thin, flexible brass
sheeting, then holding a piece of straight razor on its tip, I use the
template to cut the ribs out.
