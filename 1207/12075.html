Yes, I have flown there. For several years there was an annual flying event held there. Roughly one half of the building was for RC & the other half for free flight. More recently, they eliminated the dedicated free flight area. I have not heard anything about the event in the last few years. There is a large score board in the center, the ceiling has a lot of "clutter" & as I recall they could not or would not shut off all the fans so there was drifting & turbulence. It was a fun place to fly but not a suitable place for a serious contest. IMHO.
Gary Hodson
