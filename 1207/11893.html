I'll post what I can remember, but I believe Kang was taking notes during
awards. Hopefully he can post the full results later

Larry Coslick won EZB with 29+ minutes. Kang broke 30, but it was
unofficial and he settled for second place with 28+ minutes.

Larry also won the 0.6g Hobby Shopper contest with a flight of around 24
minutes. Wally Miller was second at 23 minutes.

Tim Chang won intermediate stick at 35:02 to Coslick's 34:55.

I snuck away with the win in F1D at 52:31. Leo was second at around 52:05.
 Steve Brown didn't make any official flights and Tim spent most of his
time on intermediate stick, otherwise I would have likely ended up third.
 I should also mention that Kang threw together an F1D built from leftover
EZB parts in less than 2 weeks and managed a couple 32+ minute flights, but
he didn't submit them as official because he was using a prop from Steve.

We also held the raffle drawing, and I'm sure Kang will be notifying people
that won.
