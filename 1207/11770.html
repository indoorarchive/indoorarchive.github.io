Yep, for many experienced indoorists (and myself) a little handshake would be sufficient. But to remove one worry off the head of any contest organizer, maybe we could make the following a tradition for this kind of contest...

Every competitor bring an interesting or useful item to the contest. These will be the prizes. Display loot on a table at end of contest, first placers choose first, etc. Not unlike how Kibbie Dome used to do, but we supply each other, instead of contest organizer having to. If you don't bring something, fine, you don't get anything. But if it catches on, everyone will bring something, if not start to go overboard.
