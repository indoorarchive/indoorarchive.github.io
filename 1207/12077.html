> I'm interested in putting together some scale models for the F1K class (CO2). Can someone send me a list of requirements (along with definitions) of what is legal?

Randy, F1K is a nonscale class. Most of the models are built very light with pretty complex DT arrangements to make them come down reliably.

There is an FAI CO2 scale class (designation escapes me at the moment). It's flown in the UK and Europe. None of the FAI scale classes are flown in the US, or at least not in accordance with FAI rules (I'm thinking of peanut and pistachio).
