Date: October 13th, 2012
Location: UIUC Armory in Urbana, IL - One of the highest CAT3 sites in America.
Time: Open from 6am. Contest will likely start ~8am and go through 5PM. Open
flying from 5PM - 9PM. Must be out of Armory by 10PM.

Cost: Preregistration - $25 for Adults/$5 Juniors and Seniors
At the door, $30/$6
Extra events: No charge
Table/two chairs rental: $10 Preregister/$15 at door if available
T-Shirt: $15 pre-order/$20 at door if available

Pre-Registration via PayPal to mkirda at UIC.edu... or snail-mail to:
Mike Kirda
6048 W. Eddy
Chicago, IL 60634
Please indicate Name, AMA#, Events to be flown, T-shirt size if desired and
Table if desired.

CD: Jeff Annis and John Kagan
Questions: mkirda at UIC.edu.

Address: UofI Armory, 505 E Armory Ave, Champaign, IL 61820
Note: This puts you on the north side of the Armory.
Entrance to the Armory floor is on the SW corner.
Free Parking is located south of the Armory next to Huff Hall.

Events: HL Glider, Catapult glider, Unlimited Catapult glider, Dime Scale,
No-cal, Phantom Flash, Race to Ceiling, A6, F1L, LPP, MiniStick and F1D.

Mass launch events planned.

Time slots to be finalized, tentatively Morning Gliders/scale, Mid - Phantom
Flash, mass launch, Race to Ceiling.
Followed by A6, LPP, F1L. Final: Ministick and F1D.

I am keeping the costs as low as possible and need 20 people to break even. Any
profits will be donated to the Bong Eagles and USIC.
