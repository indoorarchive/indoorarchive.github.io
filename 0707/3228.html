Dear coleagues ,

I have posted results in file section and some atmosphere photos in
photo section from 3 days Trial flying for F1D EM Belgrad , Serbia .
The competition was held 27-29.July in Ice Hockey stadion in Teplice (
North-western part of Czech republic), 14,5 m high auditorium , very
quiet and good Indoor site, with flat ceiling .
We flew half motor F1D and all results are with 0,30 g rubber motor .
Winner is Ivan Treger , known indoor modeler with best flight 17 min in
14,5 m ceiling with half motor !, second Mikita Kaplan and third Jozef
Kubes .

help.
