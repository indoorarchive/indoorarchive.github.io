Hi Larry,

I tend to fly in with my car (it's only an 8 hour drive from Ohio) :)

But PHL (Phili) and EWR (Newark) are only a little over an hour away,
and LGA (LaGuardia) is only about 1:40. That's all depending on
traffic, of course.

Staying by the shore can be expensive, especially during the "on"
season. But if you've got it like that, people have said that Tom's
River area is nice.

I'm staying in the Maple Shade area this time. It's about 50 minutes
away, but a whole lot cheaper.

Doug also found a few reasonable places in Brown Mills, which looks
much closer.

Don't forget that you need to get an ECIM ID for base access. And if
anyone does drive, make absolutely sure you've got your registration,
insurance, etc. up-to-date. This is an active base and they don't play.
