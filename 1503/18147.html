Very nice! I found it tedious to make slots to 00-90 nylon screws, so I
drilled a hole across the screw, inserted a short piece of 0.5mm carbon
rod instead. I then took a length of aluminium tube that fits over the
00-90 screw, and cut a slot across the end, to use the tube as screwdriver.
