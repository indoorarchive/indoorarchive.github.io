The high times from Zoltan and Cornel in the range of 27 and 28 minutes are extraordinary. 28 minutes for a new rules F1D is equivalent to 42 minutes in the old rules F1D, and this is all done at 52F/11C where the rubber energy is substantially down.
 

 The best flights reach a height of 160'. I saw one flight to 170', but it did not yield a high time like the others.
 

 Zoltan is one minute ahead of Cornel entering the final two rounds. It will be an interesting day.
