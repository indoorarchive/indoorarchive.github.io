Greetings.

I thought I would pass this along.

I used to hate drilling holes for VP mechanisms and such. A normal drill press just doesn't spin fast enough for the small bits.

On eBay you can often find Dumore Sensitive drill presses used.
They are not cheap mind you, but in this case they are much cheaper than new.

Now my bits are spinning at 17000 rpm and look like they are standing still - The chuck is dead on.
Bits don't break as easily.

Now if only I could find a work holder for it...
